Spring cooking means fresh vegetables and simple dishes. This week Martha Rose Shulman offers a new simple pasta dish with mussels. It’s a meal that tastes like you went to a lot of work, but only you will know how simple it was to put it on the table. We’ve also added some of our favorite pasta dishes to give you lots of fresh and easy options for pasta night.

Pasta With Salsa Crudo and Green Beans

Spaghetti With Broccoli and Walnut/Ricotta Pesto
	:
		This spaghetti sauce is a creamy, pungent walnut-thickened pesto that is thinned out with a small amount of cooking water from the pasta.
