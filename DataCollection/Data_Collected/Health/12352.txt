Chef Rocco DiSpirito turns his Chinatown kitchen into a gym.A decade ago, the chef Rocco DiSpirito was overweight and headed for health problems. But a sobering chat with his doctor led to some major diet and exercise changes, which Mr. DiSpirito has documented in a series of best-selling cookbooks.Now he is back with “Cook Your Butt Off!” which looks at ways that people can slice calories from their favorite meals and turn their kitchens into home gyms. I spoke with Mr. DiSpirito about the benefits of a low-tech approach to cooking, how to stock a healthy pantry, and the best low-carb replacement for rice. Here’s an edited version of our conversation.On Wednesday, Mr. DiSpirito will be answering your questions about cutting calories in your meals and in your kitchen.
