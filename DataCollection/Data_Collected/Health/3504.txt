If you’re looking for a colorful dish to brighten your holiday table, try this simple and elegant Vegetable Tian.Reader Deborah Lee Liberatore, of Fernandina Beach, Fla,. is a vegan who serves this dish to her guests. Yellow squash, eggplant, zucchini and tomatoes are basted with olive oil, flavored with herbs and roasted to perfection. Non-vegans can top it with Parmesan cheese.“It is delicious and so pretty with all of the colors of the rainbow,’’ says Ms. Liberatore.
