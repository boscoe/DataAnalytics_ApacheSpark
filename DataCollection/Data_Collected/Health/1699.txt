In a study of how ambivalence in a relationship influences health, researchers asked husbands and wives a few simple questions. To find out where your relationship rates on the ambivalence index, take the shortened version of the quiz below. Read the questions and answer them on a scale of one (not at all) to six (extremely). When you are answering about positivity, don’t think about other instances of negativity and allow that to influence your answers. When you are answering about negativity, don’t think about other instances of positivity and allow that to influence your answers.
