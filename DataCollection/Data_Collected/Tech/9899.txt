Q. How do I delete the stored credit card from the Google Play store on my Android tablet and change it to a prepaid Google Play gift card?A. To remove an existing credit card stored in your account settings, open the Google Play Store app on your Android device. In the upper-left corner, tap the Menu button, which appears as three horizontal lines, and select Account. On the Account screen, select Edit Payment Method and enter your password if requested. Select the credit card you want to delete, choose Remove and then select Yes, Remove It. (You can also remove the card from your Google account with a web browser by logging in at payments.google.com.)To add an alternative form of payment to your Google Play account, go back to the Menu button, return to the Account screen and choose Add a Payment Method. The following screen will list the available ways to pay.You can add a different credit card, a debit card, a PayPal account or the code from a prepaid Google Play Store card. On some Android devices, you may also be able to choose direct carrier billing or Google Wallet. Select the option you want and follow the steps on screen to supply the payment information.If you just need to change an expiration date and security code for an updated credit card, go to the Account screen in the Google Play store app and choose Edit Payment Method. Sign in if prompted, select the credit card from the Payment Methods list, choose Edit and change the necessary information.
