Earlier this month, Apple hosted an event to introduce its latest iPhones, iPad and Apple TV. On Tuesday, Google plans to hold its own such event.Google is expected to unveil new smartphones, among other products, at its event in San Francisco. Now is a prime time for tech companies to roll out new hardware, ahead of the holiday shopping season. Conor Dougherty will be at Google’s event on Tuesday to check out the action, so look for his dispatch. In other tech news, Yahoo decided to go ahead with spinning off its Alibaba stake into a separate entity, despite uncertainty after federal tax authorities declined to rule in advance on whether the transaction would mean huge capital gains taxes for Yahoo or its shareholders. Yahoo shareholders had been looking forward to the spinoff, so by keeping it on track, Marissa Mayer, the company’s chief executive, gets to keep those shareholders happy.
