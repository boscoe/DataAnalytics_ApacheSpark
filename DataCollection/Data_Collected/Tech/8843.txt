After the Apple product event on Wednesday, tech news slowed down.There was one notable exception: The saga of the venture capital firm Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers and Ellen Pao, one of the firm’s former partners, drew to a close. After a drawn-out battle in which Ms. Pao sued the firm for gender discrimination and lost, she said Thursday that she would drop the appeal of the decision.Elsewhere, there was still chatter about Apple — this time more in the form of questions about the new iPhones, iPad and Apple TV that were unveiled this week.
