AdvertisementSupported byBusiness BriefingBy The Associated PressA month after announcing new guidelines against harassment, Reddit, the social networking and news site, said on Wednesday that it had removed five groups. Only one of the five communities had over 5,000 subscribers, Reddit said. It ridiculed overweight people. The site did not disclose the names of the other banned groups. Users said the names of those groups included slurs related to race, gender and sexual orientation. Known for freewheeling discussions, Reddit said last month that it was unhappy with harassment on its site and that survey data showed its users were also upset. It said users could report abusive messages and posts by email. Advertisement
