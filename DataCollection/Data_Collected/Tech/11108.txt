A top executive also departed the company.It’s Intel turn on Thursday. The chip maker is scheduled to disclose quarterly results, with investors set to scrutinize the performance of Brian Krzanich, who took over as Intel’s chief executive in 2013. The Silicon Valley company, which rose to prominence by providing chips to personal computers, has been adapting to the current technology landscape where mobile devices have become the biggest growth area.
