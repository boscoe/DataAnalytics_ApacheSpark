By RAVI SOMAIYAOCT. 27, 2015
The New York Times said Tuesday that Nick Rockwell, now the chief technology officer of Condé Nast, would lead its technology operations.Mr. Rockwell, 46, will become The Times’s chief technology officer on Nov. 16. He will be responsible “for all technology operations, including infrastructure, e-commerce, enterprise computing and development of the company’s digital systems,” the company said in a statement.
