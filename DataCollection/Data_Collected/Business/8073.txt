AdvertisementSupported byBy Christine HauserThe Kraft Heinz Company has recalled some of its individually wrapped cheese products over concerns that the plastic packaging could stick to the slice after being removed and cause a potential choking hazard.The company said on Friday that the voluntary recall was in response to 10 consumer complaints about the wrapping, including three reports of choking.The company said the recall covered about 36,000 cases of 3-pound and 4-pound Kraft Singles American and White American cheese products sent to retailers in the United States, Puerto Rico and Grand Cayman. Those cases were stamped with the numbers S54 and S55, referring to the two production lines where they were manufactured.The cheese products that were recalled had use-by dates of Dec. 29, 2015, through Jan. 4, 2016. No other products were affected, the company said.Advertisement
