By REUTERSFEB. 15, 2016
The European Central Bank said it was considering eliminating the 500-euro note, describing the bill commonly used by savers as an instrument also favored by criminals. The amount of cash in the eurozone rose to more than 1 trillion euros ($1.1 trillion) last year, with almost 30 percent of it in 500-euro bills, reflecting anxiety about the solvency of most banks as well as exasperation with low returns on savings.
