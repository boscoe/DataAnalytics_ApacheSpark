By JOHN KOBLINMAY 7, 2015
Fox will not renew “The Mindy Project” for a fourth season when it announces its fall lineup in the coming days, said a source familiar with the plans for the show.But there is a chance that the show will continue on Hulu, said the person, who was not authorized to speak publicly. Universal TV, the studio behind the show, is in discussions with Hulu about doing two more seasons. No deal has been reached yet.The show is popular on Hulu, which offers all of the episodes from the first three seasons.”The Mindy Project,” created by and starring the comedian Mindy Kaling, was a critical hit and had a niche following but consistently drew low ratings. The show’s third season wrapped up in March with 2.1 million viewers and a minuscule 0.8 rating with the 18-49 age demographic that advertisers covet.News of the show’s talks with Hulu was reported by several entertainment trade publications Wednesday night.
