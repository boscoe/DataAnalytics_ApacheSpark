By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 7, 2016
The global chief of Volkswagen, Matthias Müller, will meet Wednesday with the administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency, Gina McCarthy, the regulator said. Volkswagen and United States regulators are at an apparent impasse over how to proceed with the expected recall of nearly 600,000 “clean diesel” vehicles sold with secret software designed to evade federal emissions standards.
