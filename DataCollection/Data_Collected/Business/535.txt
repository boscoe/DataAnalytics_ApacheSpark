AdvertisementSupported byBusiness BriefingBy The Associated PressHess gas stations are no more, but its annual toy truck is back. Hess Toy Trucks on Monday unveiled a red fire truck and ladder rescue that will go on sale Nov. 1. Hess had been selling toy trucks at its gas stations during the holidays since 1964. Last year, the company announced it was selling its retail gas stations to Marathon Petroleum. It remains in the business of finding and drilling for oil and natural gas. The toy will be sold online for $31 and at mall kiosks in New York, New Jersey and Pennsylvania. Advertisement
