By REUTERSJAN. 6, 2015
Boeing said on Tuesday that it delivered 723 jetliners in 2014, setting an industry record and retaining its title as the world’s biggest plane maker. Boeing said it also set company records for orders in 2014, booking 1,550 gross orders and 1,432 net orders — which account for cancellations. However, Boeing’s net orders, up nearly 6 percent from 2013, will probably not knock the Airbus Group out of the top spot for booking new business. Analysts said it was too early to say which manufacturer would win.
