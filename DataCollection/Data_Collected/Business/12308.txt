By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 4, 2016
The value of Maine fishermen’s catch topped $631 million at the dock last year, surpassing the previous year’s record total by more than $33 million. Lobsters constituted more than three-quarters of the value at more than $495 million, also a record.
