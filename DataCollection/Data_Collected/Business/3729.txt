By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 19, 2015
Reynolds American, the second-largest tobacco company in the United States after Altria, is teaming up with a pharmaceutical consultancy, Pinney Associates, to develop products that help people stop smoking. Niconovum, a unit of Reynolds American, based in Winston-Salem, N.C., will work with a Pinney affiliate to develop a new kind of nicotine gum and other nicotine-replacement products like electronic cigarettes. Reynolds bought Niconovum, which makes nicotine gum, pouches and spray products, in 2009. The unit is also expanding the availability of its nicotine gum, Zonnic, nationally.
