By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 2, 2015
United Technologies is changing the leadership at Sikorsky Aircraft, its helicopter-making subsidiary, less than a month after announcing that it may spin off the unit. United on Thursday appointed Robert Leduc president of Sikorsky, moving his predecessor, Mick Maurer, to the new role of United’s senior vice president for strategic projects. Sikorsky makes the Black Hawk and Seahawk helicopters for the military. Mr. Leduc, 59, retired in 2014 after 35 years at United.
