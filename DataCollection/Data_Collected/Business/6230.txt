By THE NEW YORK TIMESMAY 21, 2015
As fast-food workers and their supporters rallied for higher pay outside, McDonald’s shareholders gathered on Thursday at its headquarters in Oak Brook, Ill. Company officials are fighting to revive sluggish sales and convince people that McDonald’s is a “modern, progressive burger company.” Some shareholders pushed the company to consider diverting money it is spending on stock buybacks to pay higher wages.
