By MICHAEL CIEPLYOCT. 13, 2015
LOS ANGELES — David Linde, a former co-chairman of Universal Pictures, was named chief executive officer of Participant Media, the company said Tuesday.Mr. Linde succeeds Jim Berk, who resigned as chief executive in April. In the interim, Participant, which focuses on issues-oriented entertainment, was overseen by its founder and chairman, the entrepreneur Jeffrey Skoll.Mr. Linde and Marc Shmuger were ousted from their jobs as co-chairmen of Universal in 2009.
