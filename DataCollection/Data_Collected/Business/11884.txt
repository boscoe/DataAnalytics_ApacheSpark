By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 16, 2016
United States homebuilders were feeling less confident about their prospects before the spring home-selling season, though they remain positive over all, according to the National Association of Home Builders/Wells Fargo builder sentiment index. The measure, released Tuesday, slipped to 58 this month, down three points from a revised reading of 61 in January.A version of this article appears in print on February 17, 2016, on Page B2 of the New York edition with the headline: Homebuilders’ Confidence About Sales Declines.
