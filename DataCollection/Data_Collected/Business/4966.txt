By JOHN KOBLINAPRIL 6, 2015
ABC announced Monday that it would broadcast a two-hour episode of “20/20” featuring Diane Sawyer interviewing Bruce Jenner on April 24.Mr. Jenner, the former Olympic gold medalist now best known as the patriarch of the Kardashians on their hit reality TV show, has not confirmed but is widely believed to be transitioning to female from male, though he has made no public comment to that effect.He has gone through a number of plastic surgeries in the last few years, and he has been an endless topic of fascination for the tabloid media for just as long.An ABC spokesman said the interview was conducted in early February.The spokesman added that Ms. Sawyer interviewed Mr. Jenner before he was involved in a four-car accident in February in Malibu, Calif.; one person died, and a number were injured.An interview with Mr. Jenner has been highly sought. Ms. Sawyer left as anchor of “World News Tonight” in August, and has been hosting specials for ABC since the beginning of the year.A version of this article appears in print on April 7, 2015, on Page C3 of the New York edition with the headline: ABC to Air Sawyer Interview With Bruce Jenner.
