By REUTERSAPRIL 3, 2015
Tesla Motors said it delivered a record 10,030 cars in the first quarter, a 55 percent increase compared with a year ago, and it said it expected to deliver about 55,000 cars globally in 2015, an increase of about 74 percent. The company said that in the future it would publish the number of new car deliveries within three days of the quarter’s end to discourage inaccurate information.
