By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 12, 2015
The American Egg Board says it is planning a print and online advertising campaign featuring the actor Kevin Bacon and puns using his last name. The online spots feature a woman making scrambled eggs for breakfast, when Mr. Bacon appears lying suggestively on the counter behind her. At one point, she leans in to sniff the actor and says she loves the smell of bacon. Then her husband walks in. The “Wake Up to Eggs With Bacon” push by the ad agency Grey is an update on a “Wake Up to Eggs” campaign that started in 2012. “I always felt like egg farmers were too conservative,” said Bob Krouse, chief of Midwest Poultry Services, an egg producer in Mentone, Ind.A version of this brief appears in print on March 13, 2015, on Page B2 of the New York edition with the headline: Kevin Bacon Joins Campaign to Sell Eggs Online and in Print.
