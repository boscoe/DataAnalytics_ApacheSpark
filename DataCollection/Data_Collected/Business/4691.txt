AdvertisementSupported byRevolving DoorBy Neil GoughHONG KONG — Citigroup has hired a longtime China banker from Bank of America’s Merrill Lynch unit to be head of its investment banking in the country.Citigroup said on Wednesday that it had appointed Catherine Cai, who has two decades of experience in investment banking in China, as its chairwoman and head of China investment banking.Ms. Cai worked at Merrill Lynch for 10 years and was most recently its chairwoman of China investment banking. She will report to Mark Slaughter, Citigroup’s head of corporate and investment banking in the Asia-Pacific region.Ms. Cai “has excellent client relationships around the region and has been involved on several landmark and groundbreaking transactions from China during her career,” Mr. Slaughter said in a news release. “We look forward to Catherine playing a leading role in growing and further developing our investment banking business in China.”Advertisement
