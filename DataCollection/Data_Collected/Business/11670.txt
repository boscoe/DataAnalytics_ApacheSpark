By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 4, 2016
Berkshire Hathaway has purchased nearly 1.7 million more shares of Phillips 66 to give it ownership of almost 14 percent of the oil refiner. Berkshire, a conglomerate headed by the billionaire Warren E. Buffett, reported the latest purchases this week. It now owns 73.98 million Phillips 66 shares worth roughly $5.9 billion. In August, Berkshire announced that it owned more than 10 percent of Phillips 66 stock when it disclosed a stake of 55 million shares. But before Berkshire began its recent string of purchases in January, it owned only 61.49 million of the company’s shares.
