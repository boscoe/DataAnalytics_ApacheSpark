By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 12, 2016
Shares in the mobile payment services company Square rose more than 7 percent on Friday after Visa disclosed the details of its ownership stake in the company. While Visa’s stake amounts to only about 1 percent of the company, and the stake was established at least five years ago, investors interpreted the announcement as a major endorsement of Square by Visa, the world’s largest payment processing company. According to a regulatory filing, Visa owns 4.2 million of Square’s Class B shares. Because Square went public only three months ago, this is the first time the public is getting details about Visa’s ownership.
