By CHAD BRAYJUNE 26, 2015
LONDON — Shares of Europcar, a French rental car company, declined more than 3 percent in early trading in Paris on Friday after its initial public offering.Europcar sold 879 million euros, or about $985 million, in an offering of 71.8 million shares, which was priced late Thursday. The offering valued the company around €1.75 billion.The company priced its stock at €12.25 a share, at the low end of its expected range, which was narrowed this week to €12 to €13.25.
