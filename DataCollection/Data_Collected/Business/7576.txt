By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJULY 15, 2015
Delta Air Lines’ second-quarter profit nearly doubled, to $1.5 billion, as huge savings on fuel offset weak growth in revenue. The company, the third-largest airline in the United States, predicted a record quarter when it next reports financial results, in October. Excluding nonrecurring gains, Delta earned $1.27 a share, beating the $1.22 forecast from 10 analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research.
