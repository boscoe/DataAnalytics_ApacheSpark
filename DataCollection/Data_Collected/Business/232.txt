By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 7, 2015
TJX, the retailing company behind T. J. Maxx, Marshalls and other discount stores, said on Wednesday that it had named its president, Ernie Herrman, chief executive. He will succeed Carol Meyrowitz on Jan. 31. Ms. Meyrowitz, 61, who has been chief of TJX since 2007, will continue to be chairwoman and has agreed to stay with the company for three years.
