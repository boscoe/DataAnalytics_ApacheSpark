By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 2, 2016
Yahoo on Wednesday announced the start of Yahoo Esports, an online channel devoted to video games, including commentary and interviews with top players, along with articles, scores and statistics. Last month, ESPN announced its own e-sports channel.
