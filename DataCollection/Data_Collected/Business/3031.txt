By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 27, 2015
The Federal Aviation Administration seeks to fine United Airlines $1.3 million for violating rules about shipping hazardous material, often by inaccurately describing the amount of the cargo. Inspectors found at least 120 violations in 2013, most for failing to give accurate information about the weight, quantity and location of items like lithium metal batteries, corrosive liquids and radioactive materials.
