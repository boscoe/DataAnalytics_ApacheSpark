AdvertisementSupported byBy Sydney EmberThe music streaming service Spotify announced on Wednesday that it had named Barry McCarthy, a former Netflix executive and a member of Spotify’s board, its new chief financial officer.Mr. McCarthy will assume the role on July 6 and will step down from Spotify’s board, which he joined in February 2014. Peter Sterky, Spotify’s current chief financial officer, will remain with the company, a spokesman said.At Netflix, Mr. McCarthy also served as chief financial officer before he left in 2010, helping Reed Hastings, the company’s chief executive, build the online video service into a streaming giant. Mr. McCarthy has also served as the chief operating officer of Clinkle, a mobile payments start-up. He was on the board of Pandora and is currently on the boards of Eventbrite and Rent the Runway, among others.Advertisement
