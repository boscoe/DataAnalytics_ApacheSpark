By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 26, 2016
Dow Chemical said on Friday that it would pay $835 million to settle a longstanding class-action lawsuit after the death of Justice Antonin Scalia decreased its chances of prevailing at the Supreme Court. Without Justice Scalia, the Supreme Court is evenly split 4-4 between justices who are usually conservative and those who are usually liberal. Split decisions revert to the lower court’s opinion.
