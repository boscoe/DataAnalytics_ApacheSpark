By REUTERSAUG. 12, 2015
Cisco Systems, the network equipment maker, reported higher-than-expected quarterly revenue and profit, highlighting a strong recovery in demand for its switching equipment and routers. Cisco’s net income rose to $2.32 billion, or 45 cents a share, from $2.25 billion, or 43 cents a share, in the year-ago quarter. Revenue rose nearly 4 percent, to $12.84 billion. Cisco’s shares rose nearly 3 percent in extended trading on Wednesday.
