AdvertisementSupported byBusiness BriefingBy The Associated PressUnited States auto safety regulators are investigating complaints that some newer Jeeps can roll away unexpectedly after they are put in park. The inquiry covers 408,000 Grand Cherokees from the 2014 and 2015 model years. The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration says it has received 14 complaints, including reports of five crashes and three injuries. Investigators will determine how often the problem happens, how many vehicles it affects and whether to deem it a safety problem before deciding on a recall. Advertisement
