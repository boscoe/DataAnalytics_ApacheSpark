By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 2, 2015
American businesses increased hiring last month, led by strong gains in retail, finance and other service industries, a private survey found. The payroll processor ADP said on Wednesday that companies had added 217,000 jobs in November, the most in five months. Service sector firms added 204,000, while manufacturers hired just 6,000. On Friday, the government will issue its official jobs report for November.
