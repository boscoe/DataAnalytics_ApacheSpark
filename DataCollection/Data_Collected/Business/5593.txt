By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 28, 2015
Jeni’s Splendid Ice Creams is destroying more than 265 tons of ice cream after listeria was found in the production facility and in products last week. The company’s chief, John Lowe, said in a statement on Tuesday that the product to be destroyed amounted to 15 tractor-trailer loads, costing more than $2.5 million.
