
UPDATED November 30, 2015

The singer’s “25” sold a record-shattering 3.38 million copies in the United States through its first week, according to Nielsen Music. The last time an album sold nearly that well was in 2000, when 2.4 million copies of “No Strings Attached” by ’N Sync were sold. At the time, however, annual CD sales were more than five times what they are today.
