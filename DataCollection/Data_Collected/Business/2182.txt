DEC. 27, 2015
The Treasury’s schedule of financing this week includes Monday’s regular weekly auction of new three- and six-month bills and an auction of four-week bills on Tuesday.At the close of the New York cash market on Thursday, the rate on the outstanding three-month bill was 0.21 percent. The rate on the six-month issue was 0.48 percent, and the rate on the four-week issue was 0.15 percent.A version of this article appears in print on December 28, 2015, on Page B2 of the New York edition with the headline: Treasury Auctions Set for This Week.
