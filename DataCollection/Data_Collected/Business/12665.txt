By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 21, 2016
The European aircraft maker Airbus said that the inaugural test flight of its first airplane made in the United States was a success. The A321 passenger jet took off Monday from the company’s new plant in Mobile, Ala. The three-hour flight over the Gulf of Mexico was to include a series of maneuvers and tests of all the airplane’s operating systems.
