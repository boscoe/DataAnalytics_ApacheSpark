Mark Smucker, president of the consumer and natural foods unit, will become chief effective May 1. He is Richard Smucker’s nephew and Timothy P. Smucker’s son. Richard Smucker, who has been chief since 2011, will succeed Timothy P. Smucker as executive chairman.
