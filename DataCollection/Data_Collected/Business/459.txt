By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 15, 2015
The Treasury Department told Congress on Thursday that it must act by Nov. 3 or the government will be dangerously close to being unable to pay all its bills. That is a little sooner than Jacob Lew, secretary of the Treasury, said two weeks ago, when he set a Nov. 5 deadline. At the time, some Republican lawmakers said Mr. Lew was trying to rush them into approving an increase in the government’s $18.1 trillion borrowing limit.
