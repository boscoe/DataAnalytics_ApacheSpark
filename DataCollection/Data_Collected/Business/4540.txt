By CHAD BRAYMARCH 19, 2015
LONDON — Auto Trader Group, the British website for buying and selling used cars, said on Thursday that it had priced its initial public offering, valuing the company at 2.35 billion pounds, or about $3.5 billion.Funds advised by the European private-equity firm Apax Partners bought out their joint venture partner in Trader Media Group last year and renamed the company after its most popular online brand, Auto Trader.Auto Trader priced its offering at £2.35 a share. The company sold about 59 percent of its capital in the offering of 590 million shares, according to the pricing announcement.Shares in Auto Trader jumped 13 percent to £2.66 in conditional trading on the London Stock Exchange early on Thursday.
