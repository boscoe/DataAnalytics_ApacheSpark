By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 6, 2015
Southwest Airlines earned a record profit last year and its chief executive benefited with a 24 percent increase in compensation, to $5 million, according to a regulatory filing on Monday. The bulk of the compensation for the chief, Gary C. Kelly, came in stock awards, which the airline valued at $3 million when they were issued, up from $2.25 million in 2013. Last year Southwest earned net income of $1.14 billion on rising traffic and average fares, and falling jet fuel costs.
