By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 19, 2015
Etsy shares sank almost 14 percent in aftermarket trading after the company’s first earnings report as a publicly traded company showed a hefty quarterly loss because of costs related to restructuring and a tax provision. Its net loss was $36.6 million, or 84 cents a share, for the three months that ended March 31. In the year-ago quarter, the company reported a loss of $463,000. Etsy went public to much fanfare in April, but shares have declined over investor concerns about counterfeit goods. Etsy said if the dollar remained strong, that could hurt buyer behavior outside the United States.
