By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 8, 2016
Ikea says Kanye West is visiting the furniture giant’s offices in southern Sweden. The company confirmed an online report on Tuesday by the Swedish newspaper Aftonbladet that the American rapper was in Almhult, a town in southern Sweden where Ikea was founded in the 1940s and where it retains many key operations. An Ikea spokesman, Jakob Holmstrom, said that Mr. West was visiting several Ikea facilities, including its design division.
