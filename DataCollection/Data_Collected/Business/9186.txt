By MICHAEL J. de la MERCEDSEPT. 24, 2015
Deutsche Bank said on Thursday that it had hired a senior banker from Goldman Sachs to serve as one of its top advisers for banks and insurance companies.The banker, Celeste Guth, will be a co-head of the global financial institutions group with Tadhg Flood.When she joins Deutsche Bank, she will be based in New York and will report to Paul Stefanick, the bank’s global head of investment banking coverage and advisory.At Goldman, Ms. Guth was most recently a vice chairman of the financial institutions group and a global co-head of insurance investment banking. She joined Goldman in 1986 and became a partner in 2002.A version of this article appears in print on September 25, 2015, on Page B3 of the New York edition with the headline: New Senior Banker.
