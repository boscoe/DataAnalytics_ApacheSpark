A longtime advocate for artists’ rights, Prince recently began removing his music from most streaming services. But he has left his music on Tidal, the subscription service owned by Jay Z and more than a dozen other artists (Prince is not thought to be among them). Last month, Prince released a new single, “Stare,” on Spotify, another streaming service. Advertisement
