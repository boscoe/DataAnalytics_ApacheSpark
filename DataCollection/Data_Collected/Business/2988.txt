By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 26, 2015
For the first time, all varieties of apples from the United States will go on sale in China. A deal was reached last week between United States and Chinese officials to grant access to all American apple varieties, according to the Washington Apple Commission, which represents growers of the nation’s largest apple crop. China bought $55 million worth of Washington Red Delicious and Golden Delicious apples in the 2010-11 marketing year.
