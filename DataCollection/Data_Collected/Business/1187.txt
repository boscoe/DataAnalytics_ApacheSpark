AdvertisementSupported byBusiness BriefingBy The Associated PressChipotle Mexican Grill said on Tuesday that it was reopening the 43 Pacific Northwest restaurants it closed during an E. coli outbreak after tests came back negative for the bacteria. Health officials have not found the cause of the outbreak, which started last month, but concluded there was no lingering risk of contracting the illness. Chipotle will reopen the restaurants in Washington State and Oregon in the coming days after giving them a deep cleaning and ordering a fresh supply of ingredients, the company said. It also said it was testing the new food and adopting additional safety procedures. Advertisement
