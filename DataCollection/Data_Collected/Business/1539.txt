By REUTERSNOV. 24, 2015
At least 19 people may have been infected by E. coli after eating rotisserie chicken salad sold at Costco Wholesale stores, the United States Centers for Disease Control and Prevention said on Tuesday. Five people have been hospitalized, although no deaths have been reported. The ingredient linked to the infection has not been identified.
