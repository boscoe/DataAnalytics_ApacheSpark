By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJUNE 4, 2015
State Farm said that its chief operating officer, Michael Tipsord, would become its next chief executive, succeeding Ed Rust Jr., who has led the private company since 1985. State Farm, an insurance and financial company, said in a news release that Mr. Tipsord would take over in September. Mr. Rust, 64, will remain chairman of the company’s board. “Michael has a deep understanding of the opportunities and challenges emerging in our rapidly changing marketplace,” Mr. Rust told employees on Thursday. Mr. Tipsord joined the company in 1988, and became a vice president in 2002.
