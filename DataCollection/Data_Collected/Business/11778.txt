By REUTERSFEB. 10, 2016
Government tests show that some types of laminate flooring sold by Lumber Liquidators can cause irritation and breathing problems but the risk of cancer from exposure is low, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention said on Wednesday. Lumber Liquidators’ sales and shares slumped after the CBS program “60 Minutes” reported in March that the laminates, which were made in China, contained excessive levels of cancer-causing formaldehyde.
