AdvertisementSupported byBusiness BriefingBy Christopher JensenFiat Chrysler is recalling almost 505,000 2011-16 Dodge Chargers, including about 442,000 in the United States, because the vehicle might fall off the jack when a tire is being changed, the automaker said Wednesday. The problem is that the part of the vehicle’s body the jack is holding up could flex, allowing the vehicle to topple, said Eric Mayne, a Fiat Chrysler spokesman. Fiat Chrysler will supply owners with wheel chocks; the owner’s manual shows how they should be used. The automaker said it was aware of three minor injuries. Advertisement
