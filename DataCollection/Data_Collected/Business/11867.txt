By REUTERSFEB. 15, 2016
India’s exports shrank in January for the 14th straight month on continued weak demand from Europe, but the central bank said it would not follow countries like China and Japan in pushing down its currency to help. Exports fell 13.6 percent from January a year ago and imports fell 11 percent, according to data from the Ministry of Commerce and Industry.
