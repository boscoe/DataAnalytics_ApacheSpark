By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJUNE 5, 2015
Consumer borrowing expanded by $20.5 billion in April, the Federal Reserve reported on Friday, down slightly from a gain of $21.3 billion in March that was the biggest increase in eight months. The strong gains pushed consumer credit to a fresh record of $3.38 trillion.
