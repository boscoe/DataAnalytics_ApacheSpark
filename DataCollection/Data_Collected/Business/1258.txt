Delta Air Lines, Alaska Airlines and Southwest Airlines had the best ratings; all were better than 87 percent. Spirit Airlines, at 76.7 percent, and JetBlue Airways, at 80 percent, had the worst marks. Consumer complaints to the government — though rare at 1,351 complaints out of 55.8 million passengers — increased 70 percent from a year earlier. Advertisement
