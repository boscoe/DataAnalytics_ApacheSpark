By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 9, 2015
H. J. Heinz said Monday it was starting a new ketchup flavor that includes the popular red Thai hot sauce Sriracha. The company said it would start selling a ketchup blended with Sriracha flavor this month. Sriracha’s ingredients include chili pepper paste, garlic and vinegar.
