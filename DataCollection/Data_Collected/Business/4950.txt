AdvertisementSupported byBusiness BriefingBy The Associated PressJetBlue Airways plans to begin daily service between Albany International Airport and two Florida destinations late this year. Flights will begin Dec. 10 with nonstop service to Fort Lauderdale and Orlando. JetBlue already services Buffalo, Rochester, Syracuse, Newburgh, Westchester and both New York City airports. To introduce the flights, JetBlue offered limited one-way promotional fares on Monday of $16.86 — a figure picked to honor Albany’s incorporation in 1686.Advertisement
