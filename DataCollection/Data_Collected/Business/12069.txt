By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 24, 2016
The pharmaceutical company Bayer says its strategy chief, Werner Baumann, will succeed its chief executive, Marijn Dekkers, on May 1. The company said that Mr. Dekkers, 58, requested that his contract end early so that Mr. Baumann, 53, can take over after the annual shareholders’ meeting on April 30.A version of this article appears in print on February 25, 2016, on Page B2 of the New York edition with the headline: Bayer’s Strategy Chief Will Succeed Chief Executive.
