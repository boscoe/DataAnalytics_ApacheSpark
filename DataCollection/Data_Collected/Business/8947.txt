By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSSEPT. 15, 2015
The Bank of Portugal says it is postponing the sale of Novo Banco, a bank salvaged from last year’s collapse of the major lender Banco Espírito Santo, explaining that the offers it received were too low. It said the sale process would resume at an undetermined date. Novo Banco received 4.9 billion euros, or $5.5 billion, from a bank resolution fund, a mechanism adopted during the eurozone debt crisis to support the financial sector.
