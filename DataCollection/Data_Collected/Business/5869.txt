By JACK EWINGMAY 7, 2015
Siemens, the German industrial giant, said on Thursday that it would cut 4,500 jobs as a slump in oil prices has eroded its sales of equipment to the energy industry. The cuts bring the number of layoffs this year to almost 12,000 out of a global work force of 340,000, or a reduction of 3.5 percent. Half of the cuts will be in Germany.
