By REUTERSMAY 28, 2015
Discount retailer Dollar General Stores said Todd Vasos, its chief operating officer, would replace Rick Dreiling as chief executive, effective June 3. Mr. Dreiling, 61, will remain senior adviser and chairman until his retirement on Jan. 29, the company said on Thursday. Mr. Vasos, 53, joined Dollar General in December 2008 as executive vice president and chief merchandising officer.
