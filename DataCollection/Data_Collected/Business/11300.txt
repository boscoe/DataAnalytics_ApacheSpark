By REUTERSJAN. 20, 2016
Star Wars toys, though they were on sale for only four months, generated sales of more than $700 million in the United States in 2015, making them the No. 1 property in the toy industry for the year, the NPD Group, a retailing research group, said. Led by Star Wars merchandise, toy sales in the United States rose 6.7 percent to $19.4 billion in 2015, one of the strongest performances in years, NPD said.
