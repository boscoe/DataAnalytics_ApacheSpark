By JAD MOUAWADNOV. 5, 2015
United Airlines said on Thursday that its president and chief executive, Oscar Munoz, would return in the first quarter after suffering a heart attack last month. Four days after Mr. Munoz was hospitalized, United named its general counsel, Brett J. Hart, as acting chief. At the time, United provided no information about when Mr. Munoz might return.
