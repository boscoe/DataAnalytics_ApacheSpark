By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 27, 2015
Washington Coach Chris Petersen has signed a two-year contract extension keeping him tied to the Huskies through the 2020 season, the school announced.Petersen will be paid $4 million per season in 2019 and 2020, the same as he is scheduled to make in 2018, the final year of his original five-year contract.The Huskies (5-6) need a victory over rival Washington State on Friday to reach bowl eligibility.A version of this brief appears in print on November 27, 2015, on Page B12 of the New York edition with the headline: Washington Coach Signs a Two-Year Extension.
