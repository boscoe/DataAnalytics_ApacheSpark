By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 15, 2015
Novak Djokovic strolled into the third round of the Monte Carlo Masters with a 6-1, 6-4 victory over the qualifier Albert Ramos-Vinolas. In a match that sometimes resembled a practice session, Djokovic never looked troubled, although he dropped his serve once in the middle of the opening set.Gaël Monfils overcame a shaky start to rally past the qualifier Andrey Kuznetsov, 4-6, 6-3, 6-4, while Gilles Simon and Jo-Wilfried Tsonga won in straight sets.■ Maria Sharapova, citing a leg injury, has pulled out of Russia’s Fed Cup semifinal against Germany this weekend.A version of this brief appears in print on April 15, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Djokovic Advances Easily.
