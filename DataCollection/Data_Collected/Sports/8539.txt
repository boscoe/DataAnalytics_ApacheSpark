By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 25, 2015
Stephen Curry returned from a one-game absence to lead all scorers with 32 points, Klay Thompson added 17 more and the visiting Golden State Warriors defeated the Washington Wizards, 114-107. Curry played nearly 34 minutes, hitting 11 of 18 field-goal attempts.■ Derrick Rose, the Chicago Bulls’ point guard, has sustained a tear of the medial meniscus in his right knee. Surgery has been scheduled, but no timeline for his return has been determined. Bulls officials said the tear was discovered after Rose reported to practice with knee pain.A version of this brief appears in print on February 25, 2015, on Page B16 of the New York edition with the headline: Curry Sparks Warriors in Return.
