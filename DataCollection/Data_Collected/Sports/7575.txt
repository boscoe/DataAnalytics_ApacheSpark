By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 5, 2015
Seattle’s Earl Thomas, an All-Pro free safety, played the Super Bowl with a torn labrum in his left shoulder and will need surgery in the off-season to repair the injury. On Thursday, David Mulugheta, Thomas’s agent, confirmed the injury and the need for surgery.■ Ashley Moss, the girlfriend of Seahawks cornerback Richard Sherman, gave birth to the couple’s first child Thursday.
