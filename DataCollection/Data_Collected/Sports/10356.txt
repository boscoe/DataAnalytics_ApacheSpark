By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 27, 2015
Harry Kane scored 80 seconds into his England debut on Friday, providing another eye-catching moment in his breakthrough season in a 4-0 victory over Lithuania in European Championship qualifying. Kane, 21, replaced the captain Wayne Rooney and headed England’s fourth goal into an open net in the 73rd minute at Wembley Stadium. Rooney, Danny Welbeck and Raheem Sterling also scored as England improved to 5-0 in qualifying.■ The qualifying match between Montenegro and Russia was abandoned after Russia goalkeeper Igor Akinfeev was hit by a flare and a 30-minute suspension of the game was followed by a brawl among players. Dmitry Kombarov was also hit by an object, prompting a brawl involving both teams’ players and staff members. (AP)■ Two goals by Zlatan Ibrahimovic gave Sweden a 2-0 win over host Moldova to strengthen his team’s hopes of qualifying for the European Championship. (AP)■ Alvaro Morata scored his first international goal in Spain’s 1-0 home win over Ukraine.
