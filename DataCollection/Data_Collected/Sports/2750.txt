By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 12, 2015
Minnesota said it had signed football coach Tracy Claeys to a three-year contract. Claeys succeeded his friend Jerry Kill, who resigned Oct. 28 because of health reasons. Claeys had been made interim coach after Kill’s resignation. He was hired by Kill as defensive coordinator in 2010.
