By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 11, 2015
Red Berenson, Michigan’s hockey coach, earned his 800th victory when the Wolverines beat visiting Minnesota, 7-5. Berenson, 75, in his 31st season leading Michigan, became the fourth Division I men’s hockey coach to reach 800 victories.The other coaches to earn the honor are current Boston College Coach Jerry York (974), former Michigan State Coach Ron Mason (924) and former Boston University Coach Jack Parker (897). Berenson is 800-391-84.Dylan Larkin scored twice and Michael Downing had a goal and two assists for Michigan.A version of this brief appears in print on January 11, 2015, on Page SP10 of the New York edition with the headline: Milestone at Michigan.
