By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 3, 2015
A United States team anchored by Ryan Bailey won the 4x100-meter relay, defeating a Usain Bolt-led Jamaican team at the International Association of Athletics Federations World Relays meet in Nassau, the Bahamas. The American team, which also included Justin Gatlin, Tyson Gay and Mike Rodgers, finished in 37.38 seconds.
