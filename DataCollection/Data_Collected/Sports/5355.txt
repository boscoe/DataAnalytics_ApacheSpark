Roderick Bobbitt scored 22 of his 30 points in the second half, and Hawaii beat Auburn, 79-67, on Friday in the third-place game at the Diamond Head Classic in Honolulu.Bobbitt was 6 of 7 from 3-point range, 8 of 13 over all, and he made all eight of his free throws. Stefan Jankovic added 21 points, hitting three 3-pointers, and had 8 rebounds for the Rainbow Warriors (9-2).Kareem Canty led Auburn (6-5) with 22 points and 8 assists.
