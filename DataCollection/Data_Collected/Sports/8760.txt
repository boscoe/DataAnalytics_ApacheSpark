Kentucky ended a three-game losing streak against the Razorbacks (23-6, 12-4) by beating them at their own high-speed, physical game, leading by as many as 31 points with eight minutes left.Asked why this game meant a little more, Lyles said: “Just because the last couple games we lost to them.
