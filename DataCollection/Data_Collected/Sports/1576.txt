By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 25, 2015
Empire Dreams beat Royal Posse by three-quarters of a length to win the $300,000 Empire Classic, the richest of the 11 races for New York-bred horses at Belmont Park. Empire Dreams, a 4-year-old gelding ridden by Manny Franco, rallied six wide in the stretch for his fifth win in 17 starts.
