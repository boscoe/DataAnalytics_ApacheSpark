By RICHARD SANDOMIROCT. 5, 2015
Until the regular season ended Sunday, the Yankees did not know which team they would face in Tuesday night’s wild-card game, or even if they would play at home.But the Yankees began selling playoff tickets long before their wild-card opponent was determined. The Yankees, who missed the playoffs in the previous two seasons, put postseason tickets on sale last month.
