By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 10, 2015
Nerlens Noel dunked with three seconds left, lifting the Philadelphia 76ers to a 90-88 victory over the host Nets on Friday night. Brook Lopez had made a tying jumper for the Nets with 25 seconds to go. After Philadelphia took a timeout, Michael Carter-Williams ran off some of the clock before driving and passing to Noel for the winning shot. Lopez, who led the Nets with 18 points, missed on a desperation 3-point attempt at the buzzer.■ Tyreke Evans had 21 points and 10 rebounds, and the host New Orleans Pelicans beat the Memphis Grizzlies, 106-95.■ The host Washington Wizards took a 20-point first-quarter lead and beat the Chicago Bulls, 102-86.
