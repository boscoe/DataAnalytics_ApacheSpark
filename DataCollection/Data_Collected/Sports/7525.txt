By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 4, 2015
Raheem Sterling tied the score in the 86th minute, Philippe Coutinho curled a 20-yard shot over goalkeeper Andy Lonergan in stoppage time, and Liverpool won, 2-1, at Bolton in an F.A. Cup fourth-round replay. Steven Gerrard made his 700th appearance for Liverpool, which will play at Crystal Palace in the fifth round.■ Real Madrid held on to beat visiting Sevilla, 2-1, and increase its lead in the top Spanish league despite a spate of injuries and the absence of the suspended forward Cristiano Ronaldo. James Rodríguez gave Madrid a lead in the 12th minute but left with an injured right foot.■ Ivory Coast advanced to the final of the Africa Cup of Nations as Yaya Touré, Gervinho and Wilfried Kanon scored in a 3-1 win over the Democratic Republic of Congo in Bata, Equatorial Guinea.A version of this brief appears in print on February 5, 2015, on Page B16 of the New York edition with the headline: Liverpool Rallies to Win in F.A.
