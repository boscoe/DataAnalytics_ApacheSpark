Rodney Stuckey scored 23 points against his former team as the Indiana Pacers won at Detroit, 94-82, on Tuesday night for their first victory. The Pistons, however, got a dominating performance from Andre Drummond, who had 25 points and a career-high 29 rebounds, becoming the first Piston with back-to-back 20-20 games since at least 1985, according to Stats L.L.C.■ Jeremy Lamb had 20 points, going 9 of 10 from the field, as the host Charlotte Hornets routed the Chicago Bulls, 130-105, for their first win.
