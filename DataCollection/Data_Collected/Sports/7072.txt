By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 27, 2015
Alexander Khoroshilov won a night slalom in Schladming, Austria, by a huge margin, for the first Russian World Cup victory in more than three decades. Khoroshilov finished in a total of 1 minute 46.39 seconds, 1.44 seconds ahead of Stefano Gross of Italy.A version of this brief appears in print on January 28, 2015, on Page B13 of the New York edition with the headline: Russian Wins Slalom Race.
