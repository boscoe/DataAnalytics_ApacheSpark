By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 17, 2015
California Chrome, the winner of the Kentucky Derby and the Preakness Stakes last year, picked up two Eclipse Awards, including horse of the year. California Chrome also won the Eclipse for 3-year-old males, getting more than three times as many first-place nods as Bayern, the Breeders’ Cup Classic champion.A version of this brief appears in print on January 18, 2015, on Page SP10 of the New York edition with the headline: Chrome Wins Top Award.
