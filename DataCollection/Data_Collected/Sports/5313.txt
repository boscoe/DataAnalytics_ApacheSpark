By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 25, 2015
Jamauri Bogan ran for 215 yards and four scores, powering Western Michigan to a 45-31 victory over Middle Tennessee (7-6) in the Bahamas Bowl in Nassau. The Broncos (8-5) had lost all six of their previous bowl games.■ Rashaad Penny returned the opening kickoff 100 yards for a touchdown, and San Diego State routed Cincinnati (7-5) by 42-7 in the Hawaii Bowl in Honolulu. The Aztecs (11-3) won their final 10 games to match the longest winning streak in team history.
