By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 19, 2015
Wide receiver Larry Fitzgerald, an eight-time Pro Bowler, agreed to a restructured two-year contract to stay with Arizona, the Cardinals announced Wednesday. Fitzgerald, the longtime face of the franchise, had four years remaining on his previous contract.■ Minnesota Vikings General Manager Rick Spielman said he expected Adrian Peterson to return after his suspension is lifted. Peterson last played more than five months ago, before a child-abuse case involving his young son became public and he was put on paid leave by Commissioner Roger Goodell.A version of this brief appears in print on February 19, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Fitzgerald Will Remain a Cardinal.
