By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 8, 2015
Luis Suárez had two goals after Lionel Messi opened the scoring to guide Barcelona, the Spanish league leader, to a 4-0 home victory over Almería. Cristiano Ronaldo later made sure that Real Madrid stayed in contention for the league title by scoring his competition-leading 37th goal of the season in a 2-0 win at Rayo Vallecano. Barcelona’s 20th win in 21 games kept it 4 points clear of Madrid with eight rounds remaining.■ A late goal from Senad Lulic gave Lazio a 1-0 victory at Napoli and a place in the Italian Cup final against Juventus. Lazio advanced, 2-1, on aggregate and will seek its third title in the last seven years in the June 7 final.■ Zlatan Ibrahimovic scored three goals as Paris St.-Germain thrashed visiting St.-Étienne, 4-1, to reach the final of the French Cup.■ The Africa Cup of Nations is set to return to familiar territory in 2017: Gabon was chosen as the host to replace Libya.A version of this brief appears in print on April 9, 2015, on Page B18 of the New York edition with the headline: Barcelona Maintains Lead.
