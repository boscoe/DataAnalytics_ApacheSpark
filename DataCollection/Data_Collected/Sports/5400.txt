Browns General Manager Ray Farmer said Saturday that the episode was subject to the N.F.L.’s personal conduct policy.No details about Bryant’s arrest were immediately available, but Saunders was charged with driving under the influence after being stopped in Brook Park, a suburb of Cleveland, at 2:16 a.m. Friday, the police said. He was driving 75 miles per hour in a 60 m.p.h. zone, according to a citation from the Ohio State Highway Patrol.Saunders, 23, refused a breath test, according to the citation, meaning he would automatically lose his license for a year. In addition, a gun was confiscated from Saunders’s car, the police said. ■ The New England Patriots claimed defensive back Troy Hill off waivers from the Cincinnati Bengals and signed wide receiver Chris Harper to the 53-man roster from the practice squad.
