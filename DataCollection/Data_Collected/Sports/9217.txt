By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 10, 2015
Argentina advanced to the quarterfinals of the Davis Cup, beating visiting Brazil, 3-2, after Federico Delbonis defeated Thomaz Bellucci, 6-3, 3-6, 6-2, 7-5, in a match that had been suspended because of darkness on Sunday.■ Amélie Mauresmo was elected to the International Tennis Hall of Fame.A version of this brief appears in print on March 10, 2015, on Page B13 of the New York edition with the headline: Argentina Advances.
