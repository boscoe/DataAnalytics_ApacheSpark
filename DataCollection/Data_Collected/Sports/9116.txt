By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 7, 2015
Jeff Gordon crashed during the final practice before Sunday’s Nascar race at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway, where his pole-winning car was damaged enough that Hendrick Motorsports pulled out a backup Chevrolet.
