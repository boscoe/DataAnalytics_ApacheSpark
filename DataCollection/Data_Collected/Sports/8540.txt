By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 25, 2015
The Giants released the veteran defensive end Mathias Kiwanuka in a move that clears about $4.8 million of space under the salary cap. A big chunk of the total is likely go to either signing the unrestricted defensive end Jason Pierre-Paul to a long-term deal or placing the nearly $15 million franchise tag on him.■ The Cleveland Browns updated their helmet design with a bolder orange and a brown face mask.A version of this brief appears in print on February 25, 2015, on Page B16 of the New York edition with the headline: Giants Release Defensive End.
