By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 20, 2015
Mario Balotelli scored on a late penalty kick to seal Liverpool’s 1-0 victory over Besiktas in the round of 32 of the Europa League. Inter Milan and Celtic tied, 3-3; Napoli thrashed Trabzonspor, 4-0; Wolfsburg beat Sporting Lisbon, 2-0; and Sevilla edged Borussia Mönchengladbach, 1-0.A version of this brief appears in print on February 20, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Balotelli Scores in Liverpool Win.
