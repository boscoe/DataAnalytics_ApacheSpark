By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 21, 2015
Florida will not be penalized for impermissible contact with a recruit by Joker Phillips, a former assistant football coach. The N.C.A.A. ruled that the Gators had handled the situation appropriately by imposing penalties on themselves.
