By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 6, 2015
The two-time Australian Open champion Victoria Azarenka wasted two match points in the second set before Karolina Pliskova of the Czech Republic rallied to a first-round win, 4-6, 7-6 (7), 6-4, in the Brisbane International in Australia on Monday.As a result, Azarenka is unlikely to be seeded at the Australian Open, which starts Jan. 19.The American Madison Keys, 19, had a 7-5, 6-2 first-round win over fourth-seeded Dominika Cibulkova of Slovakia.On the men’s side, James Duckworth beat sixth-seeded Gilles Simon, 6-2, 6-2, and Thanasi Kokkinakis beat eighth-seeded Julien Benneteau, 6-4, 6-3.■ Serena Williams recovered to beat Flavia Pennetta, 0-6, 6-3, 6-0, at the Hopman Cup in Perth, Australia. John Isner followed up with a 5-7, 7-5, 7-6 (4) win over Fabio Fognini, and Isner and Williams combined to win the doubles for a 3-0 United States victory over Italy. (AP)■ Ivo Karlovic served his 9,000th career ace in beating Lukas Rosol, 7-6 (4), 6-3, in the first round of the Qatar Open in Doha. Goran Ivanisevic and Andy Roddick also reached that mark.Earlier, sixth-seeded Richard Gasquet advanced to the second round, beating Pablo Andujar of Spain, 6-3, 7-5.A version of this brief appears in print on January 6, 2015, on Page B12 of the New York edition with the headline: Azarenka Loses at Brisbane.
