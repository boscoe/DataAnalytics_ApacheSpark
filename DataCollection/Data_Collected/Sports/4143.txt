By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 5, 2015
Real Madrid was disqualified from the Copa del Rey after fielding Denis Cheryshev, who was ineligible, in its opening round. Madrid said it would appeal the decision by the Spanish soccer federation.■ After a two-year search, David Beckham and a team of investors said they had secured a site in Miami where they hope to build an arena for an M.L.S. team. The league’s commissioner, Don Garber, said this week that if a deal for a stadium could not be completed, then Miami was not going to have an M.L.S. team. (REUTERS)■ Frannie Crouse scored in the 24th minute as Penn State beat Rutgers, 2-0, to reach the title game of the N.C.A.A. Women’s College Cup in Cary, N.C. The Nittany Lions will try to win their first national title on Sunday against Duke, a 2-0 winner over Florida State in the second semifinal.  ■ Host Schalke eased past Hannover, 3-1, in Germany’s Bundesliga to end a five-match winless streak.■ Paulo Dybala scored a stunning goal and set up the opener to propel Juventus to a 2-0 win at Lazio in Italy’s Serie A.■ Zlatan Ibrahimovic scored twice in a 3-0 victory at Nice to become Paris St.-Germain’s career leading scorer in the French league.A version of this brief appears in print on December 5, 2015, on Page D5 of the New York edition with the headline: Real Madrid Disqualified.
