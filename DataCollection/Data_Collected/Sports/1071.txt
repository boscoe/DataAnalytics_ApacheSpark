By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 17, 2015
Rafael Nadal beat a top-five player for the first time in more than a year with a 6-2, 6-1 win over Stan Wawrinka to reach the semifinals of the Shanghai Masters. Novak Djokovic also advanced, holding off Bernard Tomic in a tight first-set tiebreaker before closing it out, 7-6 (6), 6-1. Nadal had not won against a top-five player since last year’s French Open final, when he defeated Djokovic for his 14th Grand Slam title.Nadal will play Jo-Wilfried Tsonga in the semifinals after Tsonga topped Kevin Anderson, 7-6 (6), 5-7, 6-4. Djokovic will play Andy Murray, a 6-1, 6-3 winner over Tomas Berdych.■ Venus Williams closed in on a spot in the season-ending WTA Finals by beating Alizé Cornet, 6-1, 6-1, to reach the Hong Kong Open semifinals. Williams will meet Jelena Jankovic, who beat Daria Gavrilova, 6-1, 6-1. Angelique Kerber and Samantha Stosur also advanced and will face each other in the semifinals.A version of this brief appears in print on October 17, 2015, on Page D4 of the New York edition with the headline: Nadal Notches a Win.
