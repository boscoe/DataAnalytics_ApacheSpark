By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 29, 2015
Paul George still hopes that he can return to the Indiana Pacers’ lineup this season. George, a two-time All-Star, said after practice that he felt as if he had passed all the tests to play but that he was still waiting for clearance from the team’s medical staff before he made his season debut.
