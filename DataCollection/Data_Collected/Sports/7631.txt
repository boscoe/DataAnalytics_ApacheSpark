By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 6, 2015
Atlanta’s balance overcame Golden State’s one-two backcourt punch on Friday night. Jeff Teague’s 23 points led seven scorers in double figures, and the host Hawks beat the Warriors, 124-116, in a showdown between the N.B.A.’s two conference leaders.The matchup did not disappoint. The game was tied at halftime, and Atlanta led by 4 entering the fourth quarter.
