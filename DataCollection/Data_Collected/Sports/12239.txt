By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 2, 2015
The Swiss rookie Stefan Küng rode to an impressive solo victory on the rain-soaked Tour of Romandie’s 105.5-mile fourth stage from La Neuveville to Fribourg in Switzerland. Küng, 21, the track world champion in pursuit, finished 38 seconds ahead of Jan Bakelants.
