By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 20, 2015
LOS ANGELES —  Stephen Curry scored 40 points, and the Golden State Warriors rallied from a 23-point first-half deficit to beat the Los Angeles Clippers, 124-117, on Thursday night for their 13th consecutive victory to start the season.Klay Thompson added 25 points, Harrison Barnes had 21 and Draymond Green scored 19 for the Warriors, one of four teams in N.B.A. history to open 13-0.
