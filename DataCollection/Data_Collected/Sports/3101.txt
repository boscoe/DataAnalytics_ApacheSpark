By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 16, 2015
The Seattle Mariners acquired outfielder Leonys Martin, as well as reliever Anthony Bass, from Texas, sending reliever Tom Wilhelmsen and outfielder James Jones to the Rangers. Martin is coming off a down year with the Rangers but is considered excellent defensively and will probably start in center field.
