Brook Lopez scored 24 points, Joe Johnson added 22, and the host Nets held on to top the Houston Rockets, 110-105, on Tuesday night and sweep the season series for the first time in 14 years.“I thought it was great for our team,” Johnson said. “We needed some type of spark.”Thaddeus Young finished with 20 points and 12 rebounds for the Nets, who have recorded two of their six wins this season against Houston.
