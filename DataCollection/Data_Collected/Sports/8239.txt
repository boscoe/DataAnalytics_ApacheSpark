By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 18, 2015
The N.C.A.A. placed West Virginia’s athletic department on two years’ probation for recruiting violations involving illegal text messages and phone calls in 14 sports. Most of the infractions occurred in football and in women’s gymnastics, basketball and soccer.
