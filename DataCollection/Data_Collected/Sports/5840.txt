By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 6, 2015
A Zach Auguste basket off an offensive rebound with 1 minute 7 seconds left helped No. 13 Notre Dame hold off No. 18 North Carolina (11-4, 1-1 Atlantic Coast Conference), 71-70, in Chapel Hill, N.C.The Fighting Irish (15-1, 3-0) are off to their best start in 15 seasons under Mike Brey.■ Juwan Staten scored 16 points, and Devin Williams added 14 to lead No. 14 West Virginia (14-1, 2-0 Big 12) over host Texas Tech (10-5, 0-2), 78-67.■ Andraya Carter scored a career-high 19 points, and the No. 7 Tennessee women used a solid defensive effort to beat visiting Vanderbilt, 57-49.Ariel Massengale added 12 points for Tennessee (12-2, 2-0 Southeastern Conference), which has won eight straight. Vanderbilt (8-6, 0-2) has lost four of its last five.
