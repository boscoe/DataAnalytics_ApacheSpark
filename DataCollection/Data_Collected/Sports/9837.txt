By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 20, 2015
Missy Franklin won the 200-yard individual medley at the N.C.A.A. women’s swimming and diving championships, her final college meet. Franklin, a four-time Olympic gold medalist, finished in 1 minute 52.11 seconds on the first night at the Greensboro Aquatic Center in North Carolina. Franklin plans to turn pro after the three-day meet ends Saturday, having completed two years of college.A version of this brief appears in print on March 20, 2015, on Page B10 of the New York edition with the headline: Franklin Triumphs in Her Final College Meet.
