By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 24, 2015
Jessie Diggins and Caitlin Gregg won the first major championship medals for American women in long-distance cross-country skiing racing. Diggins and Gregg stunned the established favorites by taking silver and bronze in the 10-kilometer freestyle event at the Nordic skiing world championships in Falun, Sweden, behind the winner, Charlotte Kalla of Sweden.■ The former downhill world champion Marion Rolland of France retired after sustaining serious knee injuries over the past two years.A version of this brief appears in print on February 25, 2015, on Page B16 of the New York edition with the headline: Cross-Country Feat for U.S. Women.
