By BEN SHPIGELJAN. 10, 2015
Jets running back Chris Johnson was arrested Friday in Orlando, Fla., on misdemeanor weapons charges, the police said. Johnson was charged with open carrying of weapons/firearms, a second-degree misdemeanor in Florida.Johnson was pulled over for failing to stop at a stop sign, and after an officer approached his car, she saw the handle of a firearm between Johnson’s feet, she said.Johnson, 29, recently finished the first season of a two-year, $8 million deal, rushing for 663 yards and one touchdown.
