By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 12, 2015
Bayern Munich cruised into the Champions League quarterfinals with a 7-0 win over visiting Shakhtar Donetsk. Olexandr Kucher of Shakhtar was sent off in the third minute for tripping Mario Götze. It was the fastest red card in the competition’s history.■ A Greek Cup quarterfinal between visiting Olympiakos and second-division AEK was abandoned because a few AEK fans invaded the field after a late Olympiakos goal.
