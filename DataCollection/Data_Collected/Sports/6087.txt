By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 9, 2015
Russell Henley ran off eight birdies on a gorgeous day with little wind for an eight-under-par 65, giving him a one-shot lead over Bae Sang-Moon in the opening round of the Hyundai Tournament of Champions in Kapalua, Hawaii. Bae opened with six birdies in eight holes.■ Andy Sullivan shot a two-under-par 70 to take a one-shot lead at the South African Open. Ernie Els wiped out a promising start with a 77 in the second round to tumble out of contention at his home tournament in Johannesburg.■ Tiger Woods will start the year at the Phoenix Open beginning Jan. 29. He has not played there in 14 years.A version of this brief appears in print on January 10, 2015, on Page D6 of the New York edition with the headline: Henley Leads in Hawaii.
