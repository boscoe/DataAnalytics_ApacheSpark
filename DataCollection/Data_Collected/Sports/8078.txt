By DAVID WALDSTEINFEB. 16, 2015
The Yankees are set to retire Andy Pettitte’s No. 46 jersey and honor him with a plaque in Yankee Stadium’s Monument Park in a ceremony on Aug. 23. Pettitte’s son Josh, a pitcher at Rice University, broke the news on his Twitter account that the Yankees would honor his father.Pettitte went 219-127 in the regular season for the Yankees, with a 3.94 E.R.A. He won another 18 games with the team in the postseason on his way to five World Series titles.A version of this brief appears in print on February 16, 2015, on Page D6 of the New York edition with the headline: Yankees Set to Retire Pettitte’s Jersey.
