Alex Colome and four relievers combined for a four-hitter, and the Tampa Bay Rays beat Baltimore, 2-0, in St. Petersburg, Fla., on Friday night in a relocated home game for the Orioles.The three-game series was moved from Camden Yards to Tropicana Field this week in the wake of rioting that broke out in Baltimore after the funeral of Freddie Gray, who died in police custody after sustaining a spinal cord injury.Colome (1-0), a right-hander who was reinstated from the 15-day disabled list before the game, allowed three hits and struck out six in five innings in his season debut.
