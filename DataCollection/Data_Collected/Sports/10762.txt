By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 3, 2015
Deron Williams scored a season-high 31 points, Thaddeus Young made the tiebreaking basket with 22 seconds left, and the host Nets beat the Toronto Raptors, 114-109, for their season-best sixth straight victory.
