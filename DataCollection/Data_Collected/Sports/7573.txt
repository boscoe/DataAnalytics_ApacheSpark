By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 5, 2015
Brooke Pancake shot a six-under-par 67 to take a one-stroke lead in the suspended first round of the Bahamas L.P.G.A. Classic. Play was suspended in the afternoon when more than an inch of rain fell on Atlantis Resort’s Ocean Club course in Paradise Island. In May 2013, the inaugural event was reduced to three 12-hole rounds because of flooding.The second-ranked Inbee Park was tied for second with Natalie Gulbis and Brittany Lincicome.■ Lee Westwood mounted the defense of his Malaysian Open title in Kuala Lumpur with a six-under 66 to take a share of the first-round lead with Graeme McDowell.
