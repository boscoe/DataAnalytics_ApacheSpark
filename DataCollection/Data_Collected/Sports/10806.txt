By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 5, 2015
Indiana’s All-Star guard Paul George will make his N.B.A. season debut on Sunday, just in time to help in the Pacers’ last-gasp playoff push. George has not played since breaking his right leg during a training game in August with the United States national team, which went on to win last year’s Basketball World Cup. George, 24, sustained a compound fracture of the bones of his lower leg in a collision with James Harden.The Pacers were a game and a half behind the Miami Heat for the eighth and final playoff spot in the Eastern Conference entering Saturday’s games.■ The Atlanta Hawks signed forward Austin Daye to a multiyear contract.
