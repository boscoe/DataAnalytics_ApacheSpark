By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 15, 2015
Novak Djokovic extended his winning streak to 13 matches with a 6-2, 6-1 win over Martin Klizan in the second round of the Shanghai Masters. Rafael Nadal also advanced to the third round with a 7-5, 6-7 (4), 7-6 (4) win over Ivo Karlovic.Kei Nishikori overcame a stiff challenge from Nick Kyrgios to advance with a 1-6, 6-4, 6-4 victory. After winning the first set in 30 minutes, Kyrgios began to unravel at the end of the second set, smacking a ball in frustration toward the baseline and causing a line judge to bend to avoid being hit.■ Caroline Wozniacki rallied to beat Mirjana Lucic-Baroni, 3-6, 7-6 (3), 6-4 at the Generali Ladies in Linz, Austria. Third-seeded Roberta Vinci lost her first-round match against Aleksandra Krunic, 6-1, 6-7 (3), 6-1.A version of this brief appears in print on October 15, 2015, on Page B16 of the New York edition with the headline: Djokovic Wins 13th Straight.
