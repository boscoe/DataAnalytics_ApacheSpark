By BENJAMIN HOFFMANDEC. 27, 2015
Bears (5-9) at Buccaneers (6-8)1 p.m. Line: Buccaneers by 3The Buccaneers have had an unexpectedly nice run but last week were eliminated from playoff contention. There are clearly promising signs, like the emergence of quarterback Jameis Winston. Coach Lovie Smith has seemingly found a connection with the players that he can build on next season. That type of optimism is absent in Chicago, where a bad season has turned ugly. Smith, who coached the Bears the last time they made the postseason, could give his former team a fourth consecutive loss while keeping his own team’s hopes for a .500 season alive.Pick: BuccaneersWe’re interested in your feedback on this page.
