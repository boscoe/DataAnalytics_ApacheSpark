By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 25, 2015
Weather disrupted Nascar’s top series for a second consecutive week when Saturday night’s Sprint Cup Series race was postponed because of persistent rain at Richmond International Raceway in Virginia. Nascar rescheduled the event for 1 p.m. Sunday. Rain delayed last Sunday’s race at Bristol Motor Speedway in Tennessee, and the event took nearly nine hours to complete from start to finish.A version of this brief appears in print on April 26, 2015, on Page SP4 of the New York edition with the headline: Nascar Postpones Race.
