After Monroe tied it at 96-96, Michael Carter-Williams stole the ball from Bojan Bogdanovic at the 1:30 mark, leading to Middleton’s 18-foot jumper for a 98-96 Bucks lead. The Nets’ Joe Johnson missed a 3-point attempt, and Carter-Williams then attacked the rim and drew a foul on Thomas Robinson. Carter-Williams converted one of two free throws to extend the lead to 99-96.CAVALIERS 107, 76ERS 100  LeBron James became the youngest player to reach 25,000 career points, leading Cleveland past host Philadelphia. James, who is 30, had 22 points, 11 assists and 9 rebounds and rallied the Cavaliers in the second half against a scrappy 76ers team that had built a 15-point lead. But James and the rest of the Cavaliers, the Eastern Conference champions, had enough to push back the undermanned Sixers.James scored the milestone basket on a dunk with 8:07 left in the fourth quarter. He became the sixth active player with 25,000 points and the 20th over all.Jahlil Okafor, the No. 3 overall pick in this year’s draft, fought through foul trouble and led the Sixers with 24 points.ROCKETS 110, THUNDER 105  James Harden scored 37 points, and host Houston beat Oklahoma City for the Rockets’ first win of the season. An 8-1 run by the Rockets made it 107-100 with about two minutes left.Kevin Durant led the Thunder with 29 points, and Russell Westbrook added 25 points, 8 rebounds and 11 assists. T’WOLVES 106, BLAZERS 101 Damian Lillard had 34 points and 7 assists, and visiting Portland beat Minnesota after the Timberwolves honored Flip Saunders with an emotional pregame tribute.Kevin Martin scored 24 points, and Ricky Rubio had 12 points, 9 assists and 9 rebounds for the Timberwolves, two days after they attended the funeral of Saunders, their former coach and president of basketball operations.
