Ridley, a former Patriot who agreed to a one-year deal, played in only six games last season before tearing his right anterior cruciate ligament. Ridley rushed for a career-high 1,263 yards and 12 touchdowns in the 2012 season. He had 340 rushing yards last season before he was hurt. The Jets, who have been busy in free agency under their new coach, Todd Bowles, and General Manager Mike Maccagnan, also have Chris Ivory and Bilal Powell at running back.The Jets also signed the former Vikings linebacker Erin Henderson, who sat out last season. Henderson was released by Minnesota on Feb. 7, 2014, after being arrested twice in a six-week period on charges of driving under the influence. Henderson told USA Today last year that he had entered a rehabilitation facility and considered himself “a recovering alcoholic.”■ The N.F.L. has its first full-time female game official. Sarah Thomas, who has worked exhibition games, will be a line judge for the 2015 season, the league announced. Thomas, 41, was in the league’s officiating development program in 2013 and 2014 and worked some team minicamps last year.
