By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 21, 2015
Top-seeded Simona Halep earned her second title of the year by beating 17th-seeded Karolina Pliskova, 6-4, 7-6 (4), in the Dubai Championships final in the United Arab Emirates.■ Top-seeded Rafael Nadal played until 3:18 a.m. Saturday before beating No. 6 seed Pablo Cuevas, 4-6, 7-5, 6-0, in the Rio Open in Rio de Janeiro. The ATP said one non-Grand Slam match ended later — at 3:24 a.m. in Tokyo in 2006. Nadal had about 16 hours to recover before facing No. 4 Fabio Fognini in the semifinals. In another semifinal at the ATP-WTA event, No. 6 seed Anna Schmiedlova beat No. 2 Irina-Camelia Begu, 6-3, 4-6, 6-2.■ Donald Young moved a step closer to his first ATP Tour title, beating Bernard Tomic, 4-6, 6-4, 6-2, in the Delray Beach Open semifinals.■ Gaël Monfils defeated fourth-seeded Roberto Bautista Agut to reach the final of the Open 13 with an easy 6-4, 6-2 win in Marseille, France.A version of this brief appears in print on February 22, 2015, on Page SP11 of the New York edition with the headline: Halep Wins a Second Title With a Victory in Dubai.
