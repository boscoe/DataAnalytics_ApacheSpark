By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 2, 2015
Jordan Spieth was voted the PGA Tour player of the year on Friday. The tour did not disclose how many votes Spieth received, although he removed any suspense about the award’s winner last week with his four-shot victory in the Tour Championship to win the Fed Ex Cup. Spieth won five tournaments this year, including the Masters and the United States Open.■ Jim Furyk removed himself from the Presidents Cup, which will begin in South Korea on Thursday, because of a wrist injury. He was replaced on the American team by J. B. Holmes.■ Jimmy Mullen and Anthony Wall shared a one-stroke lead after the second round of the Alfred Dunhill Links Championship in Scotland. Mullen, who is making his professional debut, added a three-under-par 69 to his opening 64 for an 11-under 133.
