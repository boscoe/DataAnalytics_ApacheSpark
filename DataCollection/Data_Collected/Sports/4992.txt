By ALLAN KREDADEC. 19, 2015
After an incident between fans and Barclays Center security guards during Tuesday’s 5-1 loss to the Florida Panthers, arena officials said alcohol sales would be cut off after the second period of Islanders games. Sales had previously taken place until the 12-minute mark of the third period. Barclays Center also said it would enforce a limit of two drinks per purchase.■ Bryan Little scored two first-period goals, Blake Wheeler had four assists, and the Winnipeg Jets beat the visiting Rangers, 5-2. Henrik Lundqvist made 31 saves and fell to 15-8-3 on the season.
