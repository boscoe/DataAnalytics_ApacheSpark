By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 13, 2015
Green Bay Packers Coach Mike McCarthy said he would no longer call plays for the team’s offense. McCarthy is handing off the duties to Tom Clements, who was promoted from offensive coordinator to associate head coach. McCarthy had called the offense’s plays since he was hired as the Packers’ coach in January 2006.■ The former Giants cornerback Terrell Thomas, 30, is retiring after his career was shortened by three reconstructive operations on his right knee.
