By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 26, 2015
Jimmy Roberts, a five-time Stanley Cup champion with the Montreal Canadiens and an original member of the St. Louis Blues who went on to coach the Buffalo Sabres and the Hartford Whalers, died on Friday. He was 75.   His death was announced by the Blues, who said the cause was cancer.
