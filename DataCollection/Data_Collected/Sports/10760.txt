By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 3, 2015
Andy Murray lost his first service game in each set but advanced to the Miami Open final in Key Biscayne, Fla., by beating Tomas Berdych, 6-4, 6-4. Aside from his two shaky service games, Murray played almost flawless tennis against Berdych and totaled only eight unforced errors. His opponent Sunday will be Novak Djokovic, who outserved John Isner and won their semifinal, 7-6 (3), 6-2.
