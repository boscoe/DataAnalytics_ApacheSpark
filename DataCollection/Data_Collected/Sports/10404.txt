By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 29, 2015
Evan Rodrigues scored with 2 minutes 24 seconds left in the third period, and Boston University held on to beat Minnesota-Duluth, 3-2, in the N.C.A.A. hockey tournament’s Northeast Regional championship in Manchester, N.H., sending the Terriers home with a berth in the Frozen Four.■ Grant Arnold scored twice, and Trevor Moore had a goal and an assist to help Denver beat Boston College, 5-2, in the East Regional semifinals of the N.C.A.A. hockey tournament in Providence, R.I. Denver (24-13-2) will play Miami (Ohio) or Providence on Sunday for a berth in the Frozen Four.■ Jocelyne Lamoureux, Kacey Bellamy and Kendall Coyne scored power-play goals in the first period, and the United States opened the women’s world hockey championships with a 4-2 victory over Canada in Malmo, Sweden.A version of this brief appears in print on March 29, 2015, on Page SP8 of the New York edition with the headline: B.U.
