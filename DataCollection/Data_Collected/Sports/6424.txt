By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 15, 2015
Top-seeded Serena Williams will play Alison Van Uytvanck of Belgium in a first-round match at the Australian Open as Williams begins an attempt to win her 19th Grand Slam singles title. Williams could meet the former No. 1 player Caroline Wozniacki in the quarterfinals and the Wimbledon champion Petra Kvitova in the semifinals. On the men’s side, No. 2 Roger Federer and No. 3 Rafael Nadal were placed in the same half of the draw.
