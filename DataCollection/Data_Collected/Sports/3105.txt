By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 16, 2015
Ireland qualified for next year’s European Championship as Jonathan Walters returned from a suspension to score twice in a 2-0 victory over Bosnia-Herzegovina in Dublin. Ireland won the playoff by 3-1 on aggregate.■ UEFA, soccer’s governing body in Europe, confirmed that the European Championship would be held in France as planned despite the Paris attacks on Friday. However, the Belgian soccer federation said a friendly between Belgium and Spain that had been scheduled to be played in Brussels on Tuesday was postponed. (REUTERS)■ Barcelona forward Lionel Messi, who injured his knee in September, was back at practice, five days before the team was to play at Real Madrid.
