The 76ers committed 10 turnovers in the first that led to 19 Detroit points.Although the 76ers dropped to 1-23, Coach Brett Brown, who has a 38-150 record in two-plus seasons coaching the team, believes better days are ahead and signed a multiyear extension that was announced before the game. PELICANS 107, WIZARDS 105 Tyreke Evans scored 27 points and New Orleans rallied to win. Visiting Washington led by 97-90 in the fourth quarter, but Evans tied the score at 98-98 with a 3-pointer with 4 minutes 13 seconds left. Anthony Davis made a free throw with 1:47 remaining for the Pelicans’ first lead of the quarter, 104-103. They went on to win by hitting 3 of 4 free throws in the final 18 seconds.THUNDER 94, JAZZ 90 Kevin Durant scored 21 points, including the last 11 for Oklahoma City.Russell Westbrook had 18 points in the first half and finished with 24 points, 7 rebounds and 7 assists for the Thunder.Gordon Hayward led the host Jazz with 19 points and eight rebounds. Utah used a 16-4 run to tie it at 87-87, but never managed to take the lead. PACERS 96, HEAT 83 With Paul George struggling, Monta Ellis had his most complete game of the season and led Indiana with 24 points, 6 assists and 6 steals in a win over visiting Miami. Ellis’s 9 points, 4 assists and steal in the third quarter helped push the Pacers to a 77-68 advantage entering the final period after trailing by 48-47 at halftime. Chris Bosh finished with 23 points and 10 rebounds but did most of his work in the first half. Dwyane Wade also cooled in the second half, finishing with 20 points, 5 rebounds and 5 assists for the Heat, who dropped their third straight game. RAPTORS 93, BUCKS 80 DeMar DeRozan had 27 points and Toronto held off Milwaukee’s late charge for the win at home. The Raptors led by as many as 19 points in the latter stages of the third, only to have the Bucks cut the lead to 4 with 5:11 to go.But the Raptors scored the next 5 points, including a 3-pointer from Cory Joseph.The Bucks trimmed it back to 5 with 1:24 left, but Toronto guard Kyle Lowry punctuated the victory with a driving layup in traffic with 24 seconds left.CAVALIERS 111, MAGIC 76 LeBron James had 25 points and 8 assists and Cleveland won on the road.The Cavaliers never trailed in the game. They broke it open with a spurt late in the second quarter and started the third quarter with a 20-4 run.Cavaliers guard Iman Shumpert made his season debut after injuring his right wrist before training camp. He played 25 minutes and scored 14.HORNETS 123, GRIZZLIES 99 Kemba Walker had 33 points and 6 assists, and Nicolas Batum added 20 for Charlotte.
