By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 3, 2015
A 10-man Roma scored a late equalizer to salvage a 1-1 draw with Juventus and keep alive its faint Serie A title hopes. Seydou Keita headed in a goal from a corner in the 78th minute after Carlos Tevez had given Juventus the lead 14 minutes earlier with a free kick. Juventus maintained a 9-point lead over Roma.■ Allen Bula, Gibraltar’s first coach, was fired.
