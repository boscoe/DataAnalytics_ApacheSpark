By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 28, 2015
A man came forward saying that Jahlil Okafor, a 19-year-old rookie center for the Philadelphia 76ers, had assaulted him during a fight in Boston that was recorded on a cellphone and posted online, the police said.
