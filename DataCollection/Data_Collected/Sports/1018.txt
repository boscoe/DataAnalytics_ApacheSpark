By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 16, 2015
Novak Djokovic reached the quarterfinals of the Shanghai Masters with a 6-2, 6-3 win over Feliciano López. Andy Murray saved all seven break points he faced and beat John Isner, 6-7 (4), 6-4, 6-4, and Rafael Nadal defeated Milos Raonic, 6-3, 7-6 (3). Kei Nishikori was upset by Kevin Anderson, 7-6 (10), 7-6 (3).■ Venus Williams advanced to the quarterfinals of the Hong Kong Open by beating Wang Yafan, 6-0, 6-2. Williams will next play Alizé Cornet, who defeated Yaroslava Shvedova, 6-7 (4), 6-3, 6-4.■ Caroline Wozniacki became the latest seeded player to have an early exit at the Generali Ladies in Linz, Austria, losing to Kirsten Flipkens, 6-4, 6-4. Wozniacki’s defeat left the event without any of its top six seeded women in the quarterfinals.A version of this brief appears in print on October 16, 2015, on Page B12 of the New York edition with the headline: Djokovic and Murray Win.
