By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 14, 2015
Jamie Donaldson of Wales won the Thailand Championship for his first title of the year, beating Clément Sordet of France and the defending champion, Lee Westwood of England, by three strokes at Amata Country Club in Chonburi.Donaldson, ranked 68th, rallied from a two-shot deficit entering the final round to shoot a seven-under-par 65 for an overall 21-under 267. The victory gave Donaldson a berth in next year’s British Open.■ Lanny and Tucker Wadkins won the PNC Father/Son Challenge with an eagle on the first hole of a four-team playoff in Orlando, Fla.Tucker Wadkins made a 40-foot eagle putt on the par-5 18th at Ritz-Carlton Golf Club. He and his father had closed with a nine-under 63 to match Davis and Dru Love (63), Larry and Drew Nelson (62), and Fred and Taylor Funk (62) at 20 under in the scramble tournament.A version of this brief appears in print on December 14, 2015, on Page D8 of the New York edition with the headline: Donaldson Rallies to Win in Thailand.
