By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 26, 2015
The two-time champion Victoria Azarenka reached the second round of the Miami Open, beating Silvia Soler-Espinosa, 6-1, 6-3, and Sloane Stephens posted a 6-1, 6-3 win over Yanina Wickmayer.
