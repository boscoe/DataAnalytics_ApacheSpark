By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 24, 2015
The fourth-tier club Cambridge United held visiting Manchester United to a 0-0 draw in the fourth round of the F.A. Cup on Friday, earning a lucrative replay at Old Trafford. Radamel Falcao and Robin van Persie each missed good chances to score in the second half as star-studded Manchester United was repeatedly frustrated by Cambridge, the lowest-ranked club left in the competition.■ Olympique de Marseille’s road form continued to undermine its French title ambitions as it was beaten, 2-1, at 10-man Nice.  (REUTERS)■ Two Asian Cup quarterfinals in Australia ended in penalty shootouts as the United Arab Emirates knocked out Japan, the defending champion, and Iraq outlasted a short-handed Iran. (AP)■ Asamoah Gyan returned from a bout with malaria to score an injury-time winner and keep Ghana’s Africa Cup hopes alive with a 1-0 win over Algeria, the tournament favorite, in Equatorial Guinea. In the day’s other game, Senegal rallied for a 1-1 draw with South Africa.
