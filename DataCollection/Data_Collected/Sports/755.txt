By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 12, 2015
Matthew Fitzpatrick signaled his emergence as one of Europe’s most prominent young golfers, shooting a three-under 68 to complete a wire-to-wire victory in the British Masters in Woburn, England, for his first professional title.Fitzpatrick, a 21-year-old Englishman playing his first full professional season on the European Tour, won by two strokes to finish with a 15-under total of 269.A version of this brief appears in print on October 12, 2015, on Page D8 of the New York edition with the headline: A First for Fitzpatrick.
