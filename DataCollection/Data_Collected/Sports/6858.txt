By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 24, 2015
Ryan Palmer shot an 11-under-par 61 in the Humana Challenge in La Quinta, Calif. Palmer had two eagles and seven birdies over nine holes for the longest eagle-birdie streak in PGA Tour history.
