By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 16, 2015
Brook Lopez and Jarrett Jack each scored 26 points as the visiting Nets broke a seven-game losing streak with a 102-80 win over the Washington Wizards on Friday.The Nets took control in the third quarter, outscoring the Wizards, 28-18, to take a 71-58 lead into the fourth. Lopez had 20 of his 21 points in the second half. Jack had 19 of his 26 points in the final two quarters.
