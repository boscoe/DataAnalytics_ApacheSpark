By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 20, 2015
Arsenal remains at the bottom of Group F in the Champions League, but the Gunners finally got their campaign underway with a 2-0 win over Bayern Munich in London. After losing both of its opening matches for the first time, Arsenal desperately needed a win to keep alive its hopes of reaching the knockout rounds.Barcelona, the defending champion, remained in control of the top spot in Group E as Ivan Rakitic provided all the scoring in a 2-0 win at BATE Borisov. Roma came back from a 2-0 deficit in a 4-4 draw at Bayer Leverkusen.In Group G, Chelsea was held to a goalless draw at Dynamo Kiev.■ Major League Soccer shifted the Red Bulls’ road game against the Chicago Fire on Sunday to 7 p.m., from 5 p.m. The change allows the Red Bulls and F.C. Dallas, teams tied at 57 points atop the overall standings, to start simultaneously on the final day of the regular season.
