By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 6, 2015
Olli Jokinen and David Backes scored in the second period as the visiting St. Louis Blues topped the Chicago Blackhawks, 2-1, for their third straight win to move into first place in the Western Conference’s Central Division.■ Steve Mason made 46 saves, and the Philadelphia Flyers scored on three of four power-play opportunities to defeat visiting Pittsburgh, 4-1. The Penguins now face the prospect of playing for a postseason spot Tuesday in Ottawa. (AP)■ Peter Holland scored the winning goal in the seventh round of the shootout to lift the host Toronto Maple Leafs to a 3-2 victory over the Ottawa Senators. (AP)■ Mike Green had a goal and an assist, and the visiting Washington Capitals edged Detroit, 2-1, putting the Red Wings in an anxious spot as they try to extend their streak of postseason appearances. (AP)■ Carey Price stopped 24 shots to earn his league-leading 42nd win, Tomas Plekanec scored his 200th career goal, and the visiting Montreal Canadiens topped the Florida Panthers, 4-1.
