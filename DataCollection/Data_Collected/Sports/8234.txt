By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 19, 2015
Darren Clarke was selected as the captain of the European team for the 2016 Ryder Cup. He was chosen over Miguel Ángel Jiménez and Thomas Bjorn by a five-man selection panel that included the last three captains: Colin Montgomerie, José María Olazábal and Paul McGinley.A version of this brief appears in print on February 19, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Clarke Will Lead European Team.
