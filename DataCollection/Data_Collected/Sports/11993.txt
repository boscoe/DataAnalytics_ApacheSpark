By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 28, 2015
Thomas Bach, the International Olympic Committee president, said India was not ready to bid for the 2024 Olympics but could be a candidate to host the Games in the future. Bach spoke after meeting in New Delhi with Prime Minister Narendra Modi, who also said India needed to be better prepared before bidding for the Games.A version of this brief appears in print on April 28, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: India Called Not Ready to Host Games.
