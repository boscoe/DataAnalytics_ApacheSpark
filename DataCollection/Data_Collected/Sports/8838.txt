By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 3, 2015
The Kentucky Derby-winning jockeys Chris Antley, Victor Espinoza and Craig Perret are among the 10 finalists for election to the National Museum of Racing’s Hall of Fame. The field includes the jockey Corey Nakatani; the trainers King T. Leatherbury and David Whiteley; and the thoroughbreds Black Tie Affair, Kona Gold, Lava Man and Xtra Heat.A version of this brief appears in print on March 3, 2015, on Page B12 of the New York edition with the headline: 10 Are Finalists for Hall of Fame.
