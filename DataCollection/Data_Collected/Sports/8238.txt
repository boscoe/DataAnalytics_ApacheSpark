By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 18, 2015
Eighth-seeded Venus Williams, second-seeded Petra Kvitova and fourth-seeded Ana Ivanovic made early exits at the Dubai Tennis Championships in the United Arab Emirates. Williams, the defending champion, lost by 6-4, 6-2 to 11th-seeded Lucie Safarova. Kvitova fell, 6-3, 4-6, 6-3, to 13th-seeded Carla Suárez Navarro. Ivanovic lost to Karolina Pliskova, 6-2, 4-6, 6-4.A version of this brief appears in print on February 19, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: High Seeds Are Ousted in Dubai.
