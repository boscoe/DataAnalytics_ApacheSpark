By REUTERSFEB. 28, 2015
Roger Federer defeated Novak Djokovic, 6-3, 7-5, to win the Dubai Championships in the United Arab Emirates on Saturday for a seventh time. Federer converted his two break points with clinical efficiency, while Djokovic failed on all seven of his, and Federer’s serve blunted Djokovic’s baseline game.■ Rafael Nadal reached the Argentine Open final in Buenos Aires with a 7-6 (7), 6-2 victory over Carlos Berlocq. Nadal will play Juan Monaco, the 2007 champion, in Sunday’s final. Monaco beat Nicolas Almargo, the 2011 champion, 6-3, 6-7 (6), 6-4, in the other semifinal.■ Lucie Safarova claimed the biggest title of her career when she beat the former world No. 1 Victoria Azarenka, 6-4, 6-3, at the Qatar Open in Doha.■ Fifth-seeded Timea Bacsinszky of Switzerland won the Mexico Open for her second WTA Tour title, beating third-seeded Caroline Garcia of France, 6-3, 6-0, in Acapulco.
