By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 9, 2015
Nikola Mirotic had 20 points and 10 rebounds to lead the visiting Chicago Bulls past the Philadelphia 76ers, 111-88. Joakim Noah had been listed as a starter instead of Mirotic, but he ended up sitting out with a sore knee.Philadelphia (0-7) has lost 17 straight, dating to March 27 — the fourth-longest skid in team history.
