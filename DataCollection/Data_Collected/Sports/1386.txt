By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 22, 2015
Tristan Thompson, a restricted free-agent forward, has agreed in principle with Cleveland on a five-year, $82 million contract, ending his holdout and giving the Cavaliers frontcourt depth as they enter a new season, the team said. Thompson helped the Cavaliers get to the N.B.A. finals last season by filling in for the injured Kevin Love. Thompson, 24, averaged 9.6 points and 10.8 rebounds in the postseason.
