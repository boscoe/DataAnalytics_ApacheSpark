By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 3, 2015
Dwyane Wade sustained a knee injury in the first half, and the Miami Heat’s playoff chances took another blow with a 114-88 road loss to the Cleveland Cavaliers. LeBron James and Kyrie Irving each scored 23 points for Cleveland, which won its 17th straight at home.
