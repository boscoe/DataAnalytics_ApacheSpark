
OCT. 28, 2015

Game 1 of the World Series went on for more than five hours, ended after 1 a.m. Eastern time and outlasted every print deadline at The New York Times. Not one paper went to press with an account of the taut, dramatic contest in which the Mets thought they had the game won in the ninth, only to see the Royals celebrate the victory in the 14th. So, in a sport that takes its history very seriously, here is what Game 1 would have looked like in print if there had been a few more minutes on the clock when Alcides Escobar raced home with the winning run.
