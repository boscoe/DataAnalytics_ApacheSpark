By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 18, 2015
The academic performance of student-athletes will be a factor in how coaches and athletic directors at University of California campuses are evaluated and compensated under a new policy approved by the university system’s president, Janet Napolitano.The incentive-pay policy scheduled to be discussed by the university system’s governing board on Wednesday holds that athletic directors and coaches with winning records will be ineligible for bonuses unless their teams maintain a minimum level of academic achievement.A version of this brief appears in print on January 18, 2015, on Page SP10 of the New York edition with the headline: Linking Grades to Pay.
