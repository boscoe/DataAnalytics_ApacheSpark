By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 2, 2015
Canada showed its attacking power with a 6-1 victory over Latvia in the first game of the world hockey championship, while the United States beat Finland, last year’s runner-up, 5-1. Russia, the defending champion, opened with a 6-2 victory over Norway, and Sweden won in a shootout, 6-5, against the Czech Republic, which is hosting the event.A version of this brief appears in print on May 2, 2015, on Page D4 of the New York edition with the headline: Canada Defeats Latvia.
