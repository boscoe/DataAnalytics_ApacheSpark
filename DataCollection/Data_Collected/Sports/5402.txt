By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 27, 2015
Louis Belpedio and Auston Matthews scored in a 41-second span late in the third period to give the United States a 4-2 victory over Canada in Helsinki in their first round-robin game in the world junior hockey championship.In the other Group A game, Dmytro Timashov had two goals and an assist in Sweden’s 8-3 victory over Switzerland. In Group B, Russia edged the Czech Republic, 2-1, in a shootout, and Finland routed Belarus, 6-0.A version of this brief appears in print on December 27, 2015, on Page SP10 of the New York edition with the headline: U.S. Tops Canada in Juniors.
