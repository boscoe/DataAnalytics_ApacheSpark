By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 23, 2015
Tim Cahill of the Red Bulls scored with an overhead scissor kick and clinched a 2-0 win against China with a header, helping Australia reach the semifinals of the Asian Cup in Brisbane, Australia. South Korea earned a 2-0 win over Uzbekistan in a quarterfinal in Melbourne.■ Yassine Chikhaoui headed in a cross in the 89th minute to give Tunisia a 2-1 comeback win over Zambia in the Africa Cup of Nations in Equatorial Guinea. Congo goalkeeper Robert Kidiaba made three tough saves in the final minutes to preserve a 0-0 draw with Cape Verde. (AP)■ A new stadium in Cuiabá in west Brazil is being closed for “emergency repairs” seven months after it hosted World Cup matches.
