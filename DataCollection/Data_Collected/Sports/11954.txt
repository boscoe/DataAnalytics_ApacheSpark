By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 27, 2015
Top-seeded Kei Nishikori successfully defended his title at the Barcelona Open by beating Pablo Andujar, 6-4, 6-4.Nishikori, ranked fifth in the world, fought back from early breaks in both sets to earn his second clay-court title at the Real Club de Tenis in Barcelona, Spain. It was his second title of the season and his ninth career title.■ Angelique Kerber, the home favorite, rallied to upset Caroline Wozniacki, 3-6, 6-1, 7-5, in the final of the Porsche Grand Prix in Stuttgart, Germany, for her second straight title.
