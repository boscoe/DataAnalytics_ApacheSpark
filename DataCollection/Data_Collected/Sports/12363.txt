By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 3, 2015
Top-seeded Roger Federer claimed his 85th title with a 6-3, 7-6 (11) win over third-seeded Pablo Cuevas in the final of the Istanbul Open.Federer claimed his first title on clay since winning in Madrid in 2012. It was Federer’s third title this season, following victories in Brisbane and Dubai on hardcourts.■ Second-seeded Simona Halep and Venus Williams, the No. 16 seed, lost in the opening round of the Madrid Open, while top-seeded Serena Williams had a trouble-free debut. Halep lost, 7-6 (6), 6-3, to Alizé Cornet. Serena Williams defeated Madison Brengle, 6-0, 6-1.Venus Williams lost to Victoria Azarenka, 6-3, 7-5. Maria Sharapova advanced by defeating Timea Bacsinszky, 6-2, 6-3. (AP)■ Richard Gasquet won the Estoril Open final in straight sets, beating Nick Kyrgios, 6-3, 6-2, in Estoril, Portugal.■ The BMW Open final between Andy Murray and Philipp Kohlschreiber was postponed because of rain midway through the first set and is set to be completed Monday in Munich. Play was halted with Kohlschreiber leading by 3-2. (AP)A version of this brief appears in print on May 4, 2015, on Page D2 of the New York edition with the headline: Federer Takes Title on Clay.
