By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 20, 2015
Katina Powell, the escort who has claimed that a former member of the Louisville coaching staff hired her and other dancers to strip for and have sex with former recruits and players, said Tuesday that she had no proof that Coach Rick Pitino knew about her activities. But Powell also said in an interview on the ABC show “Good Morning America” that with “a boatload” of recruits and dancers and with “loud music, alcohol, security, cameras” in a dormitory, “how could Rick not know?”In a recently published book, Powell said the former staff member, Andre McGee, paid her to put on 22 shows.
