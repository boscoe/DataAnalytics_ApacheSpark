By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 26, 2015
Buddy Hield scored 34 points and Jordan Woodward had 28 — both career highs — to help No. 3 Oklahoma beat Harvard, 83-71, on Friday night in the Diamond Head Classic title game in Honolulu.Hield was 11 of 14 from the field and 9 of 10 from the free-throw line, and Woodward was 9 of 13 from the field and 6 of 7 from the line.The Sooners (11-0) shot 50 percent from the field (27 of 54) and made nine 3-pointers.Zena Edosomwan scored a career-high 25 points, shooting 10 of 20, and had 16 rebounds for Harvard (5-7).■ Roderick Bobbitt scored 22 of his 30 points in the second half, and Hawaii (9-2) beat Auburn, 79-67, in the third-place game at the Diamond Head Classic.AdvertisementKareem Canty led Auburn (6-5) with 22 points and 8 assists. Bryce Brown added 20 points, and Tyler Harris had 15 points and 9 rebounds.■ Chase Fischer scored 26 points, and Kyle Collinsworth added 12 points, 17 rebounds and 6 assists to lead Brigham Young (9-4) to an 84-76 win over Northern Iowa (8-5) in the fifth-place game at the Diamond Head Classic.AdvertisementMatt Bohannon scored 21 points to lead Northern Iowa, and Jeremy Morgan had 19.■ Josh Hawkinson and Ike Iroegbu each posted a double-double, helping Washington State (8-4) beat New Mexico (7-6), 82-59, for seventh place at the Diamond Head Classic.
