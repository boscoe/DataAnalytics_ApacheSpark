By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 25, 2015
The Suns suspended forward Markieff Morris for two games without pay for what Phoenix called “conduct detrimental to the team.” The suspension came a day after Morris threw a towel at Coach Jeff Hornacek in the fourth quarter of a 104-96 home loss to Denver.■ The Philadelphia 76ers acquired point guard Ish Smith from the New Orleans Pelicans for two second-round draft picks and waived guard Tony Wroten.
