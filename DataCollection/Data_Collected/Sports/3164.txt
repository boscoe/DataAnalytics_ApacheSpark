By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 18, 2015
Fresh off his Gold Glove last week, San Francisco Giants shortstop Brandon Crawford signed a six-year, $75 million contract that will take him through the 2021 season.■ The veteran infielder Cliff Pennington became the first free agent to switch teams this off-season when the Los Angeles Angels signed him to a two-year, $3.75 million deal. Pennington began last season with Arizona and was traded to Toronto in August.
