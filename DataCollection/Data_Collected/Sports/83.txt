By REUTERSOCT. 2, 2015
A tired Wales team edged past Fiji while France endured some uncomfortable moments in its win over Canada, as both sides made it three wins out of three in the Rugby World Cup.Despite a long injury list and playing in the toughest pool in the tournament’s history, Wales backed up its win over England on Saturday with a 23-13 defeat of Fiji in Pool A. The Welsh win means that England must beat Australia on Saturday or it will become the first main host nation not to make the group stage.France scored two late tries to secure a 41-18 win over a spirited Canadian team in Pool D.A version of this brief appears in print on October 2, 2015, on Page B10 of the New York edition with the headline: Wales and France Prevail in Cup.
