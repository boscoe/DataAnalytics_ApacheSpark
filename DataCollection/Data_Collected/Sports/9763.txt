By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 19, 2015
Carlos Tevez scored two goals and set up another as Juventus won, 3-0, at Borussia Dortmund to reach the Champions League quarterfinals with a 5-1 aggregate victory.■ Torpedo Moscow will play two home games behind closed doors as punishment after its fans taunted Zenit St. Petersburg striker Hulk with racist chants on Sunday.■ The Egyptian authorities decided to allow soccer matches to resume March 30 in closed stadiums without fans.
