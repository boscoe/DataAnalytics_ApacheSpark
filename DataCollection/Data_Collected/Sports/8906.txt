By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 4, 2015
Southampton revived its unlikely bid for Champions League qualification thanks to Sadio Mane’s 83rd-minute goal in a 1-0 home victory over Crystal Palace. The team jumped provisionally to fifth in the Premier League, a point behind Manchester United. Aston Villa climbed out of the relegation zone — at least for a day — thanks to a 2-1 win over West Bromwich Albion that was secured by a penalty converted by Christian Benteke in the fourth minute of injury time.A version of this brief appears in print on March 4, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Southampton Edges Way to 5th Place.
