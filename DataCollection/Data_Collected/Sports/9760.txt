By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 19, 2015
Andy Murray beat Adrian Mannarino, 6-3, 6-3, to reach the quarterfinals of the BNP Paribas Open in Indian Wells, Calif., earning his 496th career match win to tie Tim Henman as Britain’s most successful player of the Open era. Murray set up a match with Feliciano López, who beat the fifth seed, Kei Nishikori, 6-4, 7-6 (2).A version of this brief appears in print on March 19, 2015, on Page B18 of the New York edition with the headline: Murray Ties British Victory Mark.
