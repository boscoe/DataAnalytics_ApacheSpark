By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 8, 2015
Juventus overcame a first-leg deficit with a 3-0 win at Fiorentina to advance to the Italian Cup final. Juventus advanced, 4-2, on aggregate. Napoli hosts Lazio on Wednesday in the other semifinal after a 1-1 opening leg. Juventus is cruising toward its fourth straight Serie A title and faces Monaco in the Champions League quarterfinals.
