By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 28, 2015
Lara Gut of Switzerland won the World Cup giant slalom in Aspen, Colo., after Mikaela Shiffrin crashed while well ahead, with the finish line in sight. Gut completed the course in a two-run combined time of 2 minutes 2.51 seconds, edging Austria’s Eva-Maria Brem.
