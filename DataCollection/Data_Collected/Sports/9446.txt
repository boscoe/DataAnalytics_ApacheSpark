By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 14, 2015
Anna Fenninger of Austria vaulted back in the overall lead of the women’s World Cup standings Friday after winning a giant slalom race. Fenninger finished 0.71 of a second ahead of Nadia Fanchini of Italy in Are, Sweden. Fenninger bumped Tina Maze of Slovenia, who finished 20th, from the top of the overall standings.A version of this brief appears in print on March 14, 2015, on Page D6 of the New York edition with the headline: Austrian Grabs Overall Lead.
