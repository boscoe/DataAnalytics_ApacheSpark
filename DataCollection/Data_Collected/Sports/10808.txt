By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 5, 2015
The United States defended its women’s world hockey championship with a 7-5 victory over Canada in the final in Malmo, Sweden. Brianna Decker and Kendall Coyne scored for the United States in the third period after the Americans blew a 5-2 lead. The United States has won five of the last six world titles.A version of this brief appears in print on April 5, 2015, on Page SP14 of the New York edition with the headline: U.S. Captures World Title.
