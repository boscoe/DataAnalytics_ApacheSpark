By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 28, 2015
Top-seeded Novak Djokovic will face the defending champion, Roger Federer, in the final of the Dubai Championships after a win over Tomas Berdych, 6-0, 5-7, 6-4. Federer defeated Borna Coric, 6-2, 6-1.■ Victoria Azarenka reached the final of the Qatar Open after recovering from one set down to beat Venus Williams, 2-6, 6-2, 6-4. She will face Lucie Safarova, who beat Carla Suárez Navarro, 6-3, 6-2.■ Top-seeded Maria Sharapova withdrew before her Mexico Open semifinal against Caroline Garcia of France because of a stomach virus.A version of this brief appears in print on February 28, 2015, on Page D2 of the New York edition with the headline: Djokovic to Face Federer.
