By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 7, 2015
Martin Blackman, who has coached top junior players at tennis academies and worked for the United States Tennis Association at the national level, will be the association’s general manager of player development. Blackman, 45, a former top junior who spent six years on tour, will succeed Patrick McEnroe in the job and will be charged with finding the best way for the federation to nurture the next generation of American stars.A version of this brief appears in print on April 7, 2015, on Page B17 of the New York edition with the headline: U.S.T.A.
