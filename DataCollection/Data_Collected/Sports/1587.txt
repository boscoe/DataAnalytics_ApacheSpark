By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 24, 2015
Arjen Robben scored in his return from a groin injury as Bayern Munich defeated visiting Cologne, 4-0, on Saturday for the club’s 1,000th Bundesliga victory.Robben, who had not played since being injured in an international game on Sept. 3, broke a scoreless tie in the 35th minute.Arturo Vidal made it 2-0 five minutes later with a light deflection, Robert Lewandowski scored his 13th goal of the season with a header off a free kick in the 62nd, and Thomas Müller scored on a penalty in the 77th.Bayern, which lost its first match of the season at Arsenal in the Champions League on Tuesday, moved provisionally 10 points clear of Borussia Dortmund and extended its league-record start to 10 straight wins.AdvertisementAlso Saturday, Admir Mehmedi scored in the 89th minute for host Bayer Leverkusen, which overturned a two-goal deficit and beat Stuttgart, 4-3; Wolfsburg defeated host Darmstadt, 1-0; Eintracht Frankfurt came from behind to win by 2-1 at Hannover; and Werder Bremen won by 3-1 at Mainz. Mitchell Weiser’s 10th-minute deflected strike helped Hertha Berlin win the late game, 1-0, at Ingolstadt.AdvertisementMORE PRESSURE FOR MOURINHO Chelsea Manager José Mourinho faces more trouble from the English Football Association after being sent to the stands as the team’s poor Premier League start worsened with a 2-1 loss at West Ham.Mourinho will almost certainly face repercussions from the F.A.; the incident followed a one-match suspension this month.The defeat left Chelsea, the defending champion, 15th in the standings, with 11 points from 10 matches. Bookmakers made Mourinho the No. 2 favorite to be the next Premier League manager to be fired.The current favorite is Aston Villa’s Tim Sherwood, whose team lost its sixth league game in a row, 2-1, at home to Swansea.Later, Arsenal beat visiting Everton, 2-1, to move into first.Olivier Giroud and Laurent Koscielny scored in the 36th and 38th minutes for Arsenal before Ross Barkley’s deflected strike later in the half gave Everton hope of a comeback.Arsenal has 22 points.
