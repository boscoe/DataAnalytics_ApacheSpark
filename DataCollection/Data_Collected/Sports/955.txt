By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 15, 2015
Lamar Odom, a former N.B.A. star and reality television personality, was on life support Wednesday at a Las Vegas hospital, with his estranged wife, Khloe Kardashian, by his side. Odom was found unresponsive Tuesday afternoon after four days in a Nevada brothel, and the authorities sought a warrant for blood evidence of drugs.Hospital officials would not comment on the condition of Odom, 35. He was found face down at the Love Ranch in Nevada, said Dennis Hof, the brothel’s owner.Sheriff Sharon Wehrly of Nye County said at a news conference that a brothel employee who called 911 reported Odom used cocaine Saturday and had taken up to 10 tabs of a sexual performance enhancer over the past three days.■ Amir Johnson had 19 points, Isaiah Thomas had 17, and the Boston Celtics beat the Nets, 109-105, in a preseason game at Barclays Center.
