By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 11, 2015
Nearly a quarter-century after their epic 1991 match at the United States Open, Jimmy Connors and Aaron Krickstein had a rematch at an exhibition in Boca Raton, Fla. The 62-year-old Connors, who has had three hip replacements, said it would be his final match in public, and the 1991 result was reversed when the 47-year-old Krickstein won, 8-5.A version of this brief appears in print on February 11, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Krickstein Tops Connors in Exhibition.
