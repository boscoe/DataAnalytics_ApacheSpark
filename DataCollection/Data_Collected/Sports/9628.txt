By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 16, 2015
Pete Rose has submitted a new request to be reinstated to baseball, said the new commissioner, Rob Manfred. Rose, who agreed to a lifetime ban in August 1989, applied for reinstatement in 1997 and met in 2002 with Bud Selig, who never ruled on the application.■ The Philadelphia Phillies placed pitcher Cliff Lee, who has a torn common flexor tendon in his elbow, on the 60-day disabled list, and he will try to rehabilitate without having surgery.
