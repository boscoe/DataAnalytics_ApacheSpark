By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 10, 2015
The Oakland Athletics acquired shortstop Yunel Escobar and the versatile infielder Ben Zobrist, a two-time All-Star, from the Tampa Bay Rays on Saturday for catcher John Jaso and two minor leaguers.Zobrist, 33, helped the Rays make the playoffs in four of the past seven seasons. He hit .272 with 10 home runs and 52 runs batted in last season and made starts as a second baseman, a shortstop, an outfielder (in three spots) and a designated hitter.Jaso, a left-handed hitter, played parts of three seasons with the Rays.
