By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 15, 2015
Andy Murray defeated Vasek Pospisil, 6-1, 6-3, to reach the third round of the BNP Paribas Open, while the defending women’s champion Flavia Pennetta easily advanced with a 6-4, 6-2 victory over Madison Brengle on a 91-degree day in Indian Wells, Calif. The two-time champion Maria Sharapova needed six match points to put away Yanina Wickmeyer, 6-1, 7-5, in her bid to become the first woman to win the tournament three times.A version of this brief appears in print on March 15, 2015, on Page SP6 of the New York edition with the headline: Murray Reaches the Third Round.
