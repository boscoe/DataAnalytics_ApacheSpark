By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 5, 2015
The Detroit Tigers’ Victor Martinez needs surgery after injuring his left knee during his off-season workout program for the second time in four years. The team said Martinez is scheduled for surgery Tuesday but did not say when he would return. Martinez is a five-time All-Star who missed the 2012 season after injuring the same knee.■ The left-hander Wade Miley and the Boston Red Sox have agreed to a three-year, $19.25 million contract that avoided salary arbitration.
