By AGENCE FRANCE-PRESSEDEC. 14, 2015
Kento Momota and Nozomi Okuhara, both of Japan, captured the men’s and women’s singles titles at a year-end flagship event, the Super Series finals, in Dubai.Momota, who in 2014 helped his country win the Thomas Cup world team championship for the first time, achieved another milestone when he beat Viktor Axelsen of Denmark, 21-15, 21-12, in the men’s final.Okuhara had a tougher time in her match, but she prevailed, 22-20, 21-18, against the former world champion Wang Yihan of China.A version of this brief appears in print on December 14, 2015, on Page D8 of the New York edition with the headline: Japanese Players Take Titles in Dubai.
