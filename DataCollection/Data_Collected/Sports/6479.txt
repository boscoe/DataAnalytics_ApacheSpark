Promise Amukamara scored 20 points, and No. 14 Arizona State closed the game with a 19-6 run to defeat host California, 67-52, on Friday night.Quinn Dornstauder had 13 points, and Kelsey Moos scored all 10 of hers in the second half to go with nine rebounds as the Sun Devils (16-1, 5-0 Pacific-12 Conference) continued the best start in program history with their 12th straight win.Reshanda Gray had 17 points for the Golden Bears (11-5, 3-2), and Gabby Green added 13.California scored 5 straight to cut its deficit to 48-46, but Amukamara answered with a 3-pointer with 10 minutes 2 seconds to go, and the Sun Devils pulled away. Arizona State got 3-pointers from Dornstauder, Peace Amukamara and Katie Hempen on consecutive possessions to make it 59-49 with 7:12 left. Gray made California’s final basket with 6:03 to go and its final free throw about a minute later.Arizona State shot 52.2 percent from the field and had a 34-23 rebounding edge.OREGON ST. 73, WASH.
