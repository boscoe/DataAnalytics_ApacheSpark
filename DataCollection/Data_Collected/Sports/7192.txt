By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 30, 2015
The Jets have hired Rex Hogan as the team’s senior director of college scouting. Hogan spent the last 12 years with the Bears’ scouting department He will replace Terry Bradway, who was fired by Mike Maccagnan, the team’s new general manager. Coach Todd Bowles added three assistants to his staff: a tight ends coach, Jimmie Johnson; a defensive backs assistant, Daylon McCutcheon; and Ryan Slowik, a defensive line assistant and defensive quality control coach.A version of this brief appears in print on January 30, 2015, on Page B16 of the New York edition with the headline: Jets Hire College Scout.
