By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 29, 2015
Kohei Uchimura led Japan to its first world gymnastics title in nearly 40 years. Japan held off Britain and China in a tense final in Glasgow on Wednesday, with Uchimura clinching the gold medal despite a fall on high bar in the final routine of the night.
