By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 5, 2015
Hunter Pence broke his left forearm and will be sidelined six to eight weeks after being hit by a pitch in the San Francisco Giants’ 8-6 win over a Chicago Cubs split squad. Pence has played in 383 straight games — the longest active streak in the majors — since the Giants traded for him in July 2012. He hit .277 with 20 homers and 74 R.B.I. last year.■ The Texas Rangers’ ace, Yu Darvish, left his spring training debut with tightness in his right triceps after pitching a scoreless inning in a 5-4 loss to the Kansas City Royals in Surprise, Ariz. Darvish ended last season on the disabled list.A version of this brief appears in print on March 6, 2015, on Page B13 of the New York edition with the headline: Pence’s Arm Is Broken.
