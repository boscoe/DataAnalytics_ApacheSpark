By REUTERSOCT. 24, 2015
Theo Zwanziger, Germany’s former top soccer official, told the newsmagazine Der Spiegel that Germany had used a secret slush fund to pay bribes for the right to host the 2006 World Cup. His assertion contradicted denials by his successor, Wolfgang Niersbach, who was unavailable for comment.A version of this brief appears in print on October 24, 2015, on Page D4 of the New York edition with the headline: Bribes Paid, Official Says.
