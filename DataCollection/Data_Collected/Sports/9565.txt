By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 16, 2015
Chelsea failed to capitalize fully on Manchester City’s stumble in the Premier League title race, settling for a 1-1 draw with visiting Southampton, while Manchester United defeated Tottenham, 3-0, at home. Chelsea extended its lead over City to 6 points with a game in hand. City has lost two of its last three league games.■ Gareth Bale ended his scoring drought, scoring both of Real Madrid’s goals in a 2-0 victory over Levante that kept the club 1 point behind the Spanish leader Barcelona a week before the two teams meet.A version of this brief appears in print on March 16, 2015, on Page D10 of the New York edition with the headline: Draw Extends Chelsea’s Lead.
