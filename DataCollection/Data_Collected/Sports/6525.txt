By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 17, 2015
Viktor Troicki of Serbia beat Kazakhstan’s Mikhail Kukushkin, 6-2, 6-3, to win the Sydney International title in the first ATP final between two qualifiers.Troicki, once 12th in the world rankings, sank to 847th after a 12-month doping ban for failing to take a blood test during the Monte Carlo Masters in 2013. Troicki will play his first-round match at the Australian Open against another victorious weekend qualifier, Jiri Vesely of the Czech Republic, who won the Heineken Open in Auckland, New Zealand, on Saturday.A version of this brief appears in print on January 18, 2015, on Page SP10 of the New York edition with the headline: Title for Serbian Qualifier.
