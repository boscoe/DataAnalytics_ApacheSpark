By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 10, 2015
John Tavares broke a tie with his 37th goal of the season early in the third period and the Islanders pulled away to beat the host Pittsburgh Penguins, 3-1, on Friday night.The Islanders can finish no worse than third in the Metropolitan Division, but the Penguins failed to lock up a postseason berth. Pittsburgh still has several ways it can make the playoffs.■ Cam Atkinson scored on a rebound midway through the third period, and Sergei Bobrovsky made 23 saves to lead the host Columbus Blue Jackets to a 4-2 victory over Buffalo, assuring the Sabres the worst overall record in the league and the best chance at the No. 1 pick in the N.H.L. draft.A version of this brief appears in print on April 11, 2015, on Page D7 of the New York edition with the headline: Islanders Beat Penguins.
