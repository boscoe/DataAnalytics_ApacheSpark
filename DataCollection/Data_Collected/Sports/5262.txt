By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 24, 2015
An Ohio boxing official said a Michigan-based fighter had died, days after he collapsed and lost consciousness during his professional debut.The boxer has been identified as Hamzah Ajahmi, 19, of Dearborn. Bernie Profato, the Ohio Athletic Commission’s executive director, said Ajahmi died Tuesday at a Youngstown hospital after having surgery following Saturday’s fight.Profato said Ajahmi collapsed at the end of the fourth and final round in a flyweight bout against an Ohio boxer who also was making his professional debut.A version of this brief appears in print on December 24, 2015, on Page B13 of the New York edition with the headline: Fighter Dies After His First Pro Bout.
