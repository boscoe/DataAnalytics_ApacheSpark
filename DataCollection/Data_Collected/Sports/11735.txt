AdvertisementSupported bySports Briefing | SoccerBy The Associated PressA late goal by Javier Hernández gave Real Madrid a tense 1-0 win over 10-man Atlético Madrid in Madrid and a place in the Champions League semifinals. After the first leg of the quarterfinal had finished at 0-0 last week, Hernández found the net in the 88th minute from close range. By then, Atlético was down to 10 men after midfielder Arda Turan was sent off for a second yellow card in the 76th minute. ■ In the other match, Juventus drew, 0-0, at Monaco to reach the semifinals with a 1-0 victory on aggregate. The semifinals will feature four top teams that have all won European soccer’s premier club competition multiple times. Barcelona and Bayern Munich round out the last four. Advertisement
