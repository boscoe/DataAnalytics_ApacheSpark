By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 1, 2015
Jimmie Johnson pulled away after the final restart with 13 laps to go and won the Nascar Sprint Cup race at Atlanta Motor Speedway in Hampton, Ga., on Sunday. Johnson finished 1.8 seconds ahead of Kevin Harvick.
