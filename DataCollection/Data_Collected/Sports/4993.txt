By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 19, 2015
A Russian doping scandal took a new twist when the French newspaper Le Monde reported that Lamine Diack, a former president of the International Association of Athletics Federations, had asked the leader of the Russian track federation for more than $1 million to finance the political opposition in his native Senegal. Valentin Balakhnichev, then the president of the Russian track federation, told Le Monde that he had not had any discussions about political financing with Diack.A version of this brief appears in print on December 19, 2015, on Page D6 of the New York edition with the headline: Russian Scandal Widens.
