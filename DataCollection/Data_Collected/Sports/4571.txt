By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 12, 2015
Ohio State hired the former Tampa Bay Buccaneers and Rutgers coach Greg Schiano as co-defensive coordinator.Schiano has not coached the past two seasons since he was fired by Tampa Bay. He spent two seasons with the Bucs, going 11-21.Before that he rebuilt the Rutgers program, taking one of the worst teams in major college football and turning it into a consistent winner. From 2001-11, the Scarlet Knights went 68-67 under Schiano, going to bowl games in six of their last seven seasons.A version of this brief appears in print on December 12, 2015, on Page D4 of the New York edition with the headline: Ohio State Names Assistant.
