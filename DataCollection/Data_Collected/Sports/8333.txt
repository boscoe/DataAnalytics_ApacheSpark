By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 20, 2015
The Brazilian fighter Anderson Silva said in a statement published on his Instagram account that he was not a cheat and that he had “never used any substances” to improve his performances. The statement was his first since he was suspended this week along with other Ultimate Fighting Championship fighters after failed drug tests.A version of this brief appears in print on February 21, 2015, on Page D3 of the New York edition with the headline: Silva Denies That He Used Drugs.
