By BENJAMIN HOFFMANJAN. 9, 2015
Ravens at Patriots4:35 p.m., Line: Patriots by 7Something about the playoffs brings out the best in Joe Flacco. Fans may mock him regularly for labeling himself as an elite quarterback, but it is easy to make the case that he has been just that in the postseason.Flacco has won a record seven postseason games on the road, and over his last seven playoff games he has thrown for 17 touchdowns with one interception. After winning on the road again last Saturday, Coach John Harbaugh called him the best quarterback in football.“It obviously means a lot for a head coach to stand up in front of everybody in the whole world and say those kind of things,” Flacco said. “Whether it really means anything, I don’t know. But it definitely means a lot to me.”The weather will be colder for this game than for any other one the Ravens have played this season, and New England is historically tough to beat at home, but betting against Flacco in the playoffs is starting to become a fool’s errand.Pick: RavensPicks do not reflect the betting line.A version of this article appears in print on January 10, 2015, on Page D3 of the New York edition.
