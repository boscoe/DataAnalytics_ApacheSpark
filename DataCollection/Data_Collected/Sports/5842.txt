By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 6, 2015
Tony Wroten scored 20 points and hit a go-ahead layup with 9.1 seconds left to lift the host Philadelphia 76ers to a 95-92 win over the Cleveland Cavaliers, who did not have LeBron James and Kyrie Irving with them on the trip because of injuries.■ Pau Gasol had 27 points and 14 rebounds, Jimmy Butler scored 22 points, and the host Chicago Bulls won for the 13th time in 15 games, rallying past the Houston Rockets, 114-105.■ Kemba Walker scored 20 of his 33 points in the second half, and Cody Zeller finished with a career-high 20 points on a perfect shooting night, carrying the visiting Charlotte Hornets to a 104-95 win over the Boston Celtics.■ Arron Afflalo scored 34 points, and the Denver Nuggets handed the host Minnesota Timberwolves their 12th straight loss with a 110-101 victory.  (AP)■ John Wall had 15 points and 12 assists, and the Washington Wizards ended a three-game skid with a 92-85 victory over the host New Orleans Pelicans.
