By ZACH SCHONBRUNFEB. 19, 2015
GREENBURGH, N.Y. —  Carmelo Anthony underwent successful surgery on his left knee Thursday, a procedure that might require four to six months for recovery.“Let the rehab process begin,” Knicks Coach Derek Fisher said.The Knicks declined to say where the operation had taken place and what exactly had been done. On Wednesday, the team said that Anthony’s surgery would include “left knee patella tendon debridement and repair.”Fisher created a moment of confusion Thursday when he said the operation had been conducted on the right knee.
