By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 24, 2015
Juventus settled for a 2-1 home victory over Borussia Dortmund in the first leg of their Champions League last 16 tie. Carlos Tevez fired Juventus ahead in the 13th minute, but Dortmund soon leveled through striker Marco Reus after a terrible slip by Giorgio Chiellini. Alvaro Morata, who had set up the opener, restored Juventus’s lead shortly before halftime.■ An executive meeting of Greece’s top league was broken up after officials from the rivals Olympiakos and Panathinaikos were involved in a brawl. A Panathinaikos official, Vassilis Constantinou, walked out of the meeting holding his lower lip and said he had been punched by an Olympiakos security guard.A version of this brief appears in print on February 25, 2015, on Page B16 of the New York edition with the headline: Juventus Edges Past Dortmund.
