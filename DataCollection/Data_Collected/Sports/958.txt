By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 15, 2015
The Florida freshman defensive back Deiondre Porter was suspended indefinitely after being arrested on suspicion of firing a gun in the direction of his pregnant girlfriend. One of the team’s top special teams players, Porter was arrested on four felony charges and was being held on $160,000 bond in the Alachua County Jail.The episode, the police said, occurred at 2:15 a.m. on Sept. 29, three days after the eighth-ranked Gators beat Tennessee.
