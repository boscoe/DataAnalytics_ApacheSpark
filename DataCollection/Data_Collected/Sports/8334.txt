By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 20, 2015
Second-seeded Stan Wawrinka was knocked out of the Open 13 in Marseille, France, by Sergiy Stakhovsky of Ukraine with a 6-4, 3-6, 6-4 loss in the quarterfinals Friday.■ Top-seeded Sara Errani of Italy reached the semifinals of the Rio Open in Rio de Janeiro after Beatriz Haddad Maia of Brazil retired in the deciding third set. Haddad Maia stopped with the score at 3-6, 7-6 (2), 3-0 in Errani’s favor. (AP)■ Top-seeded Simona Halep beat Caroline Wozniacki, 2-6, 6-1, 6-1, to reach the final of the Dubai Tennis Championships in the United Arab Emirates. Halep will face 17th-seeded Karolina Pliskova, who reached Saturday’s final with a 6-4, 5-7, 7-5 victory over unseeded Garbiñe Muguruza. (AP)■ Donald Young upset third-seeded Alexandr Dolgopolov, 2-6, 6-3, 6-4, at the Delray Beach Open in Florida to reach back-to-back semifinals on the ATP Tour for the first time in his career. He will face Bernard Tomic for a spot in his first career ATP final.
