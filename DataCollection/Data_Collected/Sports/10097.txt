By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 23, 2015
A judge in South Africa convicted Bob Hewitt, 75, a 15-time Grand Slam doubles champion, of raping three girls he had been coaching. The assaults occurred in the 1980s and 1990s, prosecutors said.
