By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 19, 2015
Utah Coach Larry Krystkowiak said his team was not fixated on avenging its N.C.A.A. tournament loss to Duke in the round of 16 in March.That did not mean the Utes were not savoring the 77-75 overtime upset of the No. 7 Blue Devils at the Ameritas Insurance Classic at Madison Square Garden on Saturday. Kyle Kuzma scored 21 points to lead Utah (9-2), and Jakob Poeltl had 19 points and 14 rebounds.Duke’s Luke Kennard led all scorers with 24 points. The Blue Devils fell to 9-2.Duke’s Grayson Allen entered the game averaging 21.4 points, but he did not score until 1 minute 50 seconds into the second half and finished with 7 points.
