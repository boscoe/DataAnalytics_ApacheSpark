By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 20, 2015
Real Madrid, the defending champion, has been drawn to face its crosstown rival, Atlético Madrid, in the quarterfinals of the UEFA Champions League in a rematch of last year’s final. Bayern Munich the 2013 champion, will face F.C. Porto, while Paris St.-Germain will play Barcelona. The final matchup pits Juventus against Monaco. The first legs are scheduled for April 13-15, with the return games April 21-22.A version of this brief appears in print on March 21, 2015, on Page D7 of the New York edition with the headline: A Madrid Showdown.
