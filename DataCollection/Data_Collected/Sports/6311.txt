By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 14, 2015
The Yankees obtained a pitching prospect, Chris Martin, from the Colorado Rockies for cash. Martin, 28, made his big-league debut with the Rockies last April and had a 6.89 earned run average and 14 strikeouts in 15 ⅔ innings over 16 relief appearances.■ The American League champion Kansas City Royals and Manager Ned Yost agreed on a one-year contract extension that will keep him with the franchise through the 2016 season.A version of this brief appears in print on January 14, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Yankees Acquire a Pitching Prospect.
