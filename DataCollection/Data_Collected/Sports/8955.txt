No. 3 Duke has been carried all season by one of its four freshmen. Just never this one.That freshman, Grayson Allen, scored a season-best 27 points, and host Duke routed Wake Forest, 94-51, on Wednesday night for its 10th straight win.“Coach has been giving me opportunities, and I was able to capitalize today,” Allen said.Matt Jones added 17 points for the Blue Devils (27-3, 14-3), who shot 58 percent from the field and forced 19 turnovers in locking up the No. 2 seed in next week’s Atlantic Coast Conference tournament.Codi Miller-McIntyre scored 20 points for Wake Forest (13-17, 5-12), which has lost four of five.NOTRE DAME 71, LOUISVILLE 59 Demetrius Jackson scored 21 points, and No. 12 Notre Dame made its final seven shots against No. 16 Louisville for a road victory as the Fighting Irish clinched third place in the A.C.C.Jackson’s 3-pointer with 7 minutes 52 seconds left gave the Irish a 53-48 lead.
