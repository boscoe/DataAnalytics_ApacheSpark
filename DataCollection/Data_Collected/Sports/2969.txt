By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 15, 2015
Gerald Green returned to practice with the Miami Heat, though what exactly caused his extended absence from the team remains a secret. Green expressed remorse for an incident that occurred at his apartment building Nov. 4, but did not offer any new details. Green missed Miami’s past six games and was suspended for the last two for what the Heat described as conduct detrimental to the team.A version of this brief appears in print on November 15, 2015, on Page SP6 of the New York edition with the headline: Green Returns to Heat.
