By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 5, 2015
Raising his tally to six goals in seven matches since arriving on loan from Chelsea, Mohamed Salah scored twice and Fiorentina won, 2-1, at Juventus in the opening leg of the Italian Cup semifinals. Lazio and Napoli drew, 1-1, in the other semifinal.
