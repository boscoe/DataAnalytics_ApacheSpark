By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 7, 2015
Marcel Hirscher of Austria beat his close friend Felix Neureuther of Germany in front of 15,000 spectators in Zagreb, Croatia, at a World Cup night race, setting up a duel for this season’s slalom title. The two are well ahead in the standings, with Neureuther at 320 edging Hirscher by just 4 points.A version of this brief appears in print on January 7, 2015, on Page B10 of the New York edition with the headline: Hirscher Takes Slalom Event.
