By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 21, 2015
Mike Cammalleri scored twice, and the Devils kept up their good play at home with a 4-2 victory over the Vancouver Canucks. Jordin Tootoo and Patrik Elias also scored. Adam Larsson had three assists, and Cory Schneider had 21 saves against his former teammates.
