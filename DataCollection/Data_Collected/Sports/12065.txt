By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 29, 2015
Janko Tipsarevic, a wild-card entry, rallied to beat sixth-seeded Bernard Tomic, 5-7, 6-1, 7-6 (3), in the first round of the BMW Open clay-court tournament in Munich.
