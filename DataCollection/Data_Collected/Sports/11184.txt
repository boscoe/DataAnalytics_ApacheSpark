By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 12, 2015
Madison Keys reached the finals of the Family Circle Cup with a 6-1, 6-4 victory over Lucie Hradecka.Keys, 20, displayed serves clocked as high as 124 miles and hour in taking a quick lead on Hradecka, a qualifier playing her seventh match in eight days at the event in Charleston, S.C. She will face fifth-seeded Angelique Kerber, who ended Andrea Petkovic’s run of success at the tournament to advance to the final with a 6-4, 6-4 victory. Kerber outlasted Petkovic with steady groundstrokes and crisp forehands into the deep corners.■ Camila Giorgi upset the home favorite Agnieszka Radwanska, 6-4, 6-2, in the semifinals of the Katowice Open in Poland. The third-seeded Giorgi will play Anna Karolina Schmiedlova in the final. Schmiedlova beat Alison Van Uytvanck, 6-4, 6-1.■ Andy Murray married Kim Sears at the 12th-century cathedral in Dunblane, Scotland, bringing his hometown to a standstill. The couple was showered with confetti as they left the church.
