For pro skier Tim Durtschi’s birthday, he traveled into the backcountry of Jackson Hole, Wyo., to make a huge backcountry jump.Behind the Line is a video series produced by Teton Gravity Research that takes you into the minds of skiers. In each episode, the athletes will look back on a line, a jump or a session, and discuss the moment.What is the best birthday present you could give pro-skier Tim Durtschi? How about a big backcountry jump commonly referred to as a “booter”. Last year, Durtschi, along with Powder’s Breakthrough Skier of the Year Nick McNutt ,headed into the backcountry of Jackson Hole with the goal to build the perfect jump. After scoping out the landing and two days of building the pair had an epic time jumping off of Durtschi’s birthday gift.We’re interested in your feedback on this page. Tell us what you think.
