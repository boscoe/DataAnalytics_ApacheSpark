John Wall scored 17 points, Rasual Butler had 15, and Washington ended a five-game skid, coasting to a 114-77 home win over the Nets on Saturday night.The Wizards, who were missing guard Bradley Beal because of an injured toe, led throughout the game. The 37-point margin of victory was their largest of the season.Brook Lopez scored 19 points for the Nets, who had won three straight.BULLS 107, PELICANS 72 After the Pelicans All-Star Anthony Davis hurt his right shoulder on a hard, awkward fall on an alley-oop, Pau Gasol and Chicago pulled away for a victory at New Orleans. Gasol had 20 points and 15 rebounds for Chicago, which ended a three-game skid.
