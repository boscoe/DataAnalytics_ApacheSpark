By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 14, 2015
Pitcher Ivan Nova and the Yankees agreed to a one-year contract for $3.3 million, the same salary he earned in 2014, when he missed most of the season because of a torn elbow ligament.■ The Washington Nationals acquired infielder Yunel Escobar from the Oakland Athletics for the All-Star reliever Tyler Clippard. The A’s picked up Escobar from Tampa Bay in a trade Saturday, but the Nationals had been looking for a starting second baseman.■ The Houston Astros acquired the slugger Evan Gattis and the right-hander James Hoyt from the Atlanta Braves for three minor leaguers. Gattis hit .263 with 22 homers and 52 runs batted in for the Braves last season.A version of this brief appears in print on January 15, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Nova and Yanks Have a Deal.
