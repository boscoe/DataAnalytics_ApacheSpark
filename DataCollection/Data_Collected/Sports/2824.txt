By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 13, 2015
Justin Leonard opened the OHL Classic at Mayakoba Playa del Carmen, Mexico, with a six-under-par 65 to share the lead with Aaron Baddeley, Shawn Stefani and Derek Fathauer. Leonard played bogey-free at El Camaleón before wind and rain arrived, stopping play for 1 hour 14 minutes. The first round was suspended because of darkness, and 21 players did not finish.■ Inbee Park made a 15-foot birdie putt on the final hole for a four-under 68 and a one-stroke lead the L.P.G.A.
