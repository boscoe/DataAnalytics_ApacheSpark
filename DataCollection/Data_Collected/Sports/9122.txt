By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 7, 2015
Amnet Ruenroeng of Thailand, the reigning International Boxing Federation flyweight champion, handed Zou Shiming, the two-time Olympic gold medalist for China, his first loss. All three judges scored the bout 116-111 in favor of Amnet, who improved to 15-0 by defending his flyweight title for a third time.
