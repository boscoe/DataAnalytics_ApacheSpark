By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 8, 2015
Lionel Messi scored three goals on Sunday to help Barcelona rout visiting Rayo Vallecano, 6-1, and take the lead in the top Spanish league. Messi’s hat trick was the 32nd of his career in all competitions for Barcelona, breaking a Spanish record.■ Second-tier Blackburn held host Liverpool to a 0-0 draw in the F.A.
