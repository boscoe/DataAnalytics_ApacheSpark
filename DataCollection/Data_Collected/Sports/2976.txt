By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 15, 2015
The Iowa wrestling team shattered the N.C.A.A. attendance record in the first meet held in a Division I football stadium. The Hawkeyes beat top-ranked Oklahoma State, 18-16, at Kinnick Stadium in Iowa City in front of a crowd announced at more than 42,000.
