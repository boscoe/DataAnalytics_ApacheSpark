By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 8, 2015
California Chrome, the Kentucky Derby and Preakness Stakes winner; Bayern, the Breeders’ Cup Classic winner; and Main Sequence, the Breeders’ Cup Turf winner, are the finalists for horse of the year at the Eclipse Awards. The Hall of Famer Bob Baffert, Chad Brown and Todd Pletcher are vying for trainer of the year.
