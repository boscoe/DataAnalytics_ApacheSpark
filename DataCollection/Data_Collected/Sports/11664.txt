By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 20, 2015
Chris Antley, a jockey who twice won the Kentucky Derby, was elected to the National Museum of Racing’s Hall of Fame in Saratoga Springs, N.Y., along with the thoroughbreds Lava Man and Xtra Heat and the trainer King Leatherbury. They will be inducted Aug. 7.
