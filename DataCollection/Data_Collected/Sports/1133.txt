By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 18, 2015
Brendan Steele did just enough to keep the lead at the Frys.com Open in Napa, Calif., shooting a three-under-par 69 for a one-shot lead over Andrew Loupe and Kevin Na. Loupe had five straight birdies early and four straight birdies late for a 63, while Na had a 64.■ Lydia Ko and Sung Hyun Park rebounded to top the crowded leader board in the L.P.G.A.’s KEB Hana Bank Championship in Incheon, South Korea. In position to take the top spot in the world, the second-ranked Ko overcame a double bogey with two late birdies for a three-under-par 69. Park had a 67 to match Ko at 13 under.■ Andy Sullivan of England shot a four-under 67 in windy conditions to take a five-shot lead into Sunday’s final round at the Portugal Masters in Vilamoura.■ The defending champion Michael Allen shot a three-under 69 for a share of the lead with Scott McCarron in the Champions Tour’s San Antonio Championship.
