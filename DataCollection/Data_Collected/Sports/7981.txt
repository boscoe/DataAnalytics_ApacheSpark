By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 14, 2015
Brittany Bowe led an American one-two finish in the 1,000 meters at the speedskating world championships in Heerenveen, the Netherlands, edging her teammate Heather Richardson. Martina Sablikova of the Czech Republic added a victory in the 5,000 meters to her 3,000 win from Thursday. Denis Yuskov of Russia took more than a second off the track record to win the men’s 1,500 in 1:43.36.
