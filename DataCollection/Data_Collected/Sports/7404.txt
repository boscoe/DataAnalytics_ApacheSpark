By AGENCE FRANCE-PRESSEFEB. 3, 2015
Tiger Woods fell to 56th in the world rankings and was in danger of failing to qualify for next month’s World Golf Championships event in Florida.Woods, 39, a 14-time major champion, needs to reach the top 50 after the Northern Trust Open in California from Feb. 18-22, or the Honda Classic in Florida later this month, in order to qualify for the W.G.C.
