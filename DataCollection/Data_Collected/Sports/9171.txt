By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 8, 2015
Tony Parker had a season-high 32 points in leading the San Antonio Spurs to their fifth straight victory, a 116-105 home win over the Chicago Bulls.■ Gordon Hayward scored 24 points, including 5 straight to stop a Nets rally, and the Utah Jazz won in Brooklyn, 95-88. Derrick Favors had 22 points and 8 rebounds against the Nets, his former team, who lost their third straight.■ The New Zealand Breakers won their fourth title in five seasons in Australia’s National Basketball League by beating Cairns, 83-81, on a buzzer beater by Ekene Ibekwe.
