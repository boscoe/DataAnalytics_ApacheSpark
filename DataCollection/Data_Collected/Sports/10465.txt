By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 29, 2015
Hilary Knight had two goals and an assist as the United States beat Finland, 4-1, in Malmo, Sweden, to improve to 2-0 in Pool A play in the women’s world hockey championship. The United States will face Russia on Tuesday.■ Tom Parisi broke a tie on a power-play goal with 5 minutes 1 second left, and Providence defeated Denver, 4-1, in the N.C.A.A. East Regional final in Rhode Island to advance to the Frozen Four.Providence advanced to face Nebraska-Omaha, which routed Rochester Institute of Technology, 4-0, in the Midwest Regional final in South Bend, Ind., as Ryan Massa made 40 saves.Boston University will meet North Dakota in the other Frozen Four semifinal.
