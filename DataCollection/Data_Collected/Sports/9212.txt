By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 10, 2015
Anthony Davis scored 43 points, tying a career high, as the New Orleans Pelicans pulled away for a 114-103 victory over the host Milwaukee Bucks on Monday. The Pelicans shot 59 percent for the game and 60 percent from 3-point range. Giannis Antetokounmpo had a career-high 29 points for the Bucks.■ Kyle Korver and DeMarre Carroll each scored 20 points as the Atlanta Hawks became the first N.B.A. team to reach 50 wins, defeating the visiting Sacramento Kings, 130-105. The Hawks had their highest point total this season, and their 42 assists were the best in the N.B.A. this season.■ Marc Gasol scored 23 points to lead the Memphis Grizzlies past his brother Pau and the host Chicago Bulls, 101-91.■ The free-agent guard Candice Wiggins signed with the Liberty.
