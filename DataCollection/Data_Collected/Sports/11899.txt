By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 26, 2015
Caroline Wozniacki will play Angelique Kerber in the final of the Porsche Grand Prix in Stuttgart, Germany.Wozniacki beat second-seeded Simona Halep, 7-5, 5-7, 6-2, in a semifinal match that took almost three hours. In the other semifinal, Kerber, the home favorite, defeated Madison Brengle, 6-3, 6-1, in just under an hour.It will be Wozniacki’s second final at Stuttgart after she lost in 2011 to Julia Görges.A fourth-seeded Dane, she needed almost three hours to prevail over Halep, winning four games in a row in the third set to go ahead by 5-1. After missing three match points and being broken while serving for the match, Wozniacki broke Halep again to close out the win.A version of this brief appears in print on April 26, 2015, on Page SP4 of the New York edition with the headline: A Wozniacki-Kerber Final.
