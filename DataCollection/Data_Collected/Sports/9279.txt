By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 11, 2015
Barret Jackman scored his first goal in 36 games with 1 minute 3 seconds left, launching a shot from center ice to give the host St. Louis Blues a 5-4 win over the Winnipeg Jets. After his team squandered a three-goal, third-period lead, Jackman bailed out the Blues when he got control of the puck along the left boards in the neutral zone off a face-off. He appeared to be just dumping the puck into the Jets’ end, but his shot was on net and it handcuffed goalie Ondrej Pavelec.■ Tyler Johnson scored about a minute into overtime to lift the visiting Tampa Bay Lightning over the Montreal Canadiens, 1-0, in a battle between Eastern Conference leaders.
