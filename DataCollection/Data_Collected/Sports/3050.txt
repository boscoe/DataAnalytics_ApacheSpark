Derrick Favors scored 23 points, Rodney Hood added 20, and the visiting Utah Jazz beat the Atlanta Hawks, 97-96, on Sunday to end a three-game skid.Paul Millsap had a season-high 28 points for the Hawks but failed to hit the rim on a 3-point attempt in the final minute and missed a 12-footer at the buzzer.An Al Horford 3-pointer with 33 seconds left cut Atlanta’s deficit to 97-96. Favors missed a jumper on the next possession but grabbed the rebound from Millsap’s miss to seal it.CELTICS 100, THUNDER 85 Marcus Smart had a career-high 26 points for visiting Boston and guarded Russell Westbrook effectively. He was part of a group effort that held Westbrook to 5 of 20 shooting.
