By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 23, 2015
Connecticut defensive back Byron Jones soared 12 feet 3 inches in the broad jump at the N.F.L. scouting combine. That is eight inches better than any jump listed in the combine database. Jones’s vertical jump (44 ½ inches) was also the best of the day.■ Running back DeAngelo Williams told the Charlotte television station WBTV that the Carolina Panthers planned to release him.
