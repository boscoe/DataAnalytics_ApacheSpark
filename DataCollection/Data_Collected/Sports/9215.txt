By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 10, 2015
John Tavares scored 4 minutes 38 seconds into overtime, capping the visiting Islanders’ two-goal comeback in a 4-3 victory over the Toronto Maple Leafs. With his 33rd goal and his 39th assist, Tavares stretched his lead in the N.H.L. scoring race to 3 points.
