By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 29, 2015
Denny Hamlin passed his teammate Matt Kenseth for the lead with 28 laps to go and went on to claim Toyota’s first win in the Nascar Sprint Cup Series since May with a victory at Martinsville Speedway in Virginia on Sunday. ■ Juan Pablo Montoya gave Team Penske a win in IndyCar’s season-opening race in St. Petersburg, Fla., one year after returning to the series. Montoya grabbed the lead away from his teammate, Will Power, during a cycle of pit stops, then held off a strong challenge from Power to win.  (AP)■ Sebastian Vettel drove a flawless tactical race to claim his first victory for Ferrari at the Malaysian Grand Prix in Sepang, also registering the first Formula One win for Ferrari since 2013.
