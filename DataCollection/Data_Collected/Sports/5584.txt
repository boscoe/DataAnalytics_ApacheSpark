By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 31, 2015
Christian Benteke scored the winning goal as visiting Liverpool beat Sunderland, 1-0, in the Premier League to stay in the hunt for Champions League qualification. Benteke pounced on a flicked pass from Adam Lallana to place a low finish into the bottom corner of the goal just 22 seconds into the second half. Liverpool climbed to seventh in the standings, 5 points out of the top four with exactly half the season gone.■ Lionel Messi scored in his 500th match for host Barcelona to help the team ease past Real Betis, 4-0, and ensure it kept the lead in the top Spanish league.
