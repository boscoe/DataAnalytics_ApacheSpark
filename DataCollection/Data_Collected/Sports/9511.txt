By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 15, 2015
American Pharoah, last year’s 2-year-old champion, made his return to the track appear easy, winning the Rebel Stakes in Hot Springs, Ark., on Saturday by six and a quarter lengths despite a loose shoe on a sloppy track. The victory moved the colt to fifth in the Kentucky Derby point standings.A version of this brief appears in print on March 15, 2015, on Page SP6 of the New York edition with the headline: American Pharoah Wins in Hot Springs.
