By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 10, 2015
VAIL, Colo. —  The Austrian skier Eva-Maria Brem said that she would like to see the team event included in the Olympics as a medal competition.Of course she would. Her country is always on the podium.Marcel Hirscher helped top-seeded Austria hold off Canada and defend its gold medal in the team event on Tuesday at the Alpine World Ski Championships.The Austrians won the final, 3-1, over Canada, which was seeded 10th. Sweden earned the bronze medal, beating Switzerland.Austria has now won three golds and two silvers since the event became part of the world championships in 2005.
