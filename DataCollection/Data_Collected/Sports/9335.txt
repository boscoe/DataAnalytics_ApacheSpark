By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 12, 2015
Dodging Bullets held on for a victory in the Queen Mother Champion Chase as Sprinter Sacre, a popular former winner, was pulled up on the second day of the Cheltenham Festival in England. Sprinter Sacre was the top horse in Britain in 2013, when he won the Champion Chase by 19 lengths, but has not been the same since having a heart problem last year.A version of this brief appears in print on March 12, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Close Victory for Dodging Bullets.
