By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 15, 2015
Serena Williams withdrew from the Dubai Tennis Championships in the United Arab Emirates because of lingering effects of an upper respiratory illness she had last month. Williams had a bad cough in her last few matches at the Australian Open, which she won.Last weekend, Williams played one singles match for the United States in the Fed Cup against Argentina in Buenos Aires but was replaced by CoCo Vandeweghe on the second day.■ Daniela Hantuchova saved two match points to beat Marina Erakovic, 4-6, 6-3, 7-6 (3), in the semifinals of the Thailand Open in Pattaya. Ajla Tomljanovic beat Monica Puig, 7-6 (4), 5-7, 7-6 (7), and will face Hantuchova in Sunday’s final.■ Andrea Petkovic beat Barbora Zahlavova Strycova, 7-6 (2), 7-6 (6), in Antwerp, Belgium, to reach the Diamond Games final against Carla Suárez Navarro, who beat Karolina Pliskova, 6-2, 6-2.Advertisement■ Third-seeded Tomas Berdych dispatched Gilles Simon, 6-2, 6-1, and will face Stan Wawrinka in the World Tennis Tournament final in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. Wawrinka, the fourth seed, beat second-seeded Milos Raonic, 7-6 (3), 7-6 (7).■ Kevin Anderson used his big serve to beat the American Donald Young, 4-6, 6-3, 6-4, in the Memphis Open semifinals. Anderson will play either Kei Nishikori or the American Sam Querrey on Sunday.Advertisement■ The qualifier Luca Vanni defeated João Souza, 6-4, 6-7 (5), 6-4, at the Brazil Open in São Paulo, reaching his first ATP final.
