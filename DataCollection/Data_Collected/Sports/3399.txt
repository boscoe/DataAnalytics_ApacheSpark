AdvertisementSupported bySports Briefing | High School FootballBy The Associated PressA high school football game in Miami on Friday night ended early after gunshots were heard in the distance, sending players and coaches ducking for cover. Videos show terrified fans in the stands running to the exits after the gunshots were heard. Less than three minutes remained when officials called the game, between Miami Central and Miami Carol City. Central won, 26-8. A Miami-Dade schools police officer, Hilda Hernandez, said that three males were arrested and that three guns were recovered. Advertisement
