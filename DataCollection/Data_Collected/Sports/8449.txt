By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 22, 2015
Kentavious Caldwell-Pope had 26 points, Andre Drummond added 18 points and 16 rebounds and the new-look Detroit Pistons beat the visiting Washington Wizards, 106-89, on Sunday.The Pistons are 2-0 since Stan Van Gundy, the team’s coach and president, traded four players to get Reggie Jackson and Tayshaun Prince at the trading deadline Thursday.Greg Monroe had 15 points and 15 rebounds, and Jackson had 17 points as seven Detroit players scored in double figures.Marcin Gortat had 24 points and 10 rebounds for the Wizards.
