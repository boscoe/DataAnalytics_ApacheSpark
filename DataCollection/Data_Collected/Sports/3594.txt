Miles added 32 points, the most he has scored since joining the Pacers before the start of last season, as Indiana won for the ninth time in 11 games.Miles was 8 of 9 from beyond the arc, and George also missed only once from 3-point range.Pacers guard George Hill scored 14 points in 32 minutes after missing three games with an upper respiratory infection.The Pacers finished 73.1 percent (19 of 26) from 3-point range, crushing their previous season high of 44.4 percent (12 of 27).AdvertisementGary Neal scored 23 points off the bench for the Wizards, who had won three games in a row.Bradley Beal added 20 points, and John Wall scored 18 for Washington, which has lost five of its six games in which it has allowed more than 110 points.AdvertisementHAWKS 121, CELTICS 97 Paul Millsap scored 25 points, Jeff Teague added 16, and host Atlanta subdued Boston.Kyle Korver, Thabo Sefolosha and Mike Scott each finished with 15 points for the Hawks.Atlanta had dropped four of five and was coming off a loss Saturday at Cleveland in which Coach Mike Budenholzer was ejected and later fined $25,000 for bumping into the referee Ben Taylor.The Hawks essentially put the game out of reach with an 18-3 run that made it 100-80 with 6 minutes 20 seconds remaining on a 3-point play by Teague.Get the big sports news, highlights and analysis from Times journalists, with distinctive takes on games and some behind-the-scenes surprises, delivered to your inbox every week.Please verify you're not a robot by clicking the box.Invalid email address.
