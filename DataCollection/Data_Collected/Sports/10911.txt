By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 7, 2015
Two months after promising to ramp up the drug testing of its more than 500 fighters, U.F.C. hired the federal investigator Jeff Novitzky to spearhead its antidoping initiative. Novitzky will begin serving as U.F.C.’s vice president of health and performance later this month when he leaves his job as a special agent for the United States Food and Drug Administration.
