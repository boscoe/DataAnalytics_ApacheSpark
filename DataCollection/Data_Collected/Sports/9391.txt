By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 13, 2015
LOS ANGELES —  Tim Hardaway Jr. scored 22 points, Andrea Bargnani added 16, and the Knicks ended a five-game losing streak with only their fifth road victory of the season, a 101-94 win over the Los Angeles Lakers on Thursday night.Alexey Shved had 14 points and 11 rebounds as the Knicks ended a seven-game road skid against the Lakers and gave Coach Derek Fisher, a former Los Angeles point guard, a win in his return to Staples Center.The Knicks (13-51), who have the N.B.A.’s worst record, calmly maintained a lead down the stretch. They swept their season series with the Lakers (17-47) and picked up their first road victory over a Western Conference team all season.
