By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 27, 2015
Lukas Hrusik broke a tie 19 seconds into the third period, and Slovakia defeated Belarus, 4-2, in a Group B round-robin game in the world junior hockey championship in Helsinki.In Group A, Soeren Nielsen and Mathias From scored to help Denmark open with a 2-1 victory over Switzerland.
