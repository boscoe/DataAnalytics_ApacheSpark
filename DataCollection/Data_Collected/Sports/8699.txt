By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 27, 2015
Travis Kvapil’s Nascar Sprint Cup car was stolen early Friday from a hotel parking lot in Morrow, Ga., leading him to withdraw from a race this weekend.The No. 44 car, worth $250,000, could not be located in time for Nascar’s mandatory inspection, and Kvapil’s small-budget Team Xtreme did not have a backup car in town.A trailer with the racecar inside was hitched to a pickup truck; surveillance video showed the truck and trailer being driven out of the parking lot around 5:30 a.m., Detective Sgt. Larry Oglesby said.A version of this brief appears in print on February 28, 2015, on Page D2 of the New York edition with the headline: Driver’s Racecar Stolen.
