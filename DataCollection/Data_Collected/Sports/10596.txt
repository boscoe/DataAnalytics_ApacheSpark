By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 1, 2015
Andy Murray became the ninth active male player to win 500 matches when he beat Kevin Anderson, 6-4, 3-6, 6-3, in the fourth round of the Miami Open. Murray advanced to the quarterfinals against Dominic Thiem. Tomas Berdych advanced when Gaël Monfils fell and hurt his hip. Novak Djokovic rallied from a set and a break down to beat Alexandr Dolgopolov, 6-7 (3), 7-5, 6-0.In women’s play, Venus Williams’s recent resurgence stalled when she lost in the quarterfinals to No. 12-seeded Carla Suárez Navarro, 0-6, 6-1, 7-5. Andrea Petkovic became the first semifinalist when she beat Karolina Pliskova, 6-4, 6-2.A version of this brief appears in print on April 1, 2015, on Page B13 of the New York edition with the headline: Murray Wins No. 500.
