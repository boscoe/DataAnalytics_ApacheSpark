Anthony Davis left after the first quarter with an illness, only to return in the fourth and finish with 27 points, lifting the New Orleans Pelicans to a 130-125 win over the host Denver Nuggets on Sunday night.Davis had a dominant first quarter, scoring 19 points, but he departed after feeling sick. He returned early in the fourth and gave New Orleans a spark. The Pelicans were trailing, 106-104, when Davis returned to the court with 8 minutes 20 seconds remaining. They went on a 19-10 run to wrap up the win.Will Barton had a career-high 32 points for the Nuggets.HEAT 116, TRAIL BLAZERS 109 Chris Bosh scored 29 points, Hassan Whiteside had 22 points and 11 rebounds, and host Miami survived a loss of composure to top Portland.The Heat tied the game using a 12-2 run to open the third quarter. From there, wildness ensued.
