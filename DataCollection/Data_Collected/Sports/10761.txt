By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 3, 2015
After molding a roster full of McDonald’s all-Americans into a 38-0 juggernaut that is two victories from a national championship, John Calipari was voted the Associated Press coach of the year on Friday. Tony Bennett of Virginia was the runner-up. Wisconsin forward Frank Kaminsky was named the A.P. player of the year, outdistancing Duke’s Jahlil Okafor.■ Prominent black coaches led by Tubby Smith and Shaka Smart are forming an organization to address the dwindling numbers of members of minority groups among head coaches in college basketball. The group, the National Association for Coaching Equity and Development, is a response to the dissolution of the Black Coaches Association, members said.
