By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 13, 2015
In his best opening round in almost 10 years, John Daly played bogey-free for a seven-under-par 65 and was one shot behind J. B. Holmes and Justin Hicks at the Pebble Beach National Pro-Am on Thursday.
