Kawhi Leonard had 16 points and Tim Duncan added 15 as the San Antonio Spurs regrouped after a sluggish start for a 102-75 road win over the Nets on Friday night.Brook Lopez had 17 points to lead the Nets.The Nets charged out to a 41-31 lead fueled by their reserves.
