By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 27, 2015
Justin Rose birdied the final two holes to win the Zurich Classic in Avondale, La., at 22 under par, a tournament record. Rose completed a seven-under 65 in the rain-delayed third round and closed with a 66 at TPC Louisiana for his seventh PGA Tour title.Cameron Tringale shot a 65 and finished second, a stroke behind Rose.■ Lydia Ko won the Swinging Skirts L.P.G.A. Classic in Daly City, Calif., for the second straight year, beating Morgan Pressel on the second hole of a playoff.■ Lee Westwood won the Asian Tour’s Indonesia Masters in Jakarta, Indonesia, for the third time, beating Chapchai Nirat with a birdie on the first hole of a playoff.A version of this brief appears in print on April 27, 2015, on Page D6 of the New York edition with the headline: Rose Takes Zurich Classic.
