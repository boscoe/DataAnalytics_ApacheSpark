By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 26, 2015
An attorney representing the family of the Chicago Cubs great Ernie Banks said the Hall of Famer died after suffering a heart attack.Mark Bogen spoke in Chicago with Banks’s widow, Liz. He said Banks suffered the heart attack on Friday, the day he died.In Chicago, the Cubs and the city announced that a statue of Banks would be temporarily moved from outside Wrigley Field to downtown. The statue will be on display in Daley Plaza from Wednesday through Saturday.■ Reliever Al Alburquerque and the Detroit Tigers agreed on a one-year contract.A version of this brief appears in print on January 26, 2015, on Page D6 of the New York edition with the headline: Heart Attack Killed Banks.
