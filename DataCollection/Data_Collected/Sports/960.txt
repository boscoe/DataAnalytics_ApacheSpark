By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 15, 2015
TORONTO —  Sebastian Giovinco scored his 22nd goal of the season, and Toronto F.C. beat the Red Bulls, 2-1, on Tuesday night to clinch its first playoff appearance.Giovinco sent a charge through the crowd of 18,143 when he deked several Red Bulls defenders before firing a shot into the right side of the net in the 78th minute. Giovinco flew back to Canada hours after playing for Italy in a qualifying match for the 2016 European Championship.It was the fourth straight win for Toronto F.C. (15-13-4), which is headed to the playoffs for the first time since it joined M.L.S. in 2006.Herculez Gomez gave Toronto a 1-0 lead in the 51st minute.Shaun Wright-Phillips scored for the Red Bulls (16-10-6) in the 86th minute. The Red Bulls are five points ahead of Toronto F.C. for first place in the Eastern Conference with two games remaining on their regular-season schedule.A version of this article appears in print on October 15, 2015, on Page B18 of the New York edition with the headline: Toronto Secures First Playoff Spot .
