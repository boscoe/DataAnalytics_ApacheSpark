The Rockets will face No. 7-seeded Dallas in the first round of the playoffs.Harden’s four triple-doubles this season are the most by a Rocket since Hakeem Olajuwon also had four in 1989-90.PELICANS 108, SPURS 103 Anthony Davis had 31 points and 13 rebounds, and the New Orleans earned its first playoff berth since 2011 with a home victory over San Antonio.Davis, who will make his playoff debut against Golden State this weekend, came through in the waning minutes with two clutch jumpers, two blocks and two rebounds to prevent the Spurs from pulling off a 23-point comeback.Tyreke Evans had 19 points and 11 assists for New Orleans, which had to win to make the postseason because Oklahoma City also won at Minnesota. The teams finished with identical records, but the Pelicans held the tiebreaker on head-to-head results.Jrue Holiday added 11 points, capped by game-sealing free throws with 9.5 seconds left.Tony Parker had 23 points for San Antonio, which needed to win to secure the second seed in the Western Conference. BULLS 91, HAWKS 85 Aaron Brooks scored 23 points, and host Chicago rallied from 18 down to overcome Atlanta.The Bulls locked up the third seed in the Eastern Conference and a first-round matchup with the Milwaukee Bucks with their fourth straight win.The Hawks, who won a franchise-record 60 games and earned the East’s top seed, matched a season high with their third straight loss.Brooks carried the Bulls in the second half with Derrick Rose watching from the sideline. Jimmy Butler had 21 points, and Pau Gasol had his 54th double-double (18 points and 13 rebounds).Dennis Schroder came off the bench to score 21 points for Atlanta on 9-of-13 shooting. Paul Millsap scored five after missing five games because of a sprained right shoulder.RAPTORS 92, HORNETS 87 Kyle Lowry had 26 points, and host Toronto finished the regular season with its franchise-record 49th victory, beating Charlotte.Toronto, the winner of the Atlantic Division, will open the playoffs at home on Saturday, facing the Washington Wizards.Tory Daniels scored a season-high 24 points for Charlotte.MAVS 114, TRAIL BLAZERS 98 Tyson Chandler had 22 points and 15 rebounds, and host Dallas subdued Portland while learning that it will play Houston in the first round of the playoffs.The Mavericks have been locked into the seventh seed in the Western Conference for a week, but they had to wait until the final night of the regular season to find out their opponent, which was settled with the Rockets’ win over Utah and San Antonio’s loss to New Orleans.The Blazers have the fourth seed as Northwest Division champions but will open the postseason on the road against the No. 5 seed, either San Antonio or Memphis.LaMarcus Aldridge led Portland with 19 points after missing two games with a left foot sprain.CAVALIERS 113, WIZARDS 108  LeBron James sat out Cleveland’s regular-season finale to rest for the playoffs, and the Cavaliers rallied past visiting Washington in overtime.With James out of uniform, the Cavaliers played their other starters in the first half only.Matthew Dellavedova had 18 points and 12 assists for Cleveland. His two free throws with 9.1 seconds gave the Cavaliers a 111-108 lead, and Shawn Marion sealed the game with a dunk. Martell Webster scored 20 for the Wizards.The Cavaliers, who had the best record in the Central Division, will open the playoffs this weekend against the Celtics.
