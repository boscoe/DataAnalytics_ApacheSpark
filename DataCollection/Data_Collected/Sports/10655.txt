By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 1, 2015
Monta Ellis scored 26 points, and the visiting Dallas Mavericks withstood Russell Westbrook’s eighth triple-double since the All-Star break to beat the Oklahoma City Thunder, 135-131. Westbrook finished with 31 points, 11 rebounds and 11 assists.Anthony Morrow had 32 points, and Enes Kanter added a career-high 30 points and 16 rebounds for the Thunder. According to Stats L.L.C., the last team to have three players score at least 30 points in a game was also Oklahoma City, on March 7, 2012.■ James Harden scored 51 points, surpassing his previous career best of 50 from March 19, and the Houston Rockets held on for a 115-111 home win over the Sacramento Kings, who got 24 points, 21 rebounds, 10 assists and 6 blocks from DeMarcus Cousins.■ Aron Baynes had 18 points, and San Antonio clinched a playoff berth with a 103-91 win at the Orlando Magic.
