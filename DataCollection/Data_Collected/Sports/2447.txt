By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 7, 2015
Kevin Kisner kept up his impressive debut in China with a six-under-par 66 that gave him a two-shot lead over Russell Knox at the HSBC Champions in Shanghai. Kisner never saw the Sheshan International course until the opening round because of a sore back.■ Angela Stanford and Ryann O’Toole shared the lead at seven-under 65 in the L.P.G.A. Tour’s Toto Japan Classic in Shima.Ai Suzuki was a stroke back along with Ha-Neul Kim, Ilhee Lee, Jenny Shin and Mi-Jeong Jeon.■ Roberto Castro shot a five-under 67 to take a four-stroke lead in the waterlogged Sanderson Farms Championship in Jackson, Miss.
