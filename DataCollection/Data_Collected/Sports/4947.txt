By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 18, 2015
LeBron James crashed into the crowd in Cleveland on Thursday night and injured Ellie Day, the wife of the world’s No. 2-ranked golfer Jason Day, while scrambling for a loose ball, according to multiple news media reports. James scored 33 points in the Cavaliers’ 104-100 victory over the Oklahoma City Thunder, but in the fourth quarter he plowed into Ellie Day, who was sitting next to her husband, and she was taken away on a stretcher.
