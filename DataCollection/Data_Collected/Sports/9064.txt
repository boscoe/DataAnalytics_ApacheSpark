By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 7, 2015
Alex Morgan redirected Lauren Holiday’s free kick for a goal nine minutes into the second half, and the United States’ women’s national team went on to defeat Switzerland, 3-0, in group play at the Algarve Cup in Portugal. Amy Rodriguez scored in the 72nd minute before Abby Wambach came off the bench to score.■ UEFA’s top official responsible for Crimea said the governing body of European soccer planned to set up a league in the disputed territory. Russia annexed Crimea from Ukraine in March 2014, but UEFA ruled in December that Crimean clubs could not take part in the Russian league.An Associated Press report in the Sports Briefing column on Saturday about the United States women’s national team’s victory at the Algarve Cup misidentified its opponent. It was Switzerland, not Sweden. The error was repeated in the headline.A version of this brief appears in print on March 7, 2015, on Page D2 of the New York edition with the headline: U.S. Women Beat Sweden.
