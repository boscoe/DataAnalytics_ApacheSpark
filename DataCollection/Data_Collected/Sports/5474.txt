By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 29, 2015
A Dartmouth swimmer who died at a Y.M.C.A. pool in Sarasota, Fla., had been trying to complete four laps underwater without surfacing to breathe, according to a report by the Sarasota County Sheriff’s Office.The swimmer, Tate Ramsden, 21, of Nashville, could not be revived by emergency medical personnel, the report said.
