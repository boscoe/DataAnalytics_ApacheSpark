By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 10, 2015
Texas Tech’s athletic director, Kirby Hocutt, was appointed to the College Football Playoff selection committee. Hocutt will replace Oliver Luck, who moved to a position with the N.C.A.A.■ Eastern Washington quarterback Vernon Adams, a Football Championship Subdivision all-American set to graduate in May, said he would transfer to Oregon, where he would be eligible to play next season and compete to replace Marcus Mariota.■ Oklahoma State notified officials at Texas that it wanted to take sworn statements from the Longhorns’ coach, Charlie Strong, and quarterbacks coach, Shawn Watson, about who is calling their plays. The move stems from Oklahoma State’s breach of contract dispute with Joe Wickline, a former Cowboys assistant now on Strong’s staff at Texas. Oklahoma State is suing Wickline because it says he made a lateral move to Texas and did not take a promotion with “play-calling duties” as specified in his previous contract.A version of this brief appears in print on February 10, 2015, on Page B12 of the New York edition with the headline: New Committee Member.
