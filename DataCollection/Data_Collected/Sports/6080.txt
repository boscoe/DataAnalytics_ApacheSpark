By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 10, 2015
The former Ohio State coach Jim Tressel and Kansas State Coach Bill Snyder were selected for induction to the College Football Hall of Fame. Also selected in the class of 17 were the Heisman Trophy winner Ricky Williams of Texas and Oklahoma linebacker Brian Bosworth.Tressel has two years left on the punishment he got from the N.C.A.A. for withholding information about an investigation of his players at Ohio State. Bosworth was suspended for his last college game after failing a test for performance-enhancing drugs. (AP)■ Alabama wide receiver Amari Cooper, safety Landon Collins and tailback T. J. Yeldon will skip their senior seasons to enter the N.F.L. draft. Cooper and Collins are projected as top-10 picks.
