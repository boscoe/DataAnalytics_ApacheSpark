By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 8, 2015
Li Haotong, 20, of China, was in contention at the HSBC Champions, a World Golf Championships event in Shanghai, after a six-under-par 66 in the third round left him one shot out of the lead. The leaders, Kevin Kisner and Russell Knox, were at 16-under 200.■ Michael Allen held on to the lead in the Champions Tour’s season-ending event, the Charles Schwab Cup Championship in Scottsdale, Ariz. Bernhard Langer was a stroke back and remained in position to take the points title, which comes with a $1 million annuity.■ Jenny Shin shot a seven-under 65 to take a one-stroke lead over Ha-Neul Kim in the L.P.G.A.
