By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 23, 2015
Cory Schneider made 38 saves to record his third shutout of the season as the Devils won, 3-0, at home on Monday and handed the Arizona Coyotes their season-high sixth loss in a row. Scott Gomez scored on a first-period breakaway, and Mike Cammalleri had breakaway and empty-net goals in the third period for the Devils, who have put together their first four-game winning streak since February 2013.■ Matt Grzelcyk scored on the first shot of overtime, and Boston University beat Northeastern, 4-3, to win the Beanpot title. Boston College beat Harvard, 3-2, in overtime in the consolation game at TD Garden in Boston.
