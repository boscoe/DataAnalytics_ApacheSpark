By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 20, 2015
Jack Sock and Marcos Baghdatis advanced to the second round of the Stockholm Open with hard-fought wins, but Jarkko Nieminen ended his singles career with a loss to Nicolas Almagro.The seventh-seeded Sock hit 11 aces to beat Pablo Carreno Busta, 6-3, 6-7 (1), 6-3. Baghdatis, the 2009 champion, was taken to a first-set tiebreaker before beating Sam Querrey, 7-6 (2), 6-1. Almagro beat Nieminen, who was making his 16th straight appearance in Stockholm, 3-6, 7-6 (6), 6-4.■ Gaël Monfils made a winning return from a seven-week injury layoff, rallying to beat Thomaz Bellucci, 6-7 (5), 6-3, 6-4, in the first round of the Erste Bank Open in Vienna.A version of this brief appears in print on October 21, 2015, on Page B16 of the New York edition with the headline: Sock Advances in Sweden.
