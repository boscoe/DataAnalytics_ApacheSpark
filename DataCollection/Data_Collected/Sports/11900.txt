AdvertisementSupported bySports Briefing | TennisBy The Associated PressThe defending champion Kei Nishikori is back in the final of the Barcelona Open in Spain after beating Martin Klizan, 6-1, 6-2. In Sunday’s final, Nishikori, the top seed, will face Pablo Andujar, who ousted the third-seeded David Ferrer, 7-6 (6), 6-3. Nishikori won the first five games of the match and saved all six of Klizan’s break points. Andujar, ranked 66th, hit 32 winners to Ferrer’s 19 and finished off the upset with a drop shot.
