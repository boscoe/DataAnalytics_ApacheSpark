By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 27, 2015
The Islanders placed goaltender Jaroslav Halak on injured reserve because of an upper-body injury. The move was retroactive to last Monday.Halak missed most of the preseason and the Islanders’ first four regular-season games because of the injury. Thomas Greiss started against Toronto on Sunday night.■ Sabres forward Evander Kane is under investigation by the Buffalo police in connection with a sexual offense, the team confirmed in a statement.The Buffalo News, citing unidentified police personnel, said the investigation was related to events Sunday morning in a Buffalo hotel room.
