By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 5, 2015
Thomas Müller scored two goals, and host Bayern Munich dominated from start to finish to beat Arsenal, 5-1, and close in on a place in the knockout stage of the Champions League.Neymar scored twice as host Barcelona eased to a 3-0 win over BATE Borisov and also moved closer to qualifying. Willian produced a late winner for host Chelsea, scoring from a free kick to seal a 2-1 victory over Dynamo Kiev.Miralem Pjanic scored on a late penalty, and host Roma edged Bayer Leverkusen, 3-2, to revive its chances. Zenit St. Petersburg reached the knockout phase with a 2-0 win at Lyon.A version of this brief appears in print on November 5, 2015, on Page B15 of the New York edition with the headline: Müller Leads Bayern Past Arsenal.
