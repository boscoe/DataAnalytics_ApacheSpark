By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 27, 2015
DeAndre Washington ran for 173 yards and two touchdowns as Texas Tech (7-5, 4-5 Big 12) beat the Texas Longhorns, 48-45, for its first road win in the rivalry since 1997.The loss sent Texas (4-7, 3-5) to its second consecutive losing season.■ Quinton Flowers passed for three touchdowns and ran for two more as South Florida routed Central Florida, 44-3, in Orlando.U.S.F. (8-4, 6-2 American Athletic Conference) has a chance to play in the conference championship game if Connecticut beats Temple on Saturday.
