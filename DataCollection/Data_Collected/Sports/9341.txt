By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 11, 2015
The world champion Marit Bjoergen has won a record-tying fifth overall World Cup cross-country sprint title, and Finn Haagen Krogh captured his first. Bjoergen finished third in the sprint season finale in Drammen, Norway. Krogh was second, enough to win the overall sprint title.■ A former Team Canada junior ski coach, Bertrand Charest, was charged in Quebec with sexual assault and breach of trust involving teenagers ages 14 to 16. Charest was arraigned on 47 charges, dating from 1991 to 1998.
