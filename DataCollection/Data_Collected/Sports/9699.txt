By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 18, 2015
Anthony Davis had 20 points and 12 rebounds, and the Pelicans held off the Milwaukee Bucks, 85-84, in New Orleans. When Ersan Ilyasova’s 3-point attempt at the final horn rattled off the inside of the rim twice before bouncing out, the Pelicans moved back into the Western Conference’s final playoff spot.■ Oklahoma City Thunder forward Serge Ibaka could miss the rest of the season after having an arthroscopic procedure Tuesday on his right knee. General Manager Sam Presti said Ibaka would be out four to six weeks. The playoffs begin April 18.Presti said the procedure shaved off damaged cartilage from under Ibaka’s kneecap that had caused pain and swelling.
