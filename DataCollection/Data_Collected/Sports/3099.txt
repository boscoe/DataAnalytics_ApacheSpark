By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 16, 2015
Donald Sterling, the former Los Angeles Clippers owner, lost his appeal to regain the team. The Second District Court of Appeal in California said Sterling’s estranged wife, Rochelle Sterling, had properly removed him from a family trust that owned the team and said the team’s subsequent sale, for $2 billion to Steven A.
