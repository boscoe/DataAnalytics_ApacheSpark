AdvertisementSupported byBy The Associated PressDave Roberts will be the Los Angeles Dodgers’ first minority manager, the team said Monday. The Dodgers will introduce Roberts, 43, on Dec. 1 at a news conference.Roberts’s father is black, and his mother is Japanese. Roberts was born in Okinawa, Japan, where his father served in the United States Marine Corps. Roberts will be the third minority manager in the majors, joining the Washington Nationals’ Dusty Baker and the Atlanta Braves’ Fredi Gonzalez.Roberts was San Diego’s bench coach during the last two seasons.
