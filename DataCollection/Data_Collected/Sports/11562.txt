By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 19, 2015
Florida won a third straight N.C.A.A. women’s gymnastics title, holding off Utah, 197.850-197.800, in the Super Six finals in Fort Worth, Tex. The Utes’ Georgia Dabritz recorded her second perfect 10 in two days on uneven bars, but the Gators, who shared the title with Oklahoma last year in the first tie at the national meet, got two strong closing routines in that event.A version of this brief appears in print on April 19, 2015, on Page SP8 of the New York edition with the headline: Florida Takes Third Straight Title.
