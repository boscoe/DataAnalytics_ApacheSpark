By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 19, 2015
The N.H.L. will play a three-on-three All-Star tournament instead of a traditional All-Star Game. Players will represent the Pacific, Central, Atlantic and Metropolitan Divisions in the $1 million, winner-take-all tournament in Nashville, which will feature three 20-minute games. The Central will open against the Pacific, and the Atlantic will face the Metropolitan.
