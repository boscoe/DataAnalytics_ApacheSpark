By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 20, 2015
The German Grand Prix was cut from the 2015 Formula One calendar after neither of the country’s two circuits was able to make a deal with Bernie Ecclestone, the series promoter. The last time the race was scratched from the calendar was in 1955.■ Brian Vickers has been sidelined again by blood clots that prevent him from safely driving a racecar.A version of this brief appears in print on March 21, 2015, on Page D7 of the New York edition with the headline: A Grand Prix Is Dropped.
