By PAT BORZIAPRIL 7, 2015
Diana Taurasi had surgery to repair a broken bone in her hand, an injury she sustained while playing for her Russian club team. Taurasi, who said in February that she would skip the W.N.B.A. season, will be sidelined for four to six weeks, said her W.N.B.A. team, the Phoenix Mercury. (AP)■ The Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame will have 11 inductees this year, including Lisa Leslie, a former W.N.B.A. star and a four-time Olympic gold medalist; Kentucky Coach John Calipari; Spencer Haywood, whose court case opened the doors for underclassmen to enter the N.B.A. draft; the longtime referee Dick Bavetta; the former Boston coach Tom Heinsohn; and Dikembe Mutombo, a four-time N.B.A. defensive player of the year. The induction ceremony will be in September. (AP)■ The Liberty announced Monday that they had re-signed forward Swin Cash. Cash, a 13-year veteran, averaged 4.5 points and 2.9 rebounds in 15 games with the Liberty after being acquired from the Atlanta Dream last July.
