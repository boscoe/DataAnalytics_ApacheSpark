By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 21, 2015
The Colorado Rockies agreed to a three-year, $10.4 million contract with the right-handed reliever Adam Ottavino.■ The Seattle Mariners hired Amanda Hopkins as an area scout. The team said it believed she was the first woman hired as a full-time scout by a major league club since the 1950s.■ The Pittsburgh Pirates acquired the left-handed pitcher Kyle Lobstein from the Detroit Tigers for cash.■ Wade Boggs’s No. 26 will be retired by the Boston Red Sox during a ceremony at Fenway Park on May 26.
