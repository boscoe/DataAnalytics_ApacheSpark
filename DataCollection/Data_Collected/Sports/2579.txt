By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 9, 2015
Jimmie Johnson overtook Brad Keselowski’s dominating Chase-contending car with three laps to go to win the AAA Texas 500 at Texas Motor Speedway in Fort Worth.Keselowski led a track-record 312 of 334 laps and worked hard to hold the lead after the final restart with 18 laps left in a race he felt he needed to win in order to secure one of the four spots in the Chase for the Nascar Sprint Cup championship finale in two weeks.That leaves three spots up for grabs next Sunday at Phoenix, the last race before the Nov. 22 finale at Homestead-Miami Speedway.Kyle Busch is second behind the retiring four-time champion Jeff Gordon, Johnson’s teammate at Hendrick Motorsports and the only driver locked to race for the title. Kevin Harvick is third and Martin Truex Jr. fourth. (AP)Advertisement■ Jorge Lorenzo won his third MotoGP title with a victory at the season-ending Valencia GP in Spain as Valentino Rossi failed to make up enough ground on Lorenzo, his Yamaha teammate, from the back of the grid.Rossi finished fourth but needed second place to guarantee the title. He started last on the grid because of a penalty for kicking Marc Márquez in the previous race.AdvertisementLorenzo finished with 330 points, five more than Rossi, who was trying to win his eighth world title and the first since 2009.
