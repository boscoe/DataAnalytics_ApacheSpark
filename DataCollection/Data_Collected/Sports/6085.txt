By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 10, 2015
Tim Cahill of the Red Bulls sparked a rally, and Australia beat Kuwait, 4-1, in the opening match of the Asian Cup in Melbourne. Kuwait defender Hussain Fadhel opened the scoring. Cahill scored in the 33rd minute for the Australians.■ The Welsh club Cardiff will revert to its traditional blue home uniform after the owner bowed to pressure from fans.
