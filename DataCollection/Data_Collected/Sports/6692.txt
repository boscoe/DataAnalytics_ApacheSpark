By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 21, 2015
Russell Westbrook scored 19 points and grabbed 10 rebounds, Kevin Durant added 19 points and the Oklahoma City Thunder moved over the .500 mark for the first time this season with a 94-86 win over the host Miami Heat on Tuesday.
