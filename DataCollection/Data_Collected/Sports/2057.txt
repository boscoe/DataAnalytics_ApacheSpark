By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 1, 2015
Ashley Wagner ran away with the Skate Canada women’s title in Lethbridge, Alberta. Skating to music from “Moulin Rouge,” Wagner, a three-time United States champion, scored 202.52 points with an almost flawless program that included seven triple jumps.
