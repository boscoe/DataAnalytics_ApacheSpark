By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 23, 2015
Wayne Selden Jr. and Sviatoslav Mykhaliuk each scored 18 points to lead seven Kansas players in double figures, and the No. 5 Jayhawks cruised past Chaminade, 123-72, in the opening round of the Maui Invitational on Monday night in Lahaina, Hawaii.Devonte Graham added 15 points, Frank Mason III had 14, Landen Lucas had 13, and Hunter Mickelson and Perry Ellis each had 11 for the Jayhawks, who shot 64.4 percent from the field (47 for 73).Kansas (2-1) will play the winner of the Nevada-Las Vegas-U.C.L.A. game in the semifinals Tuesday. The Jayhawks bounced back from a 79-73 loss to then-No. 13 Michigan State last week in the Champions Classic.Chaminade (0-3), a Division II team from Honolulu, dropped to 7-85 in the 32 years of the tournament.
