By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 3, 2015
A man was arrested on suspicion of murder in the disappearance of the Belarussian sprinter Yulia Balykina, 31, who ran in the 100 meters and the 4x100-meter relay at the 2012 Olympics in London and has been missing since last Wednesday.
