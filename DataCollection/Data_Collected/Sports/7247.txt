By REUTERSJAN. 31, 2015
A semiprofessional football player killed in 2013 and Aaron Hernandez, the former New England Patriots tight end, were in the “early stages of friendship” and would hang out and smoke marijuana in Mr. Hernandez’s basement, the dead man’s girlfriend testified.But the witness, Shaneah Jenkins, who is the sister of Mr. Hernandez’s fiancée, said she did not remember her boyfriend, Odin Lloyd, having phone conversations with Mr. Hernandez, who is standing trial in Fall River, Mass., for murder in Mr. Lloyd’s death. Defense lawyers contend that the two men were friends.■ Prime Prep Academy, which the N.F.L. Hall of Famer Deion Sanders helped establish, remains in financial disarray, and its Dallas and Fort Worth campuses will close immediately, the Texas education commissioner, Michael Williams, said.
