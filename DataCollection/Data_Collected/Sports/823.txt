By REUTERSOCT. 12, 2015
Ireland flanker Peter O’Mahony was ruled out of the rest of the Rugby World Cup with a knee injury, the Irish Rugby Football Union said. He was replaced by Leinster’s Rhys Ruddock.O’Mahony was injured in a 24-9 win over France on Sunday, which put Ireland atop Pool D.The captain Paul O’Connell, 35, sustained a hamstring injury and will also probably miss the rest of the tournament, including Sunday’s quarterfinal against Argentina.A version of this brief appears in print on October 13, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Ireland Flanker Is Out of World Cup.
