By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 13, 2015
Red Wings Coach Mike Babcock picked goalie Petr Mrazek, 23, to start over Jimmy Howard, Detroit’s No. 1 goalie since the 2009-10 season, for the team’s playoff opener against Tampa Bay. Mrazek played well while Howard was out with a groin injury, and Howard’s save percentage is down to .896 since he returned.■ The star right wing Patrick Kane, who broke his collarbone Feb. 24, was cleared for contact and attended practice, and Blackhawks Coach Joel Quenneville said Kane might play in Chicago’s playoff opener against the Nashville Predators.■ The Dallas Stars’ Jamie Benn, who led the N.H.L. in points with 35 goals and 52 assists, will have off-season surgery. General Manager Jim Nill said Benn had problems with both hips but should be ready for training camp in September.A version of this brief appears in print on April 14, 2015, on Page B12 of the New York edition with the headline: Detroit Changes Goalies.
