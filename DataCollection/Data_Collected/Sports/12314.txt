By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 2, 2015
The N.B.A. suspended Milwaukee forward Giannis Antetokounmpo for one game without pay for running into Chicago Bulls forward Mike Dunleavy in the Bucks’ season-ending, 120-66 playoff loss on Thursday. He will sit out the first game for which he is able to play next season.A version of this brief appears in print on May 3, 2015, on Page SP4 of the New York edition with the headline: Antetokounmpo Barred for a Game.
