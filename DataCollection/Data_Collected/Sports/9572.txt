By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 15, 2015
Henrik Kristoffersen of Norway won a men’s World Cup slalom in Kranjska Gora, Slovenia, while Marcel Hirscher of Austria recovered from a disappointing first run to place sixth and extend his lead in the standings.■ The American Hannah Kearney closed out her World Cup career in Megéve, France, with her 46th career victory, winning the overall freestyle and moguls titles. Kearney, 29, will retire after the United States championships this month.A version of this brief appears in print on March 16, 2015, on Page D10 of the New York edition with the headline: Austrian Builds Slalom Lead.
