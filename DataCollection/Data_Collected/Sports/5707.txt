By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 3, 2015
Danaejah Grant scored 17 points, Amber Thompson had 12 points and 20 rebounds, and the St. John’s women (12-1) held No. 23 Seton Hall without a field goal for almost the final nine and a half minutes in a 59-50 home win. The loss ended a 12-game winning streak for the Pirates (13-2).■ Jewell Loyd scored 20 points to help No. 4 Notre Dame beat the visiting Florida State women, 74-68. The Irish (13-1) extended their winning streak to 37 conference games.
