By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 19, 2015
Leaders of the World Anti-Doping Agency declared Russia’s antidoping operation out of compliance Wednesday — a harsh, although expected, blow. Members of the WADA foundation board approved a recommendation from an independent commission that detailed widespread rule-breaking in Russia’s track and antidoping programs. Without an operating antidoping agency, Russia cannot host or bid for international events.A version of this brief appears in print on November 19, 2015, on Page B16 of the New York edition with the headline: Russia Is Ruled Out of Compliance.
