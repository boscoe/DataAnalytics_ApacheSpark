By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 10, 2015
With Brook Lopez in foul trouble, Andrea Bargnani picked a perfect time to give the Nets his best game of the season.Bargnani scored a season-high 23 points, Thaddeus Young had 18 points and 11 rebounds, and the Nets beat Philadelphia, 100-91, on Thursday at Barclays Center.The Nets won for the sixth time in their last seven at home. The 76ers fell to 0-13 on the road.“We definitely had to win the game,” Young said. “We’re supposed to beat the teams that we’re supposed to beat. Everybody put us in favor of beating the Sixers.”Jahlil Okafor had 22 points and 10 rebounds, and T. J. McConnell scored 17 points for the 76ers.This was the second game for Okafor since returning from a two-game suspension.Advertisement“I’m trying to play more games and just getting flow back,” Okafor said. “Tonight that was the case. I’m going to try to keep getting better and play every game.”AdvertisementThe Nets allowed 13 points in the first quarter, the fewest they have given up in any period this season.
