By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 7, 2015
James Ward rallied from two sets down to beat John Isner in a Davis Cup match that lasted almost five hours in Glasgow, giving Britain a 2-0 lead over the United States. The 111th-ranked Ward produced a big upset, clinching a 6-7 (4), 5-7, 6-3, 7-6 (3), 15-13 victory on his sixth match point against the 20th-ranked Isner. Earlier, Andy Murray beat Donald Young, 6-1, 6-1, 4-6, 6-2.Elsewhere, Gaël Monfils and Gilles Simon won their opening matches to give France a 2-0 lead over Germany in Frankfurt.
