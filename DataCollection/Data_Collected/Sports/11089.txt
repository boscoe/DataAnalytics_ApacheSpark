By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 9, 2015
Arizona State hired Bobby Hurley as its men’s basketball coach. Hurley, 43, spent the past two years as the coach at Buffalo, where he led the Bulls to 23 wins and an N.C.A.A. tournament berth this past season. He replaced Herb Sendek, who was fired on March 24.
