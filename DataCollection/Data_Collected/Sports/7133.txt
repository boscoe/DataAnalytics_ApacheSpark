By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 29, 2015
Serena Williams, Venus Williams, Madison Keys and Varvara Lepchenko have been chosen for the United States team that is seeking to return to the top group in the Fed Cup. The Americans must beat Argentina in Buenos Aires on Feb. 7-8 and then win another series in April to return to the top group next year.A version of this brief appears in print on January 29, 2015, on Page B13 of the New York edition with the headline: U.S. Announces Fed Cup Selections.
