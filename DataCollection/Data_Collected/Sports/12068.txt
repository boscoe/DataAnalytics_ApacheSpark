By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 29, 2015
Lionel Messi and Luis Suárez both scored twice as Barcelona thrashed Getafe, 6-0, to stay on course to reclaim the Spanish league title.■ Bayern Munich’s bid for a three-trophy season ended when it lost on penalties, 2-0, to Borussia Dortmund in the German Cup semifinals.
