By REUTERSJAN. 7, 2015
A court invader who shoved a visiting player at a Eurocup basketball game in Montenegro was sent sprawling to the deck by a punch from the player’s teammate. Video footage of the game, between host Buducnost Podgorica and the Turkish team Banvit Bandirma, showed a fan dashing onto the court and pushing Banvit’s Sammy Mejia in the back. That prompted the American guard Earl Rowland, who played in college at St. Mary’s, to run over and throw a punch that sent the fan crashing to the floor.A version of this brief appears in print on January 8, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Eurocup Player From U.S. Hits Fan Who Rushed Court.
