By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 31, 2015
Chelsea’s slump continued Saturday with a 3-1 home loss to Liverpool while Arsenal and Manchester City kept up the pace at the top of the Premier League.Chelsea took the lead after only four minutes, before two goals by Philippe Coutinho and one from Christian Benteke gave Jürgen Klopp his first league win as Liverpool’s manager — and gave Chelsea Coach José Mourinho his sixth league loss of the season.Arsenal beat visiting Swansea, 3-0. City needed a late penalty kick by Yaya Touré to beat Norwich, 2-1, at home.
