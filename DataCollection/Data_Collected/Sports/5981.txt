By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 8, 2015
The six-time Olympic champion Marit Bjorgen of Norway won her fourth straight stage in the Tour de Ski in Dobbiaco, Italy, extending her overall lead to more than a minute and a half. On the men’s side, Alexey Poltoranin of Kazakhstan won the stage while the overall leader, Petter Northug Jr., had his lead over a fellow Norwegian, Martin Sundby, reduced to one and a half seconds.A version of this brief appears in print on January 8, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Bjorgen Continues Domination of Event in Italy.
