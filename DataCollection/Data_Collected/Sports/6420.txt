By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 16, 2015
Fernando Torres scored twice as visiting Atlético Madrid ended Real Madrid’s defense of the Copa del Rey title with a 2-2 draw on Thursday. Atlético advanced on aggregate by 4-2 and will play Barcelona in the quarterfinals.■ Ali Mabkhout scored for the United Arab Emirates after just 14 seconds to set up a 2-1 win over Bahrain in Sydney, Australia, that secured a spot in the Asian Cup knockout stage. Iran also earned a place in the quarterfinals, defeating Qatar, 1-0, in Canberra.■ Cyle Larin, a Canadian forward who played at Connecticut, was selected by the expansion team Orlando City in Major League Soccer’s draft. New York City F.C. took Oregon State forward Khiry Shelton with the second pick. (AP)■ M.L.S. renamed its Most Valuable Player award after Landon Donovan, the league’s career scoring leader.
