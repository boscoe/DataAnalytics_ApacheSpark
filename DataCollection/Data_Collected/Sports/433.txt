By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 7, 2015
The Louisville campus police said they were reviewing allegations that a former men’s basketball staff member hired escorts to dance and have sex with recruits and players in a dormitory.Katina Powell wrote in a book released online over the weekend that Andre McGee, the former Cardinals director of basketball operations, paid her $10,000 for dancers to perform 22 shows from 2010 to 2014 at the players’ Billy Minardi Hall dormitory.
