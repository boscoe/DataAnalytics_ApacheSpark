Royal St. George’s, one of the three clubs used for the British Open with a male-only membership policy, voted to allow female members for the first time.The club said 81 percent of the club’s full members voted, and 90 percent were in favor of women being eligible for membership.A version of this brief appears in print on March 5, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Royal St. George’s to Allow Women.
