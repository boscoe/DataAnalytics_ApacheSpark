By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 8, 2015
Second-seeded Ekaterina Makarova withdrew from the Family Circle Cup in Charleston, S.C., with a stomach illness only hours after winning her opening match. Makarova felt stomach pain during her 5-7, 6-3, 6-2 win over Zhang Shuai of China. Makarova’s departure means 13th-seeded Irina-Camelia Begu will advance to Friday’s quarterfinals.A version of this brief appears in print on April 9, 2015, on Page B18 of the New York edition with the headline: Second Seed Withdraws.
