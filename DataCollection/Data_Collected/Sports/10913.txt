City is 9 points behind the league leader, Chelsea, which has a game in hand. Glen Murray and Jason Puncheon gave Palace a 2-0 lead by the 48th minute even though City controlled 74 percent of possession. ■ In response to an attack on the bus of the Turkish club Fenerbahce on Saturday, the Turkish soccer authorities suspended Super League and Turkish Cup matches for a week, and Fenerbahce said it had no intention of playing its games until those responsible were caught. The bus was fired at near the northern city Trabzon, and the driver was hit in the face and hospitalized. He is in a stable condition.The Trabzon chief prosecutor’s office said Monday that it had begun an attempted-murder investigation and that one man had been detained for questioning for making threats against Fenerbahce over the Internet.
