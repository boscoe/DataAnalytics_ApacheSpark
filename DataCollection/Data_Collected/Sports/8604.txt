By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 26, 2015
The Detroit Lions released running back Reggie  Bush halfway through the four-year deal he signed as a free agent before the 2013 season.  Bush, 29, started 23 games for the Lions. He rushed for 1,006 yards in 2013, but this past season he had only 76 carries for 297 yards while slowed by injuries. Bush was the No. 2 overall pick in the 2006 N.F.L. draft.■ Jacoby Jones was cut by the Baltimore Ravens. A wide receiver and kick returner, he was a major contributor to the Ravens’ Super Bowl championship in 2013.
