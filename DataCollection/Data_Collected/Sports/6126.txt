By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 11, 2015
Aqueduct canceled the Saturday program, the fourth straight card affected by the cold snap in the New York area.The Wednesday and Thursday cards were called off. The track ran only two races on Friday before halting the remainder. Aqueduct and Belmont Park remained open for simulcast wagering on out-of-state races.
