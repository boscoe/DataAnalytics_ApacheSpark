By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 9, 2015
Peter Malnati shot a five-under-par 67 to win a rain-soaked Sanderson Farms Championship in Jackson, Miss., beating William McGirt and David Toms by one shot for his first PGA Tour win.
