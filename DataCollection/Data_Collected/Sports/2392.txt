By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 6, 2015
A final appeals officer rejected an appeal by Matt Kenseth after he challenged his two-race suspension by Nascar for intentionally crashing into Joey Logano’s car Sunday at Martinsville Speedway in Ridgeway, Va.Kenseth will miss the next two races, at Texas and Phoenix, and will be eligible to return for the Nov. 22 season finale.A version of this brief appears in print on November 6, 2015, on Page B13 of the New York edition with the headline: Kenseth’s Final Appeal Is Rejected.
