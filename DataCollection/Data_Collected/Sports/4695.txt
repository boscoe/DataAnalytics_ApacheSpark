By AGENCE FRANCE-PRESSEDEC. 14, 2015
The Japanese horse A Shin Hikari swept to victory and a prize of about $1.84 million at the Hong Kong International Races, one of the world’s richest events. The veteran jockey Yutaka Take led from the start and finished the 2,000 meters, about a mile and a quarter, in about 2 minutes 1 second.A version of this brief appears in print on December 14, 2015, on Page D8 of the New York edition with the headline: A $1.84 Million Victory in Hong Kong.
