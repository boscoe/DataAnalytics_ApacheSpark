By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 9, 2015
Big 12 football teams will now be required to play at least one nonconference game each season against Notre Dame or a team from one of the four other major football conferences. Commissioner Bob Bowlsby of the Big 12 cited a desire to improve teams’ strength of schedule.■ Top University of Minnesota officials did not know about the conduct of Athletic Director Norwood Teague before he abruptly resigned in August for sexual harassment, according to an external review that also found no fault with the “general climate” around the issue within the sports department. (AP)■ Six people were arraigned in the kidnapping of two Rochester seniors who investigators said were repeatedly beaten until a SWAT team rescued them. The seniors, Nicholas Kollias and Ani Okeke Ewo, were reported missing Saturday. According to a 2015 roster on Rochester’s website, Ewo is a cornerback for the football team. Kollias was listed as a defensive end on the 2014 roster.
