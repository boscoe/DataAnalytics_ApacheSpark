By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 8, 2015
Tyson Fury was the subject of a police investigation amid a BBC report that he had been stripped of his International Boxing Federation heavyweight title. The Greater Manchester Police in England said they had received a report of a hate crime regarding Fury’s comments about homosexuality in a newspaper interview.A version of this brief appears in print on December 9, 2015, on Page B13 of the New York edition with the headline: Champion in Police Investigation.
