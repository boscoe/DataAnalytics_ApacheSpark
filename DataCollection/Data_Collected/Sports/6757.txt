LeBron James scored 26 points, Kevin Love added 19, and the rejuvenated Cavaliers won their fourth straight, defeating the Utah Jazz, 106-92, on Wednesday night in Cleveland.Shaking off an illness that forced him to miss practice Tuesday, James had nine assists and seven rebounds. The Cavaliers have a 4-1 mark since James, who missed eight games with a strained back and knee, returned.J. R. Smith had 15 points as all five Cavaliers starters scored at least 15 for the second straight game.Enes Kanter scored 24 points and had 17 rebounds for Utah, which has dropped five of six games over all and four consecutive road games.
