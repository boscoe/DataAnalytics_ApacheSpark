By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 16, 2015
Webb Simpson and Matt Kuchar made enough birdies on the back nine to build a little separation and share the lead at the Sony Open in Honolulu.Kuchar had no bogeys in the second round, shooting a seven-under-par 63. On the par-5 18th, Simpson reached the green in two shots and two-putted for birdie with his conventional putter. That gave him a 66 and a share of the lead at 12-under 128.■ Rory McIlroy made his first competitive hole in one as a professional, acing the par-3 15th from 177 yards in the second round of the Abu Dhabi Championship. McIlroy rose to third place, two shots behind Martin Kaymer.
