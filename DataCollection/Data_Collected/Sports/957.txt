By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 15, 2015
CBS Sports filled a big void on its golf broadcasting team by hiring Dottie Pepper, giving the network, which covers the most PGA Tour events, its first female voice. She replaces David Feherty, who left CBS to join NBC Sports.Pepper was the most prominent female golf analyst at NBC Sports from 2004 until 2012.
