By REUTERSMARCH 27, 2015
Jimmy Walker took advantage of greatly improved weather conditions at his home tournament to seize a one-shot lead over Aaron Baddeley and Charley Hoffman in the second round of the Texas Open. Walker fired a five-under-par 67 on a challenging layout at T.P.C. San Antonio with three consecutive birdies in his last four holes.■ Morgan Pressel matched the course record with an eight-under 64 for a share of the second-round lead with Mirim Lee in the Kia Classic in Carlsbad, Calif.
