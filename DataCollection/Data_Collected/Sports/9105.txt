By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 8, 2015
Keith Thurman won a piece of the welterweight title with a bruising decision win in Las Vegas over Robert Guerrero in the debut of a prime-time boxing series on NBC. Thurman knocked Guerrero down in the ninth round and won easily on all three ringside scorecards to capture the World Boxing Association version of the 147-pound crown.The fight was the highlight of a card that also included a win by Adrien Broner over John Molina in a superlightweight bout.A version of this brief appears in print on March 8, 2015, on Page SP9 of the New York edition with the headline: Thurman Earns a Crown.
