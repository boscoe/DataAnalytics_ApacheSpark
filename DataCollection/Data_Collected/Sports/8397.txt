RoundupBy The Associated PressEvgeny Kuznetsov scored the lone goal in a shootout, Braden Holtby had 30 saves, and the Washington Capitals beat the visiting Islanders, 3-2, on Saturday for their fourth straight victory.After the Islanders forced overtime by scoring with goaltender Chad Johnson on the bench, Kuznetsov beat Johnson on the first attempt of the shootout. Holtby subsequently denied all three attempts by the Islanders.
