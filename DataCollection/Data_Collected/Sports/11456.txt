By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 16, 2015
Churchill Downs, which is set to host the 141st running of the Kentucky Derby on May 2 in Louisville, has banned selfie sticks, the pole-like contraptions that allow people a longer reach to snap their self-portraits. The racetrack joins a growing list of museums, music festivals and sports venues around the world that are doing so.
