By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 10, 2015
The N.H.L. picked Peter Laviolette of the Nashville Predators and Darryl Sutter of the Los Angeles Kings as the head coaches for the All-Star Game in Columbus on Jan. 25. Laviolette was chosen because the Predators have the N.H.L.’s highest points percentage at .725.
