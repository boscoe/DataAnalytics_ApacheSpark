By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 28, 2015
The minor league home run leader Mike Hessman said he was retiring as a player and would pursue a career as a coach. Hessman became the career home run leader in the minor leagues in August when he hit a grand slam to pass Buzz Arlett with his 433rd homer.
