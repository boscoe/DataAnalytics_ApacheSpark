By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 5, 2015
Ondrej Moravec of the Czech Republic helped his team win the mixed relay, the first title at the biathlon world championships in Kontiolahti, Finland. On the fourth and last leg, Moravec shot perfectly to give himself a lead over Martin Fourcade of France.■ Martins Dukurs of Latvia took a lead of 0.17 of a second over the Olympic gold medalist, Alexander Tretiakov of Russia, at the halfway stage of the skeleton world championship in Winterberg, Germany.
