By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 1, 2015
Top-seeded Andy Murray began his clay-court season with a 6-2, 6-2 victory over the local qualifier Mischa Zverev to reach the BMW Open quarterfinals in Munich. Murray saved all of the six break points he faced, including three in the final game while serving for the match.■ Second-seeded Grigor Dimitrov eased into the quarterfinals of the inaugural Istanbul Open by beating Andrey Golubev, 6-3, 6-2. Dimitrov will next play Ivan Dodig, who won his match against Dusan Lajovic, 5-7, 7-6 (1), 6-4.■ Second-seeded Timea Bacsinszky and No. 3 Flavia Pennetta lost in three-setters at the Grand Prix SAR quarterfinals in Marrakesh, Morocco. Bacsinszky was downed by Anna Karolina Schmiedlova, 6-3, 3-6, 7-5, and Pennetta fell to Timea Babos, 6-2, 5-7, 7-5.■ The ATP said that the world No. 1 Novak Djokovic had withdrawn from next week’s Madrid Open without elaborating and that he would return to competition the next week in Rome, where he is the defending champion.A version of this brief appears in print on May 1, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Sports Briefing | Tennis; Murray Wins on Clay.
