By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 29, 2015
Javier Fernandez cleanly landed two quad jumps in his free skate at the figure skating world championships in Shanghai on Saturday to edge the defending champion Yuzuru Hanyu and become the first Spanish men’s champion.In the women’s event, Elizaveta Tuktamysheva of Russia wrapped up the most successful season of her young career by winning the women’s gold, beating Satoko Miyahara of Japan by more than 15 points. It was Russia’s first win in the women’s event at worlds since Irina Slutskaya took the title 10 years ago.Two Americans finished in fourth: Gracie Gold on the women’s side, and Jason Brown with the men.A version of this brief appears in print on March 29, 2015, on Page SP8 of the New York edition with the headline: Spaniard Is World Champion.
