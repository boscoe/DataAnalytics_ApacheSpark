By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 2, 2015
Frank Beamer has decided this will be his final season as Virginia Tech’s coach, ending a 29-year run in Blacksburg, Va., that made Beamer Ball synonymous with winning.Beamer, 69, leads active major college football coaches with 277 victories. He is 235-120-2 since taking over at Virginia Tech in 1987.■ The Atlantic Coast Conference suspended the officiating crew that worked the Miami-Duke game for two league games after what it called “a series of errors” on the final play. The A.C.C. announced the decision after the Hurricanes had a combined eight laterals before Corn Elder took the final one for a score that beat the No. 22 Blue Devils, 30-27.The league said that the replay official was wrong in not declaring Miami’s Mark Walton down before he flipped the ball, and that the crew should have flagged Miami players for an illegal block in the back.
