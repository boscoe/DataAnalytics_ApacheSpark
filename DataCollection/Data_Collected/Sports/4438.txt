By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 10, 2015
Loui Eriksson and Landon Ferraro scored third-period goals 42 seconds apart, and Patrice Bergeron added a milestone score to help the Boston Bruins rally past the Canadiens, 3-1, in Montreal. With a third-period goal, Bergeron pushed past Milt Schmidt for 11th on the Bruins’ career list with 576 points.
