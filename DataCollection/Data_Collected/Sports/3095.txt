By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 17, 2015
Mario Chalmers, whom the Grizzlies acquired in a trade with the Miami Heat last week, scored 16 of his season-high 29 points in the fourth quarter, and Memphis held on for a 122-114 home victory over Oklahoma City.■ Chicago’s Jimmy Butler scored 17 points and played tough defense on Paul George on Indiana’s final possession, helping the Bulls hold on for a 96-95 home win. Derrick Rose had 23 points for Chicago but left with about six minutes remaining after he sprained his left ankle.■ Isaiah Thomas scored 23 points, and the Boston Celtics cruised to a 111-95 win at Houston. The Rockets have lost four straight for the first time since January 2013.A version of this brief appears in print on November 17, 2015, on Page B11 of the New York edition with the headline: Chalmers Leads Grizzlies.
