By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 8, 2015
The Notre Dame junior Jewell Loyd declared herself eligible for the W.N.B.A. draft, set for April 16. Loyd, who could be the top pick in the draft, is eligible under W.N.B.A. rules to turn pro early because she will turn 22 during the calendar year of the draft.■ Utah Coach Larry Krystkowiak received a contract extension through the 2022-23 season that will pay him $2.4 million per year.■ Kentucky’s John Calipari became the first two-time recipient of the Adolph Rupp Coach of the Year Award from the Commonwealth Athletic Club of Kentucky.A version of this brief appears in print on April 9, 2015, on Page B18 of the New York edition with the headline: Junior Declares for Draft.
