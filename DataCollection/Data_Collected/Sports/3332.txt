By REUTERSNOV. 21, 2015
Dinamo Zagreb midfielder Arijan Ademi has been barred for four years because he failed a drug test after a Champions League match against Arsenal in September. Ademi failed the test after Dinamo’s 2-1 home win over Arsenal in their Group F opener on Sept. 16. Dinamo Coach Zoran Mamic said the club was shocked and intended to appeal, and Ademi said he was innocent.■ One week after the terror attacks in Paris, the French soccer league resumed as Nice beat Lyon, 3-0, in a somber atmosphere. Fans held candles aloft during a minute’s silence before the match.
