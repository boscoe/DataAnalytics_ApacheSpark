By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 12, 2015
Dusan Tadic, a second-half substitute, scored the lone goal in Southampton’s first win at Manchester United in 27 years. The loss ended United’s 10-game unbeaten run in the Premier League, and Southampton dislodged United from third place. Elsewhere, Alexis Sanchez scored twice for Arsenal in a 3-0 home victory over Stoke.■ Lionel Messi did everything he could to put an end to Barcelona’s most serious club crisis in years, scoring one goal and setting up two more in a 3-1 home win over Atlético Madrid. Messi denied news reports that he had asked that Coach Luis Enrique be fired and that his father, who acts as his agent, had spoken to Chelsea and Manchester City about a possible transfer.
