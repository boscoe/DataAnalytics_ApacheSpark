By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 13, 2015
Wesley Saunders hit a go-ahead shot with 2.9 seconds left to lift Harvard over Columbia, 72-68, in Cambridge, Mass. Saunders finished with 18 points for Harvard (17-5, 6-1 Ivy League). The Crimson led by as many as 19 points in the first half over Columbia (11-11, 3-4).■ Makai Mason scored 14 points, and Yale led wire-to-wire in a 75-48 rout of host Pennsylvania to maintain a share of first place in the Ivy League. Yale (17-7, 6-1) raced to a 38-25 lead at the break. Tony Hicks had 12 points to lead Penn (7-13, 2-4).A version of this brief appears in print on February 14, 2015, on Page D6 of the New York edition with the headline: Harvard Tops Columbia.
