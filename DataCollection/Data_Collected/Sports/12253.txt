By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 2, 2015
Top-seeded Roger Federer overcame sloppy serving to beat unseeded Daniel Gimeno-Traver, 7-6 (3), 6-7 (5), 6-3, and reach the Istanbul Open semifinals. Second-seeded Grigor Dimitrov advanced with a 6-4, 6-3 win against Ivan Dodig.■ Fifth-seeded Richard Gasquet beat Nicolás Almagro, 6-1, 7-6 (4), to advance to the semifinals of the Estoril Open in Portugal. Gasquet, who had lost his last three meetings with Almagro, saved four of five break points while breaking Almagro three times.A version of this brief appears in print on May 2, 2015, on Page D4 of the New York edition with the headline: Federer Advances.
