By BENJAMIN HOFFMANNOV. 29, 2015
Cardinals (8-2) at 49ers (3-7)4:05 p.m. Line: Cardinals by 11.5The last game between these teams was one of the most lopsided in their long history, with Arizona taking advantage of Colin Kaepernick’s sloppy passing to crush their division rival, 47-7. Tyrann Mathieu, one of the defensive backs most responsible for the victory, has gone out of his way to praise Kaepernick’s replacement, Blaine Gabbert, but there is no facet of the game in which the Cardinals do not shine brighter than the 49ers. The lopsided score in Week 3 was very likely an accurate reflection in the talent discrepancy between the teams.Pick: CardinalsA headline with an earlier version of this article misstated the home of the Cardinals.
