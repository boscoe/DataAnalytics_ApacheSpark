By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 13, 2015
The former N.B.A. star Lamar Odom was hospitalized after he was found unconscious at a brothel in Nevada, Sheriff Sharon A. Wehrly of Nye County said Tuesday. She said the department got a call that afternoon requesting an ambulance at the Love Ranch in Crystal, about 70 miles outside Las Vegas.
