By REUTERS and THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 16, 2015
Paul Casey and Webb Simpson shot matching 62s for a share of the lead at eight under par in the first round of the Sony Open in Honolulu.■ Martin Kaymer made 10 birdies on the way to an eight-under-par 64 in the first round of the Abu Dhabi Championship, an event he has won three times. Thomas Pieters was one stroke back. Top-ranked Rory McIlroy shot a 67, including a superb improvised wedge shot from the lip of a fairway bunker on his 12th hole.
