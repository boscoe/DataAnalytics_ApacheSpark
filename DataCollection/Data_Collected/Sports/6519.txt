Indianapolis running back Trent Richardson did not travel to New England for the A.F.C. championship game, set for Sunday. The Colts said only that the decision was made because of personal reasons.■ Candlestick Park in San Francisco will be torn down instead of blown up following concerns that the planned implosion of the stadium might create health problems for people living nearby.The San Francisco Chronicle reported that the development company planning to build houses, a hotel and a shopping center at the site agreed last week to scrap its idea of using explosives to quickly collapse Candlestick and possibly broadcasting the destruction during the Super Bowl.
