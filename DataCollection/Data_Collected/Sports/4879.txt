In September, a three-judge panel of the court overturned a lower court’s ruling from last year that would have barred the N.C.A.A. from restricting colleges from offering top football and men’s basketball players up to $5,000 a year in deferred compensation for the commercial use of their names, images and likenesses. MARC TRACY■ Kirk Ferentz of Iowa won the Eddie Robinson Award, which is given to the top coach in the Football Bowl Subdivision by the Football Writers Association of America. Ferentz is the first Iowa coach to win the award in its 59-year history.Ferentz took a team that went 7-6 in 2014 and led it to the first 12-0 regular season in program history. Iowa lost to Michigan State in the Big Ten championship game. The Hawkeyes close out their season by playing Stanford (11-2) in the Rose Bowl on Jan. 1.
