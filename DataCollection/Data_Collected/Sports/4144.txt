By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 5, 2015
Robert Carter Jr. had 20 points, and No. 2 Maryland routed visiting St. Francis (Pa.), 96-55. Maryland (7-1) was 31 of 46 from the field (67.4 percent). Ben Millaud-Meunier led St. Francis (3-4) with 13 points.■ In a women’s game, the freshman Kalani Brown scored 16 points to lead five Baylor players in double figures, and the No. 4 Lady Bears beat visiting Northwestern State, 86-44.A version of this brief appears in print on December 5, 2015, on Page D5 of the New York edition with the headline: Maryland Wins in Romp.
