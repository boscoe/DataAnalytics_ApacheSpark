The best-selling sports books in November 2015, according to the New York Times best-seller list:1. THE BOYS IN THE BOAT by Daniel James Brown (Penguin). A group of American rowers pursues gold at the 1936 Berlin Olympic Games.2. ABOVE THE LINE by Urban Meyer, with Wayne Coffey (Penguin). Meyer, a championship-winning college football coach, shares his insights on team building and leadership.3. 50 YEARS, 50 MOMENTS by Jerry Rice and Randy O. Williams (Dey Street). The 50th anniversary of the Super Bowl is commemorated in a scrapbook of photographs, personalities, touchdowns, statistics and winning team strategies.4. MY FIGHT/YOUR FIGHT by Ronda Rousey, with Maria Burns Ortiz (Regan Arts). Rousey, an Olympic medalist in judo and a popular Ultimate Fighting Championship bantamweight fighter, tells her story.Advertisement5. THE ART OF PEACE by Morihei Ueshiba; translated and edited by John Stevens (Shambhala). Ueshiba’s teachings form the underpinning of aikido, a popular Japanese martial art and mind-body sport.Advertisement6. SUPER BOWL GOLD by the editors of Sports Illustrated (Time Inc. Books).
