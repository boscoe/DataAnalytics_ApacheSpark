By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 13, 2015
Drew Edwards scored a career-high 17 points, including five 3-pointers, and Ben Bentil came off the bench to get 16 points and 8 rebounds as No. 15 Providence (10-1) held on for a 74-67 victory at home over Bryant (2-9).■ Omar Calhoun led five Connecticut players in double figures with 14 points and the Huskies (6-3) won at home, 75-55, against Ohio State (4-5).■ In a women’s game, Kathryn Westbeld had 12 points and 14 rebounds as No. 3 Notre Dame (9-1) beat Texas Christian (7-3), 88-72, in Fort Worth.A version of this brief appears in print on December 13, 2015, on Page SP10 of the New York edition with the headline: Providence Holds On.
