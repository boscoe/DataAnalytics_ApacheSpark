By REUTERSMARCH 10, 2015
Danny Welbeck returned to haunt Manchester United by scoring the winning goal as visiting Arsenal reached the F.A. Cup semifinals with a 2-1 victory. Welbeck, who joined Arsenal last summer after his United career stalled, pounced on a horrendous back pass by Antonio Valencia in the 61st minute to help his team advance. United ended the match with 10 men after Ángel Di María was shown a red card for grabbing the referee’s shirt just seconds after being penalized for diving.■ Hope Solo had her second straight shutout, and the United States tied Iceland, 0-0, in Lagos, Portugal, to advance to the Algarve Cup final against France. The United States is seeking its 10th title in 22 Algarve Cups. France beat Japan, 3-1, earlier in the day and will play in the final on Wednesday for the first time in six Algarve Cup appearances.
