By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 7, 2015
Bob and Mike Bryan kept the United States alive in its Davis Cup first-round tie against Britain, holding off a comeback by Jamie Murray and Dominic Inglot to win Saturday’s doubles in five sets in Glasgow, Scotland. The 6-3, 6-2, 3-6, 6-7 (8), 9-7 victory by the Bryans reduced the Americans’ deficit to 2-1, and took the World Group match to a third and final day.
