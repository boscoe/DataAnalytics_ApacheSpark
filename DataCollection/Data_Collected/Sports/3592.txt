By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 25, 2015
Jean-Gabriel Pageau scored two third-period goals and the Ottawa Senators held off the host Dallas Stars, 7-4. Ottawa, fourth in the Eastern Conference at 11-5-5, was outshot, 37-20, but scored three unassisted goals after takeaways. The N.H.L. leader in goals scored, Jamie Benn, collected his 15th and 16th goals for Dallas, the top team in the Western Conference at 11-5-5.■ The Rangers are the most valuable N.H.L. franchise, at $1.2 billion, taking the top spot on the Forbes list for the first time since 2004.
