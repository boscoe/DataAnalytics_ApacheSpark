By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 24, 2015
Matt Kuchar stumbled late in the third round of the Humana Challenge in La Quinta, Calif., on Saturday, handing the lead to Erik Compton, Bill Haas, Justin Thomas and Michael Putnam.Kuchar finished a stroke behind the leaders, who were at 17-under-par 199 over all.■ Branden Grace made an eagle on the 16th hole to win the Qatar Masters in Doha by a stroke and clinch his sixth European Tour title. Grace, a South African, shot a final-round six-under-par 66 for an overall 19-under 269.
