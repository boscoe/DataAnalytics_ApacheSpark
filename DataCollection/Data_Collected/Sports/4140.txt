By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 5, 2015
Virginia hired Bronco Mendenhall away from Brigham Young to be the Cavaliers’ new head coach. Mendenhall was in his 11th season with the Cougars and went 99-42, taking them to a bowl game every season.■ East Carolina fired Coach Ruffin McNeill after six seasons.
