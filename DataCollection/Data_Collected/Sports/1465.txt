By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 23, 2015
P. J. Walker threw a late 23-yard touchdown pass to Robby Anderson, and No. 22 Temple rallied to beat host East Carolina, 24-14. Walker completed 19 of 35 passes for 250 yards for the Owls (7-0, 4-0 American Athletic Conference).
