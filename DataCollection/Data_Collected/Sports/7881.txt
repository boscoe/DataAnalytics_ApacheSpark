By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 11, 2015
The Winnipeg Jets sent the embattled left wing Evander Kane to the Buffalo Sabres in a seven-player trade, loading up in hopes of making their first playoff run since the franchise moved from Atlanta in 2011. Kane is expected to be ready for next year’s training camp after having season-ending surgery on his left shoulder.A version of this brief appears in print on February 12, 2015, on Page B14 of the New York edition with the headline: Jets’ Kane Traded to Sabres.
