By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 27, 2015
Host Australia defeated the United Arab Emirates, 2-0, in a semifinal match at the Asian Cup on Tuesday. Australia took a firm grip on the contest when defenders Trent Sainsbury and Jason Davidson scored within the first 15 minutes. Australia will meet South Korea in the final on Sunday in Sydney.■ Ghana came back from the brink of elimination with two goals in the final 17 minutes to beat South Africa, 2-1, and reach the Africa Cup of Nations quarterfinals in Equatorial Guinea. Algeria also reached the quarterfinals, with a 2-0 win over Senegal.■ Branislav Ivanovic headed in a goal in extra time to advance Chelsea to the League Cup final with a 1-0 home victory over Liverpool.
