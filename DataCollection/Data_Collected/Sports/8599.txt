Rondo was benched with 8 minutes 10 seconds remaining in the third quarter of Dallas’ 99-92 win over Toronto on Tuesday. He was bringing the ball up the court when Carlisle angrily shouted for a timeout and exchanged words with Rondo from several feet away as the player headed to the bench. ■ The W.N.B.A. All-Star Game and the league’s draft will be held this year at the Mohegan Sun Arena in Uncasville, Conn.
