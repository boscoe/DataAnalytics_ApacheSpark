By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 5, 2015
Kevin Harvick dominated a race he had to win to advance to the second round of Nascar’s playoffs, while the six-time champion Jimmie Johnson was eliminated from the field.Harvick, who had been mired in 15th in the standings, led for 354 laps at Dover International Speedway in Delaware and earned the third automatic berth in the 12-driver Chase for the Sprint Cup.A version of this brief appears in print on October 5, 2015, on Page D8 of the New York edition with the headline: Harvick Wins to Advance.
