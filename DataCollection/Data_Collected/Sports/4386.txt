By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 8, 2015
Cristiano Ronaldo scored four goals in Real Madrid’s 8-0 Group A victory over visiting Malmo on Tuesday, becoming the first player with at least 10 goals in a group stage of the Champions League.
