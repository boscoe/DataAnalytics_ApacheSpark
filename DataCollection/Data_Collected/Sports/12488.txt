By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 6, 2015
Gov. Mark Dayton of Minnesota helped announce a bid for the 2020 College Football Playoff National Championship for the still-incomplete stadium going up in downtown Minneapolis. A decision on the game’s site is expected in October.
