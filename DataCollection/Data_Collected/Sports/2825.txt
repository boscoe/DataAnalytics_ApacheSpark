By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 13, 2015
Matt Williams is returning to his old job as third-base coach of the Arizona Diamondbacks. The team announced the hiring just over a month after he was fired as manager of the Washington Nationals. Williams was on the Diamondbacks coaching staff from 2010 through 2013, the last three seasons as third-base coach.■ The Seattle Mariners addressed their bullpen problems by acquiring the right-handed reliever Joaquin Benoit from the San Diego Padres for two minor league prospects. Benoit, 38, was 6-5 with a 2.34 E.R.A. in 67 appearances last season. The Mariners’ relievers ranked 25th last season with a 4.15 E.R.A., and they had 24 blown saves.A version of this brief appears in print on November 13, 2015, on Page B12 of the New York edition with the headline: Williams Back in Arizona.
