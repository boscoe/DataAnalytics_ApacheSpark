By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 14, 2015
Kennedy Meeks had 25 points and 11 rebounds to help No. 1 North Carolina start its season with a 91-67 win over Temple at the Veterans Classic in Annapolis, Md.■ Jamal Murray scored 13 of his 19 points in the second half, and No. 2 Kentucky outlasted Albany, 78-65, in Lexington.■ Brannen Greene hit all five of his 3-point attempts and had 18 points, and No. 4 Kansas pounded Northern Colorado, 109-72, in Lawrence.■ Grayson Allen scored 20 of his 26 points in the first half, and No. 5 Duke beat Siena, 92-74, in the first round of the 2K Classic in Durham, N.C.■ In a women’s game, A’ja Wilson had 20 points and 14 rebounds, Alaina Coates added 17 points, and No. 2 South Carolina, playing at home, withstood Kelsey Mitchell’s 36 points to beat No. 6 Ohio State, 88-80.A version of this brief appears in print on November 14, 2015, on Page D4 of the New York edition with the headline: No. 1 and No. 2 Triumph.
