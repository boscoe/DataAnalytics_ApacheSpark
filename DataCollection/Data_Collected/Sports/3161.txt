By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 18, 2015
The police in Lincoln, Neb., were investigating a report of a rape at the residence of Nebraska quarterback Tommy Armstrong Jr. and receiver Jordan Westerkamp.A police spokeswoman said that a 20-year-old woman had gone to a Lincoln hospital on Sunday and said that she had been raped.
