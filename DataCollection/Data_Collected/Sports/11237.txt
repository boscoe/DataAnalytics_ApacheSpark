By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 13, 2015
After Providence beat Boston University, 4-3, for its first men’s hockey championship Saturday night, hundreds of fans held a rowdy celebration near the college. Although the police said no one was arrested, an officer was injured when someone threw a bottle that hit his head, The Providence Journal reported. The police said some revelers burned a couch and set other fires in the street.The injured officer was released from the hospital after having surgery and getting 20 stitches. An official celebration was expected to be held Tuesday.■ Two Iowa State football players were arrested in separate incidents after Saturday’s spring game. The Des Moines Register reported that Jack Rhoads, who is the son of Coach Paul Rhoads, was one of the two. The other was Terry Ayeni. Police records say that Jack Rhoads was arrested around 11:30 p.m. on suspicion of public intoxication.
