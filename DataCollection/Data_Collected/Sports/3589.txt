The awards will be handed out Dec. 10. ■ The N.C.A.A. football oversight committee will wait until next week to complete a plan for matching sub-.500 teams with bowls if there are not enough bowl-eligible squads to fill 40 games. Seventy-one teams have won at least six games and secured at least a .500 record, the minimum for playing in the postseason.
