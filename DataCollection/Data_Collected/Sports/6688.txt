AdvertisementSupported bySports Briefing | RacewalkingBy The Associated PressFive Russian racewalkers were banned by the Russian Anti-Doping Agency. Bans of three years and two months were handed to the Olympic champions Sergei Kirdyapkin and Olga Kaniskina, and the world champion Sergei Bakulin. Valery Borchin, an Olympic gold medalist, was banned for eight years for a second doping offense. Vladimir Kanaykin was banned for life for a repeat offense. The bans do not affect any Olympic medals. Advertisement
