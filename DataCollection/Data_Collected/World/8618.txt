By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 25, 2015
A west Indian city has banned most public gatherings in an attempt to halt the spread of swine flu, which has claimed at least 926 lives nationwide in 11 weeks. Officials prohibited gatherings of five or more people in Ahmedabad, the capital of Gujarat State, starting Wednesday.
