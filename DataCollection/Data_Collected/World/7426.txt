By JAMES KANTERFEB. 2, 2015
A man with a gun and a chain saw in his car was detained Monday by the police near the European Parliament, prompting the temporary evacuation of several buildings. The man, a Slovakian citizen born in 1982 and identified only as W.R., was dressed in camouflage and sought a meeting with the Parliament’s president, according to a statement by the Brussels prosecutor’s office. He was arrested, accused of threatening an attack and possessing unauthorized weapons.
