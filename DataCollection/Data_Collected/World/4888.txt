By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 16, 2015
Germany on Wednesday introduced a new police unit that officials said would be better armed, outfitted and trained to deal with terrorism. The teams will be based in areas where the national police have helicopters, meaning they can be moved into situations quickly, Interior Minister Thomas de Maizière said as he presented the first group of 50 new officers.
