By REUTERSAPRIL 17, 2015
Four members of the British Navy have been charged with sexual assault in connection with an attack that took place in Nova Scotia on April 10, a spokesman for the Canadian Department of National Defense said on Friday. The four men are members of a British Navy hockey team that was in the province for a tournament, according to the spokesman. The assault took place at a party and involved a Halifax-area woman, The Chronicle-Herald newspaper reported. The British Ministry of Defense said in a statement that it took allegations of this nature very seriously, but declined to comment further.
