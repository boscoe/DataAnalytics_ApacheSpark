By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 25, 2015
A string of bombings ripped mainly through commercial areas in the capital, Baghdad, on Sunday, killing at least 15 people and wounding dozens, officials said. The deadliest attack took place in the Bab al-Sharji area of Baghdad, where a bomb went off outside a small restaurant, killing seven civilians and wounding 22, a police officer said. Other bombs exploded in the central Sibaa district, the southern suburb of Arab Jabour and the southern district of Abu Dashir.
