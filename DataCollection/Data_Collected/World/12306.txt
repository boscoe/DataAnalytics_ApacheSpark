By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 2, 2015
Prime Minister Stephen Harper of Canada made a surprise visit to Iraq on Saturday and pledged continued support for the country’s battle against the Islamic State group. Mr. Harper’s visit to Baghdad and Iraq’s Kurdish region in the north came as seven women and children were killed by a roadside bomb and a suicide blast killed six Iraqi soldiers.
