By REUTERSJAN. 6, 2015
President Yahya Jammeh shuffled three major posts in his government, state television reported, a week after a coup attempt in the West African nation. No reason was given for the shakeup involving the ministers of foreign affairs, information and transportation. Mr. Jammeh, who came to power in a 1994 coup, has accused foreign-based dissidents of last Tuesday’s attack. Prosecutors in the United States have charged a Texas businessman with bankrolling the coup plot with the support of a former Army sergeant.
