By REUTERSAPRIL 8, 2015
The British police were hunting Wednesday for a gang that raided safe deposit boxes in London’s main jewelry district, making off with cash and gems in what was thought to be a major heist. The Metropolitan Police in London said the thieves used heavy cutting equipment to get into the vault at Hatton Garden Safety Deposit, where they are believed to have broken into 60 to 70 boxes. The police have not released an estimate of the value of items stolen.A version of this brief appears in print on April 9, 2015, on Page A6 of the New York edition with the headline: Europe; Britain: Gang Hits Jewelry Quarter.
