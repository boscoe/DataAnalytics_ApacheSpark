By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSOCT. 8, 2015
The Supreme Court on Thursday dismissed a lawsuit by the political opposition that had sought to block constitutional changes to presidential term limits, clearing the way for President Paul Kagame to run again. The judges’ decision said that there was nothing in the Constitution that prohibited lawmakers from modifying it.
