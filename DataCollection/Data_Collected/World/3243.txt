By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 18, 2015
The United States and Cuba on Wednesday signed an agreement to join forces and protect the vast array of fish and corals they share as countries separated by just 90 miles. It is the first environmental accord since the announcement of plans to renew diplomatic relations in December.
