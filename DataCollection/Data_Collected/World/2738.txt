Kurdish forces aided by thousands of lightly armed Yazidi fighters captured a strategic highway in northern Iraq in the early stages of an offensive to reclaim the town of Sinjar from the Islamic State, which seized it last year.Kurdish pesh merga fighters, part of a convoy, waited on Mount Sinjar.
