
JAN. 30, 2015

Thousands of buildings were burned, damaged or destroyed in northern Nigerian towns in recent days when Boko Haram militants stormed through, according to witnesses, officials and analyses of satellite images by Digital Globe, Amnesty International and Human Rights Watch. About 57 percent of Doro Gowon, home to a now-destroyed military base, appears to have been leveled.
