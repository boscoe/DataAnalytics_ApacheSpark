By REUTERSJAN. 14, 2015
An Israeli soldier fatally wounded a Palestinian on Wednesday in a confrontation over an attempt to steal a vehicle in the occupied West Bank, the police said. A soldier shot the man, in his 20s, fearing he might be an armed militant, as police officers chased him and another person suspected of trying to hot-wire a vehicle at a supermarket outside a Jewish settlement block near Bethlehem, a police spokeswoman, Luba Samri, said.A version of this brief appears in print on January 15, 2015, on Page A8 of the New York edition with the headline: Israel: Soldier Kills Palestinian Suspected in Theft.
