By REUTERSNOV. 2, 2015
President Pierre Nkurunziza said Monday that Burundians must surrender illegal firearms by Saturday or risk being “dealt with as enemies of the nation.” The demand came after months of protests over his re-election in July. The president’s plan to seek a third term, which many critics contended was forbidden by the Constitution, threw the country into turmoil.
