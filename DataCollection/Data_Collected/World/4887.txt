By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 16, 2015
The United Nations’ highest court ruled Wednesday that Nicaragua had violated Costa Rica’s territorial sovereignty by establishing a military camp and digging channels near the mouth of the San Juan River, and it ordered Nicaragua to pay compensation.
