
UPDATED JAN. 11, 2015

The three men killed by the French police on Friday have been linked by French and American intelligence officials to Islamic militant organizations. Two of the men were brothers suspected in the killings at the satirical newspaper Charlie Hebdo. The third man was suspected of seizing hostages at a kosher supermarket and threatening to kill them if the police attacked the brothers. Here is a look at key moments in their lives, where they intersected and what might have influenced them.
