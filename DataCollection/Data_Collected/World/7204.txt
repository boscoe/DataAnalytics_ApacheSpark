By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 29, 2015
The cabinet on Thursday approved a law offering dual citizenship to the descendants of Sephardic Jews, the term commonly used for those who once lived in the Iberian Peninsula. The rights will apply to those who can demonstrate “a traditional connection” to Portuguese Sephardic Jews. Portugal says it is redressing a historic wrong, five centuries after thousands of Jews were burned at the stake and others were expelled or forced to convert to Christianity.
