

      By THE NEW YORK TIMES

JAN. 22, 2015

During his upcoming visit to India, President Obama is scheduled to attend Republic Day celebrations on Jan. 26 as an honored guest of Prime Minister Narendra Modi.  It will be the first time that an American president attends the commemoration of India's constitution.Mr. Obama and Mr. Modi are also to discuss climate change, a potential agreement on civilian nuclear plants, military purchases, and investment and manufacturing. But it remains to be seen whether the president’s trip will be symbolic or lead to significant agreements.What do you expect from Mr. Obama’s visit to India?
