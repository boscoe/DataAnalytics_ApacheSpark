By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 18, 2015
Indonesia on Sunday brushed aside last-minute appeals by foreign leaders over the weekend and executed by firing squad six people convicted of drug trafficking, including five foreigners. Four men from Brazil, Malawi, Nigeria and the Netherlands and two women from Indonesia and Vietnam were shot to death on Sunday in Central Java Province.
