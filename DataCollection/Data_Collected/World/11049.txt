By REUTERSAPRIL 8, 2015
The University of Cape Town decided Wednesday to remove a statue of the British imperialist Cecil Rhodes, which has set off protests by students who say it symbolizes an institutional racism that prevails in South Africa decades after the end of apartheid. A special council set up by the university to decide the issue said the statue would be taken down and stored pending a decision from the heritage authorities on a location.
