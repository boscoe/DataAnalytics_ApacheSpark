By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 31, 2015
The government has revoked the citizenship of 72 Bahrainis as part of its responsibility to “protect the security and stability of Bahrain,” the Interior Ministry said Saturday. The list includes both Sunni and Shiite Bahrainis. In the past, the government has revoked the citizenship of opposition activists, but among those on the latest list is Turki al-Binali, a Sunni preacher and one of the leading ideologues for the Islamic State extremist group.
