By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSNOV. 12, 2015
Two more people have died of burns sustained in a nightclub fire last month in Bucharest, officials said Thursday, raising the total number of deaths in the blaze to 53. Twenty-six people initially died in the Oct. 30 fire, but people who sustained severe burns have since succumbed to their injuries almost daily. The Health Ministry said that 67 people remained hospitalized in Bucharest, and that 31 burn patients were being treated in other countries.A version of this brief appears in print on November 13, 2015, on Page A8 of the New York edition with the headline: Europe; Romania: Fire’s Death Toll Increases.
