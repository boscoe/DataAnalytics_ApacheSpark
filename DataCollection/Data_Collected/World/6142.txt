By REUTERSJAN. 10, 2015
Cambodian health authorities have found 212 villagers infected with H.I.V. in a district where an unlicensed medic has been accused of spreading the virus with contaminated equipment, according to findings released Saturday by the government and international health agencies. Prosecutors charged the medic with murder last month.
