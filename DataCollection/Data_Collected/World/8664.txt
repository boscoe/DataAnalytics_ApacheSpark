By REUTERSFEB. 26, 2015
Three Shiite Muslims were sentenced to death on Thursday and seven others were sentenced to life in prison after being convicted of killing three police officers last year, the public prosecution office announced on its Twitter account. The case centered on a bombing near the capital, Manama, last March that left three police officers dead. Shiites took to the streets four years ago to demand greater political rights from the Sunni monarchy.
