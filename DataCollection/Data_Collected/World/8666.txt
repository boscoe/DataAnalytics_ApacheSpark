By AGENCE FRANCE-PRESSEFEB. 26, 2015
Bombings in a north-central city, Jos, and a northeastern town, Biu, left at least 35 people dead on Thursday, officials said. At least 18 people were killed when a bomber blew himself up at a bus station in Biu, officials said.
