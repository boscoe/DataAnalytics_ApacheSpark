By REUTERSMARCH 3, 2015
The American first lady, Michelle Obama, will travel to Japan and Cambodia this month as part of an effort to support education for girls, the White House said Tuesday. She will visit Tokyo and Kyoto in Japan, and then the Cambodian city of Siem Reap, it said in a statement.
