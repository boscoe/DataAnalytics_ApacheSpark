By REUTERSMAY 2, 2015
A French patrol ship rescued 217 migrants from three small boats that had run into trouble off the coast of Libya on Saturday, the maritime police said in a statement. The French crew also detained two people suspected of being involved in efforts to smuggle migrants into Europe, according to the statement. The French ship was responding to a call from the rescue coordination center in Rome as part of the European Union’s maritime mission.
