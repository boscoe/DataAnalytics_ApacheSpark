By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 7, 2015
A masked gunman shot and killed a Kenyan lawmaker and three other people early Saturday on a street in the capital, Nairobi, a senior police official said. The lawmaker, George Muchai, his two bodyguards and a driver were killed after they stopped to buy a newspaper, said the official, Chief Paul Wanjama of the Nairobi Central Police.
