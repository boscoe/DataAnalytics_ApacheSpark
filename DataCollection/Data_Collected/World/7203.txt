By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 29, 2015
A 19-year-old man brandishing a fake weapon threatened a security guard to gain access to the headquarters of the Dutch national broadcaster NOS and demanded airtime on Thursday before the police stormed the television studio and arrested him. Nobody was injured in the confrontation in Hilversum, but it forced NOS off the air for about an hour. The police said that the man’s gun was a fake and that he had no criminal record. His motives were not immediately clear. A prosecutor, Johan Bac, said the man was from the town of Pijnacker.
