By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJAN. 4, 2015
A suicide car bomber detonated his explosives near a convoy of elite Somali troops known as the Alpha Group in the capital, Mogadishu, killing at least four people, a Somali police officer said Sunday. The Shabab militant group claimed responsibility for the attack, according to the group’s Radio Andulus.
