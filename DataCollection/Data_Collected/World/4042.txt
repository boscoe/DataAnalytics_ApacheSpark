By REUTERSDEC. 2, 2015
The heaviest rainfall in decades has caused widespread flooding across the southern state of Tamil Nadu, driving thousands from their homes, shutting auto factories and paralyzing the airport in the state capital, Chennai. The national weather office on Wednesday predicted three more days of torrential rain in the state of nearly 70 million people.
