By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 10, 2015
President Vladimir V. Putin of Russia and his Egyptian counterpart, Abdel Fattah el-Sisi, said Tuesday in Cairo that the two countries planned to jointly build Egypt’s first nuclear power plant. The announcement came at a news conference after talks at a former palace, where Mr. Putin arrived to a 21-gun salute and 200 Egyptian schoolchildren waving flags and crying out the leaders’ names.
