By IHSANULLAH TIPU MEHSUDOCT. 19, 2015
ISLAMABAD, Pakistan — At least 10 people were killed and 20 were injured when a powerful bomb ripped through a bus carrying commuters in Quetta, the capital of Pakistan’s restive Baluchistan Province, on Monday, the police said. A senior police official, Zahoor Afridi, said in a telephone interview that the bomb had been placed on the roof of the bus and was set off by remote control.
