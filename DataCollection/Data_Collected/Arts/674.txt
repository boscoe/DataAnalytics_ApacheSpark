(ABC)WHAT’S STREAMING NOWMEAN GIRLS (2004) After years of home schooling, Cady, played by Lindsay Lohan, returns from Africa to attend high school in her native United States in this film written by Tina Fey and produced by Lorne Michaels. Two classmates show her the ropes and help her adjust by explaining the cliques. The worst of these is the Plastics, which Cady infiltrates in a revenge plot. She slowly becomes a part of the Plastics’ world. “The sureness of tone in the script achieves such clarity that our sympathies remain with Cady even as she begins to become what she has beheld,” Elvis Mitchell wrote in The Times.
