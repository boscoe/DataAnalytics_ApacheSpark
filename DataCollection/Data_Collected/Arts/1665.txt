By ANDREW R. CHOWDEC. 13, 2015
Kathryn Bigelow is developing a dramatic series about jihadi recruitment for HBO alongside the rapper and poet K’Naan Warsame. Ms. Bigelow, who won an Oscar for directing “The Hurt Locker,” will executive produce the series, according to The Hollywood Reporter.The series will be titled “The Recruiters,” and is set in Minnesota. Mr. Warsame, a Somali-Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter and poet, will write and direct it. In two opinion pieces for The New York Times, Mr. Warsame wrote about spreading awareness about the violence and poverty in his homeland, Somalia, as well as about the difficulties of adjusting to fame.This will be Mr. Warsame’s first television project, and he recently posted a casting call for the series’ lead role of a “young Somali man” on Twitter.A version of this article appears in print on December 14, 2015, on Page C3 of the New York edition with the headline: Series on Jihadists Is in the Works for HBO.
