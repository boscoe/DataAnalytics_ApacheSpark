This exhibition, presented in collaboration with the Carter Center, examines international efforts to control and wipe out diseases. The episode, titled “Making a Way Out of No Way,” covers the years 1897 to 1940. On Wednesday, Ms. Wilkerson, the author of “The Warmth of Other Suns: The Epic Story of America’s Great Migration,” the award-winning 2010 book, will discuss the migration of blacks from the American South.
