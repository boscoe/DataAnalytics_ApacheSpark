By NEIL GENZLINGERJAN. 17, 2015
Haven’t we already seen ride-along shows set in Louisiana? Yes, we have. So what’s different about “Nightwatch,” which begins Thursday on A&E and follows police, firefighters and ambulance workers on the overnight shift in New Orleans?Well, it has Dick Wolf’s name attached to it. Mr. Wolf, creator of the “Law & Order” franchise, has been having success in the reality genre. He’s an executive producer of “Cold Justice,” which re-examines cold cases and has begun its third season on TNT, and he’s also an executive producer on “Nightwatch,” billed as a nine-episode series.
