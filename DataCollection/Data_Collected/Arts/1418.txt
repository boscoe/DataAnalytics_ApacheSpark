Join some country crooners at the Grand Ole Opry or rehash Season 1 of “Mozart in the Jungle.”What’s on TVTHE WIZ LIVE! 8 p.m. on NBC. Can they ease on down the road without tripping? Tune in as the newcomer Shanice Williams, playing Dorothy, joins Queen Latifah as the Wiz; Mary J. Blige as Evillene, the Wicked Witch of the West; David Alan Grier as the Cowardly Lion; Ne-Yo as the Tin Man; and Common as the Bouncer in this live production of the Broadway musical, revamped by Harvey Fierstein. Stephanie Mills, who played Dorothy in the original production, returns here as Auntie Em. (Image: Amber Riley, left, and Ms. Williams)HAPPY-GO-LUCKY (2008) 1:30 p.m. on Showtime. In this Mike Leigh study, a London schoolteacher’s irrepressible optimism turns the days of those she’s intent on brightening up into a living hell. Sally Hawkins is “glorious,” Manohla Dargis wrote in The New York Times, a “gurgling, burbling stream of gasps, giggles and words.” (Image: Ms. Hawkins, left, and Alexis Zegerman)THE TALENTED MR. RIPLEY (1999) 5:30 p.m. on HBO2. Starting with the loan of a Princeton jacket, Tom Ripley, a men’s room attendant played by Matt Damon, begins his ascent of the social ladder — then murders his way up the rest of the rungs. Jude Law, Gwyneth Paltrow and Philip Seymour Hoffman round out the cast of Anthony Minghella’s thriller, which “offers diabolically smart surprises wherever you care to look,” Janet Maslin wrote in The Times.CMA COUNTRY CHRISTMAS 9 p.m. on ABC. Heath Ledger and Jake Gyllenhaal are cowboys secretly in love in Ang Lee’s Oscar-winning adaptation of the Annie Proulx short story.
