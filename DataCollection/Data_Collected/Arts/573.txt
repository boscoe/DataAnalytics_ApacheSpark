New year, new “Sherlock.” BBC and PBS Masterpiece’s runaway television hit, “Sherlock,” will return with a special, “Sherlock: The Abominable Bride,” on Jan. 1.A trailer for the 90-minute special, released over the weekend, teases Martin Freeman’s Watson and Benedict Cumberbatch’s Holmes solving crimes in 1890s London, the original setting of Arthur Conan Doyle’s mystery series. While the rest of the series is updated to the modern day, the trailer shows Mr. Freeman with a handlebar mustache and Mr. Cumberbatch with a pipe and his character’s customary deerstalker hat.The episode will be shown in theaters across Britain as well as on BBC One and PBS in the United States, marking the first time that the series has arrived in Britain and the United States on the same day.The cast for the special includes Rupert Graves, Una Stubbs, Louise Brealey and Amanda Abbington.Filming of the fourth season is expected to begin in 2016.A version of this article appears in print on 10/26/2015, on page C3 of the NewYork edition with the headline: Spend New Yearu2019s Day With u2018Sherlocku2019.
