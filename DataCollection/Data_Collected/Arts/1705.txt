By KATHRYN SHATTUCKDEC. 16, 2015
Sing it! Patti LaBelle and James Wright Chanel whip up some sweet potato pie. Buddy the Elf, voiced by Jim Parsons, deals with his daddy issues. And some cast members of “Nashville” hit the road.What’s on TVPATTI LABELLE’S HOLIDAY PIES 8:30 p.m. on Cooking Channel. Whoa, yeaaaaahhh! James Wright Chanel, who became a viral sensation singing the praises of Ms. LaBelle’s sweet potato pie, wails on some high notes with that R&B diva in the kitchen.REQUIEM FOR A DREAM (2000) 6:15 p.m. on Starz Cinema. Jared Leto, Ellen Burstyn, Jennifer Connelly and Marlon Wayans are an ensemble tour de force as four addicts — a Brooklyn pretty boy, his mother, his girlfriend and his pal — self-destructing as they search for a reality-blurring high. “Be warned,” Elvis Mitchell wrote in The New York Times about this hyperkinetic drama from Darren Aronofsky. “It’s a downer, and a knockout.” (Image: Mr. Leto)ELF: BUDDY’S MUSICAL CHRISTMAS 8 p.m. on NBC. Jim Parsons stars as Buddy, the North Pole elf who sets off for New York City to find his biological father. Mark Hamill, Ed Asner, Fred Armisen, Jay Leno, Matt Lauer and Gilbert Gottfried lend their voices to this animated version of the movie and the Broadway show. (Image: Buddy)GONE GIRL (2014) 8 p.m. on HBO. Rosamund Pike plays Amy Dunne, who on her fifth wedding anniversary goes missing from her home after what looks like a struggle, with broken glass and mopped-up blood. Ben Affleck is her husband, Nick, now the prime suspect. And suddenly their small Missouri town is trapped in a nationwide media circus. At its strongest this David Fincher thriller “plays like a queasily, at times gleefully, funny horror movie about a modern marriage,” Manohla Dargis wrote in The Times. But by the movie’s second half, “you may wish that Amy would stay gone.” (Image: Ms. Pike)AdvertisementTHE MAGICIANS 10 p.m. on Syfy. A grad student at a secret university specializing in magic discovers that his upstate New York fantasy world is a little too real for comfort. Sneak a peek at this new series spun from the Lev Grossman novels before its official premiere on Jan. 25.NASHVILLE: ON THE RECORD 3 10 p.m. on ABC. Clare Bowen, Chris Carmack, Charles Esten, Sam Palladio, Aubrey Peeples, and Lennon and Maisy Stella tour the country performing songs from the “Nashville” TV series.THE TONIGHT SHOW STARRING JIMMY FALLON 11:35 p.m. on NBC. It’s Will Ferrell, fresh off his “Saturday Night Live” impersonation of former President George W. Bush. Alicia Vikander of “The Danish Girl” sidles up next to him. Twenty One Pilots perform.AdvertisementWhat’s StreamingALEX GIBNEY’S PICKS on SundanceNow Doc Club.
