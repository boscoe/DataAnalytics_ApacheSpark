Tickets to the coming film “Star Wars: The Force Awakens” will go on sale on Monday evening, after the broadcast of the first full trailer. The trailer will have its premiere during halftime of “Monday Night Football” on ESPN, which starts at 8:15 p.m. Eastern time. A Spanish-language version of the trailer will be broadcast simultaneously on ESPN Deportes.The trailer will be the peak of a campaign push that started last November when a teaser was released. A merchandise blitz followed, as well as appearances at San Diego Comic Con. The movie poster was released on Sunday, and it prominently features the franchise newcomer Daisy Ridley, who plays Rey in the new movie, as well as Harrison Ford’s celebrated Han Solo. The actor John Boyega, who will play Finn, also posted what appears to be a snippet of the trailer on his Instagram. Tickets will be available everywhere movie tickets are sold. The film, which is directed by J. J. Abrams and stars Mr. Ford, Ms. Ridley and Mr. Boyega, as well as Carrie Fisher, Adam Driver and Lupita Nyong’o, is scheduled to open on Dec. 18.
