By KATHRYN SHATTUCKDEC. 10, 2015
We’re still just kids at heart when Tom Hanks, Tim Allen and company celebrate the 20th anniversary of “Toy Story.” Michael Bublé croons in the holidays in Tinseltown. And Dolly Parton remembers her Tennessee childhood and a gift that changed everything.What’s on TVTOY STORY AT 20: TO INFINITY AND BEYOND 8 p.m. on ABC. You’ve got a friend in us: Tom Hanks, Tim Allen, Annie Potts, John Ratzenberger, the songwriter Randy Newman and the director John Lasseter celebrate the 20th anniversary of Pixar Animation Studios’ “Toy Story.” This heart-tugger — about Woody, a cowboy doll who grows jealous when Buzz Lightyear, a swaggering spaceman figure, takes his place on the bed of their young owner — follows at 9. In 1995, Janet Maslin, writing in The New York Times, called it “the sweetest and savviest film of the year.” (Image: Woody and Buzz Lightyear)MICHAEL BUBLÉ’S CHRISTMAS IN HOLLYWOOD 8 p.m. on NBC. Celine Dion, Sharon Jones and the Dap-Kings, and Tori Kelly help Mr. Bublé celebrate his favorite holiday with renditions of “Here Comes Santa Claus,” “Jingle Bells,” “White Christmas,” “Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas” and his new song, “The More You Give (The More You’ll Have).” Gigi Hadid, Kylie Jenner, Jay Leno, Eva Longoria, William Shatner and Blake Shelton provide comic relief. (Image: Mr. Bublé)THE BIG BANG THEORY 8 p.m. on CBS. Sheldon has an earworm.BONES 8 p.m. on Fox. Brennan and Booth go undercover at an Old West-style shooting competition before investigating a police officer’s murder that strikes a little too close to home.AdvertisementDAFT PUNK UNCHAINED 9 p.m. on Showtime. Kanye West, Pharrell Williams and Nile Rodgers speculate on the covert career of Thomas Bangalter and Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo, the French musicians who make up this masked electronic duo.DOLLY PARTON’S COAT OF MANY COLORS (2015) 9 p.m. on NBC. Ms. Parton harks back to her roots in the Great Smoky Mountains of Tennessee, circa 1955, in this tale about a raggedy patchwork coat that helped set her on the path toward success. Alyvia Alyn Lind plays Ms. Parton at 9 years old, Jennifer Nettles is her mother, Ricky Schroder her father and Gerald McRaney her grandfather. (Image: Ms. Parton)BILLY ON THE STREET 10:30 p.m. on TruTV. Will Ferrell ambushes New Yorkers with Christmas presents and holiday cheer. Rachel Dratch takes on an obstacle course called “Leah Remini’s Escape From Scientology.”AdvertisementWhat’s StreamingTHE FORBIDDEN ROOM (2015) on Fandor.
