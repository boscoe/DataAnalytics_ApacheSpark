This week the best teams of the season meet for the “Cage Match” finals. At 11 p.m., Upright Citizens Brigade Theater, 307 West 26th Street, Chelsea, 212-366-9176, newyork.ucbtheatre.com. (Elise Czajkowski)‘Dave Gorman’s PowerPoint Presentation’ (Friday, Saturday, Tuesday, Wednesday, and next Friday and Saturday) Mr. Gorman specializes in high-concept comedic adventures, such as his breakout 2000 show, “Are You Dave Gorman?,” for which he traveled in search of others who shared his name. For “PowerPoint Presentation,” which is getting a two-week run at SubCulture, this British comic has “formed a double act with a projector screen.” (Through Jan. 31.) Fridays and Saturdays at 7 and 9 p.m., Tuesday and Wednesday at 7:30 p.m., 45 Bleecker Street, near Lafayette Street, East Village, 212-533-5470, subculturenewyork.com. She’ll receive support from Ilana Glazer, one of the stars of “Broad City.” At 6:30 p.m., Upright Citizens Brigade Theater East, 153 East Third Street, East Village, 212-366-9231, east.ucbtheatre.com. (Czajkowski)★ Louis CK (Saturday and Tuesday) He reigns among the greatest working comics today: Not only has Louis CK won over comedy fans of all stripes with his Emmy-winning FX series, “Louie,” but he is also noted for his devotion to the craft of stand-up, regularly performing short sets at small clubs and theaters to perfect his act. At 8 p.m., Madison Square Garden, 866-858-0008, thegarden.com. (Czajkowski)Trevor Noah (Friday through Sunday) This young South African comedian, known for his hilarious, heartfelt stories of growing up mixed race (born to a black South African mother and a white Swiss-German father) during the apartheid era, recently became a contributor on “The Daily Show With Jon Stewart.” Friday at 8 and 10:30 p.m., Saturday at 7:30 and 10 p.m., Sunday at 8 and 10 p.m., Gotham Comedy Club, 208 West 23rd Street, Chelsea, 212-367-9000, gothamcomedyclub.com. (Czajkowski)‘Sideshow Goshko’  (Thursday) Leslie Goshko celebrates the six anniversary of her storytelling show with Andy Christie, Catie Lazarus and Martin Dockery. In honor of the occasion, six bottles of her “Sideshow Sauce” wine will be given away to selected audience members. At 7 p.m., KGB Bar, 85 East Fourth Street, East Village, 212-505-3360, kgbbar.com. (Czajkowski)★ ‘TJ & Dave’ (Friday through Monday) TJ Jagodowski and Dave Pasquesi, the Chicago long-form improvisers and the focus of the 2009 documentary “Trust Us, This Is All Made Up,” return to the Barrow Street Theater with one of the best and most reliably funny improv shows in the country. Friday through Sunday at 9:30 p.m., Monday at 9 p.m., Barrow Street Theater, 27 Barrow Street, at Seventh Avenue South, West Village, 212-868-4444, barrowstreettheatre.com.
