Compiled by LORI HOLCOMB-HOLLANDJAN. 4, 2015
Organizers of Spoleto Festival USA have announced the lineup for the 39th annual event, including more than 150 performances of opera, dance, theater and music.Highlights of the 17-day festival include the world premiere of “Paradise Interrupted,” a contemporary opera composed by Huang Ruo and designed and directed by Jennifer Wen Ma, whose visual installations were used in the opening and closing ceremonies of the 2008 Beijing Olympics. Also making world debuts will be “What Moves You,” by the dancer Lil Buck and the cellist Ashley Bathgate, as well as the Spoleto-commissioned performance of Francesco Cavalli’s opera “Veremonda l’amazzone di Aragona.” Other festival highlights will include a new production of “Romeo and Juliet” by Shakespeare’s Globe theater company, the Carlo Colla and Sons Marionette Company performing “Sleeping Beauty,” and the Casus Circus of Australia. Featured dance performances will include Trisha Brown Dance Company, Shen Wei Dance Arts, and Scottish Ballet’s adaptation of the Tennessee Williams play “A Streetcar Named Desire.” In the musical category, highlights include performances by the Westminster Choir, Emmylou Harris and Rodney Crowell.Spoleto Festival USA runs May 22 through June 7 in Charleston, S.C.
