A searchable guide to these and other shows is at nytimes.com/events.‘Ask Roulette’  (Thursday) Jody Avirgan hosts this unscripted conversation show, which features strangers asking one another any questions they choose. This month’s guests include Michelle Wolf, Eli Bolin and Tracy Clayton. At 7 p.m., Housing Works Bookstore Cafe, 126 Crosby Street, near Houston Street, SoHo, housingworksbookstore.org, 212-334-3324. (Elise Czajkowski)★ Bideawee’s Laugh for A-Paws Comedy Night (Wednesday) This night of stand-up raises funds for Bideawee, an animal welfare organization that rescues and adopts dogs and cats around New York. The impressive lineup includes Nick DiPaolo, Dan Soder, Monroe Martin, Big Jay Oakerson, Laurie Kilmartin and Aaron Berg. At 8 p.m., the Stand, 239 Third Avenue, at 20th Street, 212-677-2600, thestandnyc.com. (Czajkowski)Margaret Cho  (Friday and Saturday) Ms. Cho has always been a brazen comedian whose energetic shows touch on liberal politics, sexuality and L.G.B.T. rights. This weekend she’ll tape her new comedy special at the Gramercy Theater. Friday at 8 p.m., Saturday at 7 and 10 p.m., 127 East 23rd Street, Manhattan, 212-614-6932, livenation.com. (Czajkowski)Advertisement‘Des Bishop: Made in China’ (Monday, Thursday and next Friday) Mr. Bishop moved to China with the intention of ultimately performing stand-up in Mandarin. In this show, he recounts the year he spent there, during which he worked as a host in a restaurant and appeared on a Chinese dating show. (Through March 29.) Monday at 7:30 p.m., Thursday and next Friday at 9:30 p.m., Barrow Street Theater, 27 Barrow Street, at Seventh Avenue South, West Village, 212-868-4444, barrowstreettheatre.com. (Czajkowski)★ ‘Uptown Showdown’ (Tuesday) This show, performed every two months, features two teams of comedians debating the least pressing issues of the day. Josh Gondelman, Dave Hill, Sheng Wang, Victor Varnado and Brooke Van Poppelen square off on “Wine vs. Beer.” At 8 p.m., Symphony Space, 2537 Broadway, at 95th Street, 212-864-5400, symphonyspace.org.
