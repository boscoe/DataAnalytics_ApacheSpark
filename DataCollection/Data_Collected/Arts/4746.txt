Las Vegas is stocking up on divas.Following in the extravagant footsteps of Celine Dion, Shania Twain, Britney Spears and Mariah Carey, whose residency on the Strip got off to a pitchy start last week, Jennifer Lopez has announced a run of shows at the Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino’s AXIS Theater, beginning in January.Ms. Lopez, 45, shared the news on Tuesday’s episode of “American Idol,” where she serves as a judge. Fox announced this week that the singing competition would end next year, following its 15th season, with Ms. Lopez returning for the last hurrah.“I’ve been preparing for this moment my entire life,” Ms. Lopez said of the Las Vegas concerts in a statement. “The show is going to be a multifaceted, high-energy ‘Jenny From the Block’ party, mixing all aspects of what my fans and I love — dance, hip-hop, Latin and pop. It’s going to be the show I’ve always dreamt of creating.”Tickets for the 20 scheduled shows — which will be next January, February, May and June — go on sale Saturday, May 16. More information is at JLoVegas.com.A version of this article appears in print on 05/14/2015, on page C3 of the NewYork edition with the headline: Jennifer Lopez Plans Run Of Las Vegas Shows.
