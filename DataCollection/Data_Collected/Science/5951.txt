
JAN. 6, 2015

Astronomers have found a recipe for rocky, Earth-like planets orbiting other stars.

Photo illustration by The New York Times

Astronomers at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics have found a recipe for Earth-like planets orbiting other stars. Small, rocky planets in other solar systems seem to contain the same basic ingredients that form our own planet.
