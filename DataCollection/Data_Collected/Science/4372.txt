
DEC. 9, 2015

A new version was released of the international agreement that negotiators at the Paris climate conference have been haggling over for more than a week.
The latest draft of the international climate agreement that 195 nations have been haggling over was released midafternoon Wednesday. The remaining points of disagreement will seem fairly abstruse to anyone who doesn’t follow climate politics closely, but they have important implications.AdvertisementThe latest draft of the international climate agreement that 195 nations have been haggling over was released midafternoon Wednesday.
