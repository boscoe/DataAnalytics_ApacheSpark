Remember when it seemed as though Ajit Pai, the new chairman of the Federal Communications Commission, was seeking the tech industry’s input on changes to the so-called net neutrality rules?Perhaps he did not listen very carefully to what they had to say.On Wednesday, Mr. Pai outlined a plan to loosen the government’s oversight of high-speed internet providers. The heart of that oversight has been the concept of net neutrality.
