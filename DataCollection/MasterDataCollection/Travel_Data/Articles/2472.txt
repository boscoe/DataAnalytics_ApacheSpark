Wherever the leaves change color, expect hotel rates to rise as fall color peaks. Still, there are a few deals in leaf-peeping season, especially if you can travel midweek.In the Catskills, the 11-room Arnold House offers Shandelee Mountain hikes and midweek rates from $169, with breakfast. Weekends begin at $229.In the Adirondacks, Whiteface Lodge offers fall rates from $282 a night, down from $448 in high season.
