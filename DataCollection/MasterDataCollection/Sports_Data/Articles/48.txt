The best-selling sports books in February 2016, according to the New York Times best-seller list.1. THE BOYS IN THE BOAT by Daniel James Brown (Penguin). A group of American rowers pursues gold at the 1936 Berlin Olympic Games.2. UNBROKEN by Laura Hillenbrand (Random House). An Olympic runner’s story of survival as a prisoner of the Japanese in World War II; a 2014 movie.3. MANNING by Peyton Manning and Archie Manning with John Underwood (It/HarperCollins). The personal story of the legacy football family.4. LIFE IS NOT AN ACCIDENT by Jay Williams (Harper). Memoir of an ESPN commentator whose early rise toward a promising career in professional basketball was cut short by a motorcycle accident.
