By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 6, 2016
Andy Murray and Rafael Nadal will meet again in the semifinals of the Madrid Open on Saturday, a rematch of last year’s final won by Murray. The winner will face either Novak Djokovic or Kei Nishikori. Murray defeated Tomas Berdych, 6-3, 6-2, while Nadal downed João Sousa, 6-0, 4-6, 6-3.
