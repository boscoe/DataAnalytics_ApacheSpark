By DAVID WHITENOV. 13, 2016
8:30 p.m. Eastern, ESPN  Line: Bengals by 1In the Bengals’ last game, in London before a bye week, they did nothing but confuse the hosts with a soccer-style tie. Monday night could prove more exciting as two top-12 pass offenses meet two bottom-12 pass defenses. By the time the game is over, the analyst Jon Gruden may have Giants quarterback Eli Manning and his Cincinnati counterpart, Andy Dalton, on the Hyperbole Express to Canton.Giants receiver Odell Beckham Jr. is unlikely to repeat his Monday night meltdown from last month, when he earned a split decision against a kicking net. Still, expect to see his eccentric side, as well as a lot of throws his way.
