By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 29, 2016
Jrue Holiday scored 10 of his 22 points in the fourth quarter, and the New Orleans Pelicans ended a three-game slide with a 99-91 victory over the visiting Knicks on Monday night.Returning from a two-game absence because of a toe injury on his left foot, Holiday took over a tight game in the final minutes, scoring 6 points during a 9-0 run that gave New Orleans a 93-85 lead.Carmelo Anthony scored 22 for the Knicks, while Robin Lopez added 17.
