Dwyane Wade scored 24 points and became the 41st player in N.B.A. history to reach 20,000, the rookie Josh Richardson added 19, and the host Miami Heat led throughout on their way to beating the Cleveland Cavaliers, 122-101, on Saturday night.Goran Dragic had 18 points and 11 assists for Miami, Joe Johnson also scored 18, and Hassan Whiteside finished with 16 points and 13 rebounds. The Heat outrebounded the Cavaliers, 42-26, and led by a season-high 33 points at one time.LeBron James scored 26 points for Cleveland but sat out the final 15 minutes, with the outcome already decided.
