By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 17, 2016
NASHVILLE —  James Neal and Ryan Johansen each scored a goal and had an assist to lead the Nashville Predators to a 4-2 win over the slumping Islanders on Thursday night.Ryan Ellis and Austin Watson also scored for Nashville, which has recorded at least 1 point in the standings in 16 of its last 17 games.The rookie Ryan Pulock and Brock Nelson had the goals for the Islanders, who have lost of four of their last five.Neal scored the game’s first goal at 9 minutes 15 seconds of the opening period. Calle Jarnkrok carried the puck off the right wall to the circle and fed Neal skating through the slot, where he beat Thomas Greiss with a wrist shot on the stick side.
