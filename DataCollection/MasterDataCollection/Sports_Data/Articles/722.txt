By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 6, 2016
Kevin Molino scored in the second half to help host Orlando City tie the Red Bulls, 1-1, in a testy match that featured eight yellow cards and a red card. Molino’s fourth goal came in the 67th minute. The Red Bulls took the lead in the 19th minute when Bradley Wright-Phillips scored off an assist by Sacha Kljestan, who leads M.L.S. with six.
