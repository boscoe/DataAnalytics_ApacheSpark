Thomas Müller tied the score in the first minute of injury time, and Bayern Munich scored two more goals in extra time to complete a thrilling 4-2 home victory over Juventus on Wednesday that sent Bayern into the Champions League quarterfinals.Thiago Alcantara scored in the 108th minute to put Bayern ahead for good in the game, the second leg of a round-of-16 matchup. Kingsley Coman, on loan from Juventus, added Bayern’s fourth goal just two minutes later to seal a 6-4 victory on aggregate.Bayern trailed, 2-0, at halftime after goals from Paul Pogba and Juan Cuadrado had put Juventus in charge.
