He made his first start since last November and had not played in two of the Knicks’ last three games.The Knicks hit a season-high 16 3-pointers. Derrick Williams scored 18 points and Jose Calderon added 10 points and 12 assists as the Knicks won for the second time in six games.The rookie Devin Booker led Phoenix with 32 points. Tyson Chandler’s nine rebounds gave him 9,005 for his career, making him the 50th player in N.B.A. history to reach that milestone.The Suns tried to mount a comeback in the third quarter and trimmed a 20-point halftime deficit to 13 on a jumper by Alex Len with 3 minutes 35 seconds to go. But Anthony responded with his third 3-pointer of the game to make the score 83-67, and the Suns never got closer than 13 points in the second half.The Knicks opened a 54-28 advantage in the first half. The former Suns center Robin Lopez blocked a shot at one end, then put in a hook shot at the other during their run that broke open the game.Anthony scored 12 points in the second quarter and made 8 of 10 shots in the first half.
