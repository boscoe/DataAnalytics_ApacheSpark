CONNECTICUT 74, COLORADO 67Rodney Purvis, Daniel Hamilton and a stifling defense carried Connecticut back from an 11-point first-half deficit, giving the Huskies enough for a first-round victory over Colorado in Des Moines.Purvis had 19 points, Hamilton added 17, and the No. 9-seeded Huskies (25-10) advanced to the second round of the South Region.
