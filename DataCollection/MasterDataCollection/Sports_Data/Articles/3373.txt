By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 28, 2016
SALT LAKE CITY —  Thaddeus Young had 21 points and 8 rebounds, and Brook Lopez added 19 points to lead the Nets to a 98-96 victory over the Utah Jazz on Saturday night.Wayne Ellington had 16 points for the Nets, who ended a three-game losing streak to the Jazz dating to last season.Gordon Hayward scored 27 points to lead Utah — with 7 of them coming in the final minute — and Rudy Gobert chipped in 12 points, a season-high 19 rebounds and 6 blocks. Rodney Hood scored 15 points for the Jazz while Derrick Favors added 13 points and 11 rebounds.That was not enough to keep Utah from losing at home to an Eastern Conference opponent for just the second time in nine games.AdvertisementTrailing by 13 points midway through the fourth quarter, the Jazz rallied and cut the deficit to 92-89 on a 3-pointer from Hayward with 46.9 seconds left.
