The jockey Russell Baze, who has the most career victories in North American thoroughbred racing history, announced his retirement Monday, effective immediately.Baze, 57, has had 53,578 mounts, compiling 12,842 first-place finishes, 9,600 seconds and 7,855 thirds and $199.3 million in purse earnings during a 42-year career.Baze, a native of Vancouver, was inducted into the National Museum of Racing’s Hall of Fame in 1999 and the Canadian Horse Racing Hall of Fame in 2012.
