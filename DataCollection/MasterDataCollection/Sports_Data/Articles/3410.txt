By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSFEB. 27, 2016
Antoine Griezmann scored a goal early in the second half to give Atlético Madrid a 1-0 win at Real Madrid on Saturday, keeping Atlético’s title hopes alive and virtually ending Real’s chances in the Spanish league.Griezmann scored with a left-footer in the 53rd minute after a cross by left back Filipe Luís, helping visiting Atlético pull to within 5 points of first-place Barcelona, which hosts Sevilla on Sunday.Real Madrid could drop to 12 points behind Barcelona if Barcelona wins on Sunday.The loss was Real Madrid’s first at home since a humiliating 4-0 defeat against Barcelona in November. The team had won seven straight at home.Fans jeered Real Madrid’s president, Florentino Pérez, calling for his resignation. After the final whistle, many fans waved white handkerchiefs to show their displeasure with him. Some protested outside the stadium, prompting the police to intervene to disperse the crowd.Advertisement“The league is over for us,” Real Madrid Coach Zinedine Zidane said. “To lose to Atlético at home is very difficult to overcome.”AdvertisementThis is the third season in a row that Atlético has won at Real Madrid in Spanish league play.“This win gives Atlético Madrid fans reason to feel very proud of their players,” said Diego Simeone, Atlético’s coach. “Atlético keeps improving.
