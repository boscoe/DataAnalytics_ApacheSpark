By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSAPRIL 15, 2017
FEDERAL WAY, Wash. — Chyna J. Thomas, the 22-year-old sister of Boston Celtics guard Isaiah Thomas, was killed in a one-car crash early Saturday, the police said.The Washington State Patrol said Chyna Thomas died at the scene on Interstate 5 in Federal Way after a crash at about 5 a.m. The police report said she had not been wearing a seatbelt.Isaiah Thomas is a high-scoring guard for the Boston Celtics. The family is from Tacoma, which is about 12 miles south of Federal Way, and Isaiah played at Curtis High School there and at the University of Washington.“We are terribly saddened by the tragic loss of Chyna Thomas,” the Celtics said in a statement. “The thoughts and prayers of the entire Celtics organization are with Isaiah and his family.”A version of this article appears in print on April 16, 2017, on Page SP6 of the New York edition with the headline: Pro Basketball; Celtics Guard’s Sister Killed.
