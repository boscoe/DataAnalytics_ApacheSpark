By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 8, 2016
Monta Ellis scored 26 points and Paul George added 23 to help the Indiana Pacers end the San Antonio Spurs’ eight-game winning streak with a 99-91 victory in Indianapolis on Monday night.It was only the second loss for the Spurs in 17 games against the Pacers since 2008.Kawhi Leonard and LaMarcus Aldridge each had 23 points for San Antonio, hours after the team said Coach Gregg Popovich had not made the trip because of a “family medical situation.” The Spurs did not provide additional details.Before the game, the Pacers signed the troubled point guard Ty Lawson, who was cut by Houston last week. Lawson was arrested twice last year on drunken-driving charges.GRIZZLIES 106, CAVALIERS 103 Tony Allen, back after missing eight games with a knee injury, scored a season-high 26 points, and visiting Memphis, playing without four injured starters, stunned Cleveland.AdvertisementLeBron James scored 28 points and Kyrie Irving 27 for the Cavaliers. James also moved past the Hall of Famer John Havlicek into 13th place on the N.B.A.’s career scoring list.CLIPPERS 109, MAVERICKS 90 Chris Paul scored 18 of his 27 points in the third quarter, DeAndre Jordan had 23 points and 20 rebounds, and Los Angeles rolled at Dallas.Paul outscored the Mavericks by himself in the third while going 8 of 9 from the field. The boos were again strong for Jordan’s second game in Dallas since he spurned the Mavericks in free agency.Dirk Nowitzki led the Mavericks with 22 points.
