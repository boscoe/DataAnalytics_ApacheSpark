By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSDEC. 31, 2016
HOUSTON — James Harden had 53 points, 17 assists and 16 rebounds for his second straight triple-double, and the Houston Rockets beat the short-handed Knicks, 129-122, on Saturday night.Harden set a career highs for points and matched his career best for assists. He passed his previous career best of 51 points on a 3-pointer with 1 minute 16 seconds left, leading to a standing ovation.The Knicks got within 3 points several times in the fourth quarter — the last on a layup by Joakim Noah with about four minutes left.
