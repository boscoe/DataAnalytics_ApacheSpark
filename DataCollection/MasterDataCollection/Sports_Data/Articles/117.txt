By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 7, 2016
The Memphis Grizzlies fired Dave Joerger on Saturday in a surprising move after three seasons and three straight playoff appearances. A team battered by injuries finished the regular season at 42-40 before being swept in the first round by San Antonio. The Grizzlies used an N.B.A.-record 28 players this season. Memphis becomes the fourth N.B.A. team currently without a coach, joining the Knicks, the Sacramento Kings and the Indiana Pacers, who fired Frank Vogel on Thursday.
