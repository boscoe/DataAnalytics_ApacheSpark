By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 6, 2016
Cathryn Sophia rolled to victory Friday in the 142nd Kentucky Oaks, the $1 million showcase for 3-year-old fillies, on Friday at Churchill Downs. Cathryn Sophia overtook Lewis Bay, who finished third, and pulled away to beat Land Over Sea by two and three-quarters lengths. Cathryn Sophia, ridden by Javier Castellano, ran the mile and an eighth in 1 minute 50.53 seconds for her fifth win in six starts.
