By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 20, 2016
SAN JOSE, Calif. — Martin Jones made 22 saves in his second straight shutout and Tomas Hertl scored twice Thursday to give the San Jose Sharks a series lead for the first time in four trips to the Western Conference finals with a 3-0 victory over the St. Louis Blues.Joonas Donskoi also scored to give the Sharks a two-games-to-one series advantage and move them as close as they have ever been to their first Stanley Cup finals.
