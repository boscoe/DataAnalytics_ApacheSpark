By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJUNE 4, 2016
MIAMI — Left fielder Michael Conforto ended an 0-for-20 slump Saturday with a run-scoring single in the eighth inning and scored the tiebreaking run on pinch-hitter Matt Reynolds’s two-out single later in the inning to help the Mets rally past the Miami Marlins and win, 6-4.Conforto also hit a sacrifice fly in the sixth.
