Rupert Murdoch, the executive chairman of News Corporation, and the former model Jerry Hall were married on Friday at a centuries-old mansion in central London, weeks after announcing their engagement.Mr. Murdoch, 84, and Ms. Hall, 59, were photographed leaving Spencer House after the ceremony.
